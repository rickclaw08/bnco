Shipping Policy

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

Thank you for shopping with FORGE. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping.

1. Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to the United States only (all 50 states, Washington D.C., and U.S. territories). We do not ship internationally at this time. International shipping will be announced on our Site and social media when available.

2. Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout before payment.

3. Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days (Monday–Friday, excluding federal holidays). Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

4. Estimated Delivery Times

MethodEstimated Delivery
USPS First Class Mail3–5 business days after shipment

Delivery times are estimates and are not guaranteed. Delays may occur due to weather, carrier issues, high volume periods (holidays), or other circumstances beyond our control.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a USPS tracking number. You can track your package at usps.com or through the link provided in your shipping confirmation email.

6. Shipping Address

Please ensure your shipping address is correct and complete at checkout. We are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer. If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect address, we will reship the order at the customer's expense.

7. P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO Addresses

We ship to P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO/DPO military addresses via USPS. Delivery times to military addresses may be longer than standard estimates.

8. Lost, Stolen, or Damaged Packages

If your package appears lost (no tracking updates for 7+ business days after shipment) or arrives damaged:

  • Contact us at [support@YOURDOMAIN.com] with your order number.
  • For damaged items, please include photos of the packaging and product.
  • We will work with USPS to locate your package or send a replacement/refund at no additional cost.

FORGE is not responsible for packages marked as delivered by USPS. If your tracking shows delivered but you have not received your package, please check with neighbors, your building's front desk, or your local post office. If still unresolved, contact us and we will assist you.

9. Expedited Shipping

Expedited shipping options (USPS Priority Mail, Priority Mail Express) may be available at checkout for an additional cost. Availability and pricing are calculated in real-time based on your location and order weight.

10. Holiday and Peak Season Shipping

During peak seasons (Black Friday, Cyber Monday, holiday periods), processing and delivery times may be extended. We recommend ordering early to ensure timely delivery. We will communicate any delays via our Site and email.
