Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

At FORGE, we stand behind the quality of our men's grooming products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

1. Return Window

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to request a return or refund. After 30 days, we are unable to offer a refund or exchange.

2. Eligibility for Returns

2.1 Unopened Products

Products that are unopened, unused, and in their original packaging are eligible for a full refund. The product must be in resalable condition.

2.2 Opened / Partially Used Products

Because our products are consumable grooming items (beard oil, beard balm, beard wash), we understand you need to try them. If you are unsatisfied with an opened product:

  • Products used less than 25% are eligible for a full refund.
  • Products used between 25%–50% are eligible for a 50% refund or store credit for the full amount.
  • Products used more than 50% are not eligible for a refund, but may qualify for store credit at our discretion.

2.3 Non-Returnable Items

  • Gift cards
  • Products purchased during final sale or clearance promotions (unless defective)
  • Products not purchased directly from our Site

3. Defective or Damaged Products

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect product, contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will provide a full refund or replacement at no additional cost, including return shipping. Please include photos of the damage or defect when contacting us.

4. Allergic Reactions

We recommend reviewing the full ingredient list before use. If you experience an allergic reaction or skin irritation, discontinue use immediately and consult a healthcare professional. Contact us within 30 days for a full refund on the product. We may request a photo of the reaction for our quality assurance records.

5. How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact us at [support@YOURDOMAIN.com] with your order number, the product(s) you'd like to return, and the reason for the return.
  2. We will respond within 2 business days with return instructions and a return authorization number.
  3. Ship the product back to us using the provided return address. Please pack items securely to prevent damage during transit.
  4. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to our error (wrong product, defective item, or damage in transit).

6. Refund Processing

  • Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you via email of the approval or rejection of your refund.
  • Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
  • If you haven't received your refund after 10 business days, please check with your bank or credit card provider, then contact us.

7. Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges at this time. To exchange a product, please initiate a return and place a new order for the desired item.

8. Store Credit

Store credit, when issued, is delivered via email as a unique discount code. Store credit does not expire and can be applied to any future purchase on our Site.

9. Order Cancellations

If you need to cancel an order, contact us as soon as possible at [support@YOURDOMAIN.com]. Orders that have not yet been shipped can be cancelled for a full refund. Once an order has shipped, it cannot be cancelled and must follow the return process outlined above.
