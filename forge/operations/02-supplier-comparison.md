## Recommended Strategy

### Phase 1: Launch (Months 1–3)
**Primary:** Anthem Private Label or Aromasong (if verified)
- Ultra-low MOQ lets you test product-market fit
- Higher per-unit cost acceptable for initial validation
- Fast turnaround for quick iteration

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 4–8)
**Primary:** Oregon Private Label, Global Custom Commerce, or PL Supplements
- MOQ 100–500 sweet spot
- Better per-unit economics
- Still manageable cash outlay ($500–3,000 per SKU)

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 9+)
**Primary:** Bulk Apothecary or Royal Labs
- Best unit economics ($1.50–4/unit)
- Higher MOQ requires proven demand
- FDA-registered facilities for retail/wholesale credibility
