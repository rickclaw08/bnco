## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What makes FORGE different from other beard care brands?

Honestly? The formula and the attitude. We use natural, plant-derived ingredients — no synthetic fragrances, no parabens, no petroleum. But more importantly, we're not trying to sell you an identity. No Viking branding, no "alpha male" nonsense. Just well-made products for men who want their beard to look and feel good without the costume.

### 2. What's in your products?

Everything is listed on the label and on each product page. Our base ingredients include jojoba oil, argan oil, shea butter, beeswax, coconut oil, and essential oil blends (cedarwood, black pepper, bergamot). Full ingredient lists are on every product page. We don't hide behind "proprietary blends."

### 3. What does FORGE smell like?

Warm, grounded, and subtle. Our signature scent is built around cedarwood, black pepper, and bergamot. Think: freshly built bookshelf, not body spray at a nightclub. It's designed to be noticeable up close but not aggressive — it layers well and won't compete with your cologne.

### 4. I'm new to beard care. Where do I start?

The Starter Kit ($44). It includes full-size versions of all three products plus a routine card. Wash 2-3 times a week, oil daily, balm daily. Takes 90 seconds. You'll notice a difference within a week.

### 5. How long does each product last?

With daily use: Beard Oil lasts ~60 days, Beard Balm lasts ~60 days, Beard Wash lasts ~45 days (at 2-3 washes per week). The Starter Kit will carry most guys through two full months.

### 6. Will this work on a short beard? What about a long one?

Yes to both. Our formulas work on stubble through full beards. For shorter beards, use less product (2-3 drops of oil, a small amount of balm). For longer beards, scale up. The Beard Wash works regardless of length.

### 7. Is FORGE cruelty-free?

Yes. None of our products are tested on animals. Ever. We also use recyclable packaging — amber glass bottles, aluminum tins, and minimal plastic.

### 8. Do you use synthetic fragrances or parabens?

No. Our scent comes entirely from essential oils. No synthetic fragrances, no parabens, no sulfates, no petroleum derivatives. We're not chasing a "clean" label — we just don't think those ingredients belong on your face.

### 9. Can I use the Beard Oil and Beard Balm together?

That's the move. Oil first (for moisture and skin health), then balm on top (for shape and hold). They're formulated to layer — same scent profile, complementary textures. The oil hydrates; the balm seals it in and gives you control.

### 10. What's your shipping policy?

Free shipping on all orders over $40 (The Starter Kit qualifies). Standard orders ship within 1-2 business days via USPS. Most deliveries arrive within 3-5 business days depending on location. We ship to all 50 states.

### 11. What's your return policy?

If you're not satisfied, reach out within 30 days of delivery. We'll make it right — exchange, refund, whatever works. We don't make you jump through hoops or write a 500-word explanation. If it's not working for you, we want to know.

### 12. Do you offer subscriptions?

Not yet, but it's coming. When we do, it'll be straightforward — a discount, a schedule you control, and a cancel button that actually works. We'll announce it to our email list first.

### 13. Is FORGE vegan?

The Beard Oil and Beard Wash are vegan. The Beard Balm contains beeswax, so it's not vegan but is cruelty-free. We're exploring plant-based wax alternatives for a future reformulation.

### 14. Where are your products made?

Formulated and produced in the United States. Small-batch manufacturing with quality control at every stage.
