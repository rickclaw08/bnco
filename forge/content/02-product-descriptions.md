## 3. FORGE Beard Wash — $18

**Tagline:** Clean slate.

**Short Description:**
A gentle, sulfate-free beard wash that cleans without stripping. Because the bar soap thing stopped being acceptable a while ago.

**Full Description:**

Here's the problem with regular shampoo on your beard: it strips the natural oils that keep your facial hair soft and your skin healthy. Here's the problem with bar soap: everything.

FORGE Beard Wash is formulated specifically for facial hair and the sensitive skin beneath it. Our coconut-derived surfactants clean thoroughly without disrupting your skin's moisture barrier, while aloe vera and chamomile extract calm irritation and reduce redness.

Use it 2-3 times a week. Your beard will be cleaner. Your skin will be happier. And you can stop pretending Irish Spring counts as grooming.

**Scent:** A lighter expression of the FORGE signature — cedar-forward with clean, green undertones.

**Key Benefits:**
- Sulfate-free, gentle cleansing
- Won't strip natural oils
- Soothes irritation and reduces flaking
- Works on all beard lengths
- Preps beard for oil and balm absorption

**How to Use:**
Wet beard thoroughly. Massage a dime-sized amount into beard and skin beneath. Lather for 30 seconds. Rinse completely. Follow with FORGE Beard Oil. Use 2-3x per week.

**Size:** 4 oz / 120 mL (approximately 45-day supply)

**Ingredients:** Water, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Chamomile Extract, Glycerin, Jojoba Oil, Cedarwood Essential Oil, Bergamot Essential Oil, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin.
