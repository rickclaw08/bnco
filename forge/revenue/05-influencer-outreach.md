### Ideal Influencer Profile
| Criteria | Target |
|----------|--------|
| Followers | 2K-50K (micro/nano) |
| Engagement rate | 3%+ (check with HypeAuditor free tool or manual count) |
| Content style | Authentic, face-to-camera, not overly polished |
| Audience | Male, 22-40, US/Canada/UK |
| Posts about | Grooming, lifestyle, fitness, fashion, or fatherhood |
| Red flags | Fake followers, no comments, promotes 10+ brands/month |

### Where to Find Them
1. **TikTok search** → sort by "This month" for rising creators
2. **Instagram Explore** → hashtag pages → "Recent" tab
3. **YouTube Shorts** → "beard" searches, sort by upload date
4. **Competitor tagged posts** → see who's posting about Beardbrand, Honest Amish, etc.
5. **Barber accounts** — they demo products on clients. Gold.

## DM Outreach Templates

### Template 1: The Direct Offer
> Hey [Name] — love your content, especially [specific post/video]. I'm the founder of FORGE, a new men's beard care brand launching [month]. All-natural, no BS formulas.
>
> I'd love to send you our full lineup to try — no strings. If you dig it, a quick post or story would mean the world. Interested?

### Template 2: The Collab Angle
> [Name]! Your [beard/grooming] content is exactly the vibe we're building at FORGE. We're a new beard care brand and we're putting together a crew of creators for our launch.
>
> Here's the deal: free products + 15% commission on every sale through your link. We handle everything — you just be you.
>
> Want in? I can send details.

### Template 3: The Barber/Professional
> Hey [Name] — your work is insane. I run FORGE, a new premium beard care line, and I think your clients would love it.
>
> I'd love to send you a kit to use in the shop. If it fits your standard, we can talk about an ongoing partnership (free product + affiliate commission).
>
> Cool to send you some?

### Follow-Up (if no reply after 3-5 days)
> Hey [Name] — just bumping this in case it got buried. No pressure at all — just thought FORGE would be a solid fit for your audience. Lmk either way 🤙

## Outreach Process

1. **Find 50 creators** matching the ideal profile
2. **Engage first** — like 3-5 posts, leave a genuine comment, watch their stories for 2-3 days
3. **DM on Day 3-4** — use appropriate template
4. **Follow up** if no reply after 5 days (once only)
5. **If interested** — get shipping address, send product with handwritten note
6. **After delivery** — check in after 5-7 days: "Did it arrive? What do you think?"
7. **If they post** — repost immediately, comment, share to stories
8. **Track everything** in the spreadsheet below

## Tracking Spreadsheet Structure

Create in Google Sheets or Notion:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | Creator's name |
| **Handle** | @username |
| **Platform** | TikTok / IG / YouTube |
| **Followers** | Follower count |
| **Engagement %** | Estimated engagement rate |
| **Niche** | Beard care / grooming / lifestyle / barber |
| **Contact Date** | Date of first DM |
| **Template Used** | 1, 2, or 3 |
| **Response** | Yes / No / Pending |
| **Response Date** | When they replied |
| **Product Sent** | Yes / No |
| **Ship Date** | When product was sent |
| **Tracking #** | Shipping tracking |
| **Content Posted** | Yes / No |
| **Post Link** | URL to their post |
| **Post Date** | When they posted |
| **Affiliate Code** | Their unique discount code (e.g., FORGE_[NAME]) |
| **Affiliate Link** | Trackable link |
| **Sales Generated** | $ from their code/link |
| **Notes** | Any relevant context |

### Status Tags
- 🔍 Researching
- 📩 DM Sent
- 💬 In Conversation
- 📦 Product Shipped
- ✅ Content Posted
- 🤝 Ongoing Partner
- ❌ Declined / No Response

## Budget & Unit Economics

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Product cost per kit (seeding) | ~$15-25 |
| Shipping per kit | ~$5-8 |
| Target sends (Month 1) | 20-30 kits |
| Total seeding budget | $400-$1,000 |
| Expected conversion to post | 30-50% |
| Expected posts from 25 kits | 8-12 posts |
| Cost per piece of content | ~$50-80 |

This is 5-10x cheaper than paid UGC or macro-influencer rates. And the content is reusable in ads.

## Repurposing Influencer Content

Every piece of UGC you get can be:
1. **Meta ad creative** (with permission — get it in writing/DM)
2. **Website testimonial** (embed on product pages)
3. **Email content** (social proof in welcome/post-purchase flows)
4. **Organic social repost** (tag the creator)
5. **Landing page social proof** (pre-launch referral page)

Always ask: *"Mind if we use this in our marketing? We'll tag you."* — almost everyone says yes.
