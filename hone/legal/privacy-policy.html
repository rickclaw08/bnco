1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping and billing address when you create an account or place an order.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your full card number on our servers.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers, and referring URL.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, and other browsing behavior on our Site.
  • Location Data: Approximate geographic location based on your IP address.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies as described in Section 4.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and payment processing
  • Communicate with you about orders, products, promotions, and customer service inquiries
  • Send marketing emails and newsletters (with your consent, via Klaviyo)
  • Personalize your shopping experience and recommend products
  • Analyze Site usage and improve our Site, products, and services
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We share your information with the following third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business:

3.1 Shopify (E-Commerce Platform)

Our Site is hosted on Shopify. Shopify processes your order data, stores your account information, and facilitates transactions. Shopify's privacy policy: shopify.com/legal/privacy

3.2 Stripe (Payment Processing)

We use Stripe to process credit and debit card payments. When you make a purchase, your payment information is transmitted directly to Stripe's secure servers. We do not store your full payment card details. Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

3.3 Klaviyo (Email Marketing)

We use Klaviyo to manage our email marketing campaigns and newsletters. If you subscribe to our mailing list, your email address, name, and purchase history may be shared with Klaviyo to send you relevant marketing communications. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email. Klaviyo's privacy policy: klaviyo.com/legal/privacy

3.4 Google Analytics (Website Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Site. Google Analytics collects information such as how often you visit the Site, what pages you view, and what other sites you visited before. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects your IP address but does not collect your name or other identifying information. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3.5 Meta Pixel (Advertising)

We use the Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Pixel to measure the effectiveness of our advertising, deliver targeted ads, and build custom audiences. The Meta Pixel collects data about your browsing activity on our Site and may link it to your Facebook/Instagram account. Meta's privacy policy: facebook.com/privacy/policy. You can manage your ad preferences at facebook.com/adpreferences.

3.6 Shipping Carriers

We share your name and shipping address with USPS (and any other carriers we may use) to deliver your orders.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Site to function (shopping cart, checkout, authentication).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the Site (Google Analytics).
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign performance (Meta Pixel, Klaviyo).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect Site functionality.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services, fulfill orders, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Order records are retained for a minimum of 7 years for tax and accounting purposes. If you request deletion of your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information. Our Site uses SSL/TLS encryption for data transmitted between your browser and our servers. Payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS compliant providers (Stripe). However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
