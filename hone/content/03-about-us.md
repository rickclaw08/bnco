## The Story

HONE started with a question nobody was answering honestly: why does every men's grooming brand feel like it was designed by someone who's never actually had a beard?

On one end, you've got the drugstore aisle — plastic bottles with vague promises, synthetic ingredients, and branding that screams "your dad's aftershave." On the other, the so-called premium brands — $65 oils in apothecary bottles, Viking mythology, and marketing copy that reads like a Renaissance Faire program.

We wanted something in the middle. Something that works. Something that looks good on your shelf without requiring a personality crisis to purchase. Something made with ingredients you can actually pronounce, at a price that doesn't assume you're an idiot.

So we made it. And we kept refining it until it was right.

---

## What We Believe

**Ingredients matter more than branding.**
Every HONE product starts with the formula. Natural carrier oils, plant-based butters, essential oil blends — all chosen because they work, not because they sound exotic on a label. We don't use synthetic fragrances, parabens, sulfates, or petroleum derivatives. Not because it's trendy. Because they're bad for your skin.

**Simplicity is a feature.**
We make three products. Not twelve. Not a "system" with seven steps and a subscription you can't cancel. Wash, oil, balm. That's the routine. It takes 90 seconds. It works.

**Respect is built in.**
We price our products fairly. We tell you exactly what's in them. We don't manufacture urgency with fake scarcity drops or countdown timers. If our product is good — and it is — you'll come back because it works, not because we tricked you.

**Your beard is yours.**
We're not here to tell you what kind of man you are. We're not a lifestyle brand. We're not selling an identity. We're selling beard care products that do their job so you can get on with yours.
