### The Short Version

HONE exists because we got tired of choosing between drugstore wax that smells like a frat house and overpriced boutique oils that belong in a spa you'd never visit.

We wanted something in between. Something that actually works, smells like it was made by adults, and doesn't require a lumberjack costume to buy.

So we made it. Deliberately.

Every HONE product is formulated with natural carrier oils, plant-based butters, and essential oil blends that we'd actually put on our own faces. No synthetics. No parabens. No petroleum. No apologies.

This is beard care for men who sharpen their craft — whatever that craft happens to be. You don't need a brand to tell you who you are. You just need products that don't waste your time.
