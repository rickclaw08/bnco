# HONE — 25 TikTok/Reels Content Ideas

---

## Pre-Launch Phase (Ideas 1-8)

### 1. "The Beard Aisle Tier List"
**Hook:** "Ranking every beard product at Target so you don't have to."
**Format:** POV walking down the aisle, holding up products, rating them. Brutally honest. End with: "Or you could just wait for something sharper. Link in bio."
**Script Notes:** Quick cuts, text overlays with ratings (S-tier through F-tier). Be specific — name ingredients, not brands. Keep it under 45 seconds.

### 2. "What's Actually in Your Beard Oil"
**Hook:** "Flip your beard oil over. Read the ingredients. I'll wait."
**Format:** Green screen with common ingredient labels. Break down what mineral oil, fragrance, and dimethicone actually are. End with HONE's ingredient list for comparison.
**Script Notes:** Educational tone, not preachy. "I'm not saying it'll kill you. I'm saying you deserve better."

### 3. "The 90-Second Beard Routine"
**Hook:** "Your entire beard routine should take less time than this video."
**Format:** Real-time demonstration. Wash, oil, balm. Timer on screen. Clean, well-lit bathroom. No rushing — show it actually takes 90 seconds.
**Script Notes:** No talking head. Just hands, product, beard. Satisfying ASMR-adjacent audio. Text overlays only.

### 4. "Men's Grooming Red Flags"
**Hook:** "If your beard products have any of these, throw them away."
**Format:** Listicle format with text reveals. Synthetic fragrance, petroleum jelly, "proprietary blend" (what are you hiding?), Viking on the label.
**Script Notes:** Witty, not aggressive. The Viking joke lands if delivered deadpan. Under 30 seconds.

### 5. "Building HONE — Day 1"
**Hook:** "We're building a beard care brand from scratch. Here's day one."
**Format:** Behind-the-scenes. Raw footage of formulation meetings, ingredient sourcing, label design. Series potential.
**Script Notes:** Founder-led. Authentic, unpolished. Show real work — spreadsheets, supplier emails, lab samples. This is the "hone your craft" ethos in action.

### 6. "Things Guys With Great Beards Never Do"
**Hook:** "Men with great beards never do this."
**Format:** Quick list with visual demos. Use bar soap. Skip moisturizing. Use their girlfriend's conditioner. Ignore the skin underneath.
**Script Notes:** Light humor. Relatable. End with "They also don't settle for dull products."

### 7. "I Tried Every Beard Oil Under $30"
**Hook:** "I bought every beard oil under $30 so you don't have to."
**Format:** Desk spread of 8-10 products. Quick reviews — texture, scent, absorption, ingredients. Rank them. HONE last (the reveal).
**Script Notes:** Genuine reviews of competitors. Don't trash them unfairly. Honesty builds more trust than dunking. 60-90 seconds.

### 8. "The Beard Ick List"
**Hook:** "Things that give me the ick about men's grooming brands."
**Format:** Trending "ick" format. Skull logos. "For MEN" in aggressive font. Bacon-scented beard oil. "Unleash your inner warrior." Subscription traps.
**Script Notes:** Funny, relatable. Appeals to the target demo who also hates this stuff. Under 30 seconds.

## Launch Week (Ideas 9-16)

### 9. "We Launched a Beard Brand. Here's Everything."
**Hook:** "We just launched HONE. Here's every product, every ingredient, every price."
**Format:** Clean product shots on a workbench. Pick up each product, state what it does, what's in it, what it costs. No hype. Just facts.
**Script Notes:** Confident, straightforward energy. This is the anti-hype launch video. "We're not going to tell you it'll change your life. It'll sharpen your routine."

### 10. "Unboxing HONE (by a guy who hates unboxings)"
**Hook:** "I hate unboxing videos but I made one anyway because the packaging is that good."
**Format:** Standard unboxing but with self-aware commentary. Appreciate the packaging design without being precious about it. Open everything, smell everything.
**Script Notes:** Humor through self-awareness. "Yes, I'm smelling the box. Judge me."

### 11. "First Application — Real Time Reaction"
**Hook:** "Trying HONE for the first time. No script. No edit."
**Format:** Single continuous shot. Apply oil, then balm. React honestly. Show before/after in split screen.
**Script Notes:** Authenticity is everything here. If you genuinely like it, it shows. Don't oversell.

### 12. "What $44 Gets You vs. $44 at [Competitor]"
**Hook:** "What $44 gets you at HONE vs. what $44 gets you at most beard brands."
**Format:** Side-by-side comparison. HONE Starter Kit (3 full products) vs. a single premium competitor product. Show sizes, ingredients, value.
**Script Notes:** Don't name competitors directly. Let the comparison speak. "I'm not saying who. You know who."

### 13. "POV: Your Beard After One Week of HONE"
**Hook:** "Day 1 vs. Day 7."
**Format:** Split screen or transition. Show genuine before/after. Document real daily use over a week. Time-lapse feel.
**Script Notes:** Must be real results, real person. Credibility > production value.

### 14. "Reading Our Ingredient List vs. Theirs"
**Hook:** "Let's play a game. I'll read two ingredient lists. You guess which one you'd put on your face."
**Format:** Two cards. Read List A (chemicals, synthetic fragrance, methylparaben). Read List B (jojoba, argan, cedarwood). Reveal: List B is HONE.
**Script Notes:** Educational but entertaining. The contrast sells itself.

### 15. "The HONE Scent Breakdown"
**Hook:** "What does HONE actually smell like? Let me break it down."
**Format:** Show each essential oil ingredient. Cedar chip, black peppercorns, bergamot fruit. Describe each note. "It smells like a well-built cabin, not a frat house."
**Script Notes:** Sensory content performs well. Close-up shots of raw ingredients. ASMR-adjacent.

### 16. "My Girlfriend/Partner Reviews HONE"
**Hook:** "I made my girlfriend smell every HONE product and rate them."
**Format:** Reaction video. Genuine reactions to scent, texture, the before/after on your beard. Unscripted.
**Script Notes:** Partner reaction content performs extremely well in grooming. Let them be honest.

## Ongoing Content (Ideas 17-25)

### 17. "Beard Mistakes I Made So You Don't Have To"
**Hook:** "5 years of beard mistakes in 60 seconds."
**Format:** Personal story format. Photos/videos from early beard days. What went wrong, what fixed it. End with current routine.
**Script Notes:** Vulnerability + humor. "Year two was rough. We don't talk about year two."

### 18. "Barber Reacts to HONE"
**Hook:** "I gave a barber our products without telling him the brand."
**Format:** Blind test. Professional barber uses HONE on a client. Gets their honest take on quality, scent, performance. Reveal the brand at the end.
**Script Notes:** Third-party credibility. Choose a barber with their own following for cross-promotion.

### 19. "How to Trim Your Beard (Without Ruining It)"
**Hook:** "Stop guessing where your neckline should be."
**Format:** Tutorial. Clear, well-lit demo. Neckline, cheek line, mustache trim. Practical tips. HONE products used in the post-trim routine.
**Script Notes:** Genuine value-add content. Product placement is organic, not forced. 60-90 seconds.

### 20. "Deinfluencing Men's Grooming"
**Hook:** "Grooming products you do NOT need to buy."
**Format:** Trending deinfluencing format. Beard glitter (yes, it exists). Beard straighteners. 7-step beard systems. $80 combs.
**Script Notes:** Positions HONE as the reasonable alternative. "You need like 3 things. That's it."

### 21. "What Your Beard Says About Your Grooming Routine"
**Hook:** "I can tell how you take care of your beard just by looking at it."
**Format:** Comment response or stitch format. Analyze different beard types/conditions. Dry = no oil. Patchy = check your nutrition. Wiry = you need balm.
**Script Notes:** Helpful, not judgmental. Position as the knowledgeable friend, not the expert on a pedestal.

### 22. "HONE vs. the Bathroom Shelf"
**Hook:** "Show me your grooming shelf and I'll tell you what to keep."
**Format:** UGC-style. Followers submit photos of their bathroom shelves. Review what they have, suggest swaps. Honest — keep good products even if they're not HONE.
**Script Notes:** Builds trust by not pushing your product for everything. "That moisturizer is fine. Keep it."

### 23. "The $10 vs. $28 Beard Oil Test"
**Hook:** "Is expensive beard oil actually worth it? Let's find out."
**Format:** Split-face test. Cheap oil on one side, HONE on the other. 8-hour wear test. Check-ins throughout the day. Honest comparison.
**Script Notes:** Confidence play. If your product is good, this content makes itself. Show real differences in absorption, scent longevity, feel.

### 24. "Things I Wish Someone Told Me About Beard Care"
**Hook:** "Nobody tells you this when you start growing a beard."
**Format:** Talking head with text overlays. The itch phase is temporary. Your skin matters more than the hair. Wash your beard separately. Oil goes on damp, not dry.
**Script Notes:** Evergreen content. Helpful, non-promotional. HONE mention only at the end if at all. Build the brand through value.

### 25. "Behind the Scenes: How HONE Is Made"
**Hook:** "Here's exactly how we make HONE Beard Oil."
**Format:** Factory/lab footage. Raw ingredients → mixing → bottling → labeling → shipping. Show the actual process.
**Script Notes:** Transparency content. People love seeing how products are made. "This is a Tuesday for us." Satisfying, process-oriented. Keep it under 60 seconds with good music.

## General Content Notes

**Posting cadence:** 4-5x/week across TikTok and Reels
**Best performing formats for this niche:** Before/after, ingredient education, blind tests, partner reactions
**Hashtag strategy:** #beardcare #mensgrooming #beardoil #groomingtips #honegrooming #honeyourcraft
**Music:** Avoid trending sounds that date quickly. Use ambient/lo-fi or licensed tracks with longevity.
**Tone across all content:** Knowledgeable friend at a bar, not a guru on a stage. Confident but never arrogant. Witty but never try-hard.
