Regional brokerage with 45 agents across 3 offices

This growing brokerage was drowning in manual lead routing, follow-up sequences, and document preparation. Agents spent more time on admin tasks than selling. We built an end-to-end automation system that handles everything from lead capture to closing prep.

The Challenge

Reducing Customer Churn by 35% with Predictive AI

B2B project management SaaS, 2,800 active accounts

Churn was killing growth. This SaaS company was acquiring customers fast but losing them almost as quickly. They needed a system that could identify at-risk accounts before they churned and trigger targeted interventions automatically.

The Challenge

Monthly churn was running at 6.2%, meaning they replaced nearly half their customer base every year. The CS team only learned about unhappy customers when they canceled. Onboarding completion was stuck at 40%, and most churned users had never fully adopted the product.

Our Solution

We built a churn prediction model using product usage signals, support ticket sentiment, login frequency, and billing patterns. High-risk accounts trigger automated outreach sequences: personalized re-engagement emails, in-app feature tours, and CS team alerts for VIP accounts. We also rebuilt the onboarding flow with automated progress tracking and nudge campaigns.
