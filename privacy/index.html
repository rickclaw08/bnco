1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name

  • Any other information you choose to provide via contact forms, intake forms, or during consultations

1.2 AI Receptionist and Communication Data

When you or your customers interact with our AI Receptionist or automated communication systems, we may collect:

  • Phone numbers (incoming and outgoing)

  • Call recordings and transcriptions (where permitted by applicable law and with required consent)

Important: If you are a ClawOps client using our AI Receptionist, you are responsible for ensuring that your customers and callers are informed that they are interacting with an automated AI system and that calls may be recorded or transcribed, in compliance with applicable state and federal laws (including two-party consent states). ClawOps provides tools and templates to help you meet these requirements.

For healthcare clients using our scheduling, intake, or communication services, we may process:

  • Patient names and contact information
  • Appointment scheduling data
  • Insurance information provided through intake forms
  • General health-related information submitted during intake

We process healthcare data in accordance with HIPAA requirements where applicable. Healthcare clients requiring HIPAA compliance must execute a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with ClawOps before we process any protected health information (PHI). We will not process PHI without a signed BAA in place.

1.4 Legal Intake Data

For law firm clients using our intake automation services, we may process:

  • Potential client names and contact information
  • Case type and legal issue descriptions

  • Qualification responses and case details

ClawOps AI systems collect and organize information for attorney review. They do not provide legal advice, case evaluations, or legal opinions. All intake information is preliminary and subject to review by a licensed attorney.

When you visit our Site, we may automatically collect:

  • Pages viewed and time spent on pages
  • Device identifiers

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Site and store certain information. We use essential cookies required for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our Site. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

3.3 Shield Scanner Data

When you use the ClawOps Shield scanner service, we collect and process the following data:

  • Authentication Data: If you create an account, Firebase Authentication collects your email address and, if you sign in with Google, your Google profile information (name, email, profile picture).
  • Usage Data: Google Firestore stores your scan counts, user profile information, subscription tier, and plan limits.
  • Payment Data: If you subscribe to a paid plan, Stripe processes your payment information (credit card details, billing address). ClawOps does not store your full payment card details.
  • Scan Data: File scanning runs entirely client-side in your browser. Your files are not uploaded to or stored on ClawOps servers. We do not have access to the content of files you scan.

3.4 Named Sub-Processors

We use the following sub-processors to deliver our services:

  • OpenAI - AI processing for automation services
  • Anthropic - AI processing for automation services
  • Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication, Firestore) - User authentication and data storage for Shield scanner
  • Stripe - Payment processing for Shield subscriptions and service invoicing
  • Cloud hosting providers - Infrastructure and site hosting
