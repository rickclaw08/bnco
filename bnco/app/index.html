Share my scores on leaderboards
When enabled, your Vibe Score and stats appear on studio and city leaderboards
🎯 Control Score (40% of bnco Score)
Measures how controlled your eccentric movements are vs using momentum. Sourced from WHOOP's Muscular Load metric. Higher control means better form and more effective work.
🧗 Stillness Index (35% of bnco Score)
Tracks wrist and hand stability during strap work using Apple Watch's Core Motion sensors. Wobble indicates core disengagement. Less wobble = stronger core activation.
💨 Respiratory Efficiency (25% of bnco Score)
Monitors your breathing patterns during high-load movements. Holding your breath during exertion reduces effectiveness. Steady, rhythmic breathing earns higher scores.
