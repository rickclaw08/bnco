# Privacy Policy for BNCO

**Last Updated:** March 4, 2026

BNCO ("we", "our", "us") operates the BNCO mobile application and website (https://bnco.studio). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Name, email address, and profile details you provide during registration.

### Health and Fitness Data
- With your explicit consent, we collect data from connected wearable devices (Apple Watch, WHOOP, Fitbit, Garmin) including heart rate, strain, steps, calories burned, recovery metrics, and workout activity.

### Usage Data
- App usage patterns, feature interactions, leaderboard participation, and session data.

### Device Information
- Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting and optimization.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **Scoring and Leaderboards:** Calculate your Relative Effort Score (RES) and Pilates Vibe Score, and display them on leaderboards.
- **Gamification:** Track XP, streaks, achievements, and progression.
- **Studio Features:** Enable studio missions, community challenges, and studio-specific leaderboards.
- **App Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience.
- **Communications:** Send important updates about the app and your account.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal data. We share information only in these cases:

- **Leaderboards:** Your display name, avatar, and scores are visible to other users on leaderboards you participate in.
- **Studio Owners:** Studio administrators can see aggregated and individual performance data for members of their studio.
- **Service Providers:** We use third-party services (hosting, analytics) that process data on our behalf under strict data protection agreements.
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights.

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), secure authentication (OAuth 2.0), and encrypted storage for sensitive data.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Disconnect wearable devices at any time
- Opt out of non-essential communications

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics.

## 7. Children's Privacy

BNCO is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
