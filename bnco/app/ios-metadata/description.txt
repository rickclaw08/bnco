BNCO - Your Pilates, Gamified.

Turn every rep, every step, every heartbeat into a healthy competition. BNCO connects your wearable data to real-time leaderboards, so you can compete with your studio, your city, and the world.

KEY FEATURES:

Relative Effort Score (RES) - Your wearable data becomes a single, fair score that normalizes fitness levels. Whether you're a beginner or an elite athlete, everyone competes on equal ground.

Live Leaderboards - See where you rank during class, across your studio, in your city, state, or globally. Climb the ranks and claim the crown.

Ghost Racing - Race against yesterday's top performer in real time. Beat the ghost, earn bonus XP.

Studio Missions - Join challenges created by your studio. Hit targets together, unlock rewards, and build community.

Pilates Vibe Score - Your unique Pilates performance breakdown across Power, Flow, Grit, and Zen categories. Track your progress and find your strengths.

Gamified Progression - Level up with XP, maintain streaks, earn achievements, and unlock new ranks from Rookie to Legend.

City Boss Challenge - Every city has a Boss. Beat their score to claim the title and inspire your community.

Works with your favorite wearables including Apple Watch, WHOOP, Fitbit, and Garmin. Connect your device and start competing today.

Built for Pilates studios, fitness communities, and anyone who believes a little competition makes everything better.
