Welcome to BNCO 1.0!

We are thrilled to launch the first version of BNCO, your gamified Pilates companion.

What's included:
- Relative Effort Score (RES) powered by your wearable data
- Live class leaderboards with studio, city, state, and global rankings
- Ghost Racing against yesterday's top performers
- Pilates Vibe Score with Power, Flow, Grit, and Zen breakdowns
- Studio Missions and community challenges
- XP progression, streaks, and achievements
- City Boss challenge
- Wearable integration (Apple Watch, WHOOP, Fitbit, Garmin)

Connect your wearable, join your studio, and start competing today. Feedback? Reach out at https://bnco.studio/support.
