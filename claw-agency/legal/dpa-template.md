# ClawOps Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

**Version 1.0 - Effective Date: February 23, 2026**

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") forms part of the Master Services Agreement or Terms of Service (the "Agreement") between:

**Data Controller ("Client"):** [Client Legal Name]
**Data Processor ("ClawOps"):** ClawOps, operated by Rick Claw

This DPA applies where ClawOps processes Personal Data on behalf of Client in the course of providing Services under the Agreement.

---

## 1. Definitions

**"Applicable Data Protection Law"** means all applicable laws relating to the processing of Personal Data, including (where applicable) the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and any other applicable privacy or data protection legislation.

**"Data Controller"** means the entity that determines the purposes and means of processing Personal Data.

**"Data Processor"** means the entity that processes Personal Data on behalf of the Data Controller.

**"Data Subject"** means an identified or identifiable natural person whose Personal Data is processed.

**"Personal Data"** means any information relating to a Data Subject that is processed by ClawOps in connection with the Services, as further described in Annex 1.

**"Processing"** means any operation performed on Personal Data, including collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination, alignment, combination, restriction, erasure, or destruction.

**"Security Incident"** means any accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure of, or access to Personal Data transmitted, stored, or otherwise processed by ClawOps.

**"Sub-Processor"** means any third party engaged by ClawOps to process Personal Data on behalf of Client.

---

## 2. Scope and Roles

2.1 **Client as Controller.** Client is the Data Controller of Personal Data. Client determines the purposes and means of processing and is responsible for ensuring a lawful basis for processing under Applicable Data Protection Law.

2.2 **ClawOps as Processor.** ClawOps is the Data Processor. ClawOps will process Personal Data only on documented instructions from Client, except where required by applicable law. In such a case, ClawOps will inform Client of the legal requirement before processing (unless prohibited by law).

2.3 **Details of Processing.** The subject matter, duration, nature, purpose, types of Personal Data, and categories of Data Subjects are described in Annex 1.

---

## 3. Client Obligations

3.1 Client shall ensure that:

(a) It has a lawful basis for providing Personal Data to ClawOps for processing.

(b) It has provided all necessary notices to, and obtained all necessary consents or authorizations from, Data Subjects as required by Applicable Data Protection Law.

(c) Its processing instructions to ClawOps comply with Applicable Data Protection Law.

(d) It will promptly inform ClawOps if any processing instruction infringes Applicable Data Protection Law.

---

## 4. ClawOps Obligations

4.1 **Processing Instructions.** ClawOps will process Personal Data only in accordance with Client's documented instructions, the Agreement, and this DPA. If ClawOps believes an instruction violates Applicable Data Protection Law, ClawOps will promptly notify Client.

4.2 **Confidentiality.** ClawOps will ensure that all personnel authorized to process Personal Data are bound by confidentiality obligations, whether contractual or statutory.

4.3 **Security Measures.** ClawOps will implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect Personal Data against Security Incidents, including:

- Encryption of Personal Data in transit (TLS 1.2 or higher) and at rest
- Secure credential storage using encrypted password vaults
- Role-based access controls limiting access to authorized personnel only
- Regular review of security practices
- Secure deletion of Personal Data in accordance with the retention schedule

4.4 **Personnel.** ClawOps will ensure that access to Personal Data is limited to personnel who need access to fulfill obligations under the Agreement and that such personnel are trained on data protection requirements.

---

## 5. Sub-Processors

5.1 **Authorization.** Client provides general written authorization for ClawOps to engage the Sub-Processors listed in Annex 2. ClawOps will impose data protection obligations on each Sub-Processor that are no less protective than those in this DPA.

5.2 **Notification of Changes.** ClawOps will notify Client at least 14 days before adding or replacing a Sub-Processor by updating the Sub-Processor list and notifying Client by email.

5.3 **Right to Object.** Client may object to a new Sub-Processor by providing written notice to ClawOps within 14 days of receiving notification. If the objection is not resolved within 30 days, Client may terminate the affected Services without penalty.

5.4 **Liability.** ClawOps remains liable for the acts and omissions of its Sub-Processors to the same extent as if ClawOps performed the processing directly.

---

## 6. Data Subject Rights

6.1 ClawOps will, taking into account the nature of the processing, assist Client by appropriate technical and organizational measures (insofar as possible) in fulfilling Client's obligations to respond to Data Subject requests to exercise their rights under Applicable Data Protection Law, including rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and objection.

6.2 If ClawOps receives a request directly from a Data Subject, ClawOps will promptly redirect the Data Subject to Client and notify Client of the request, unless prohibited by law.

---

## 7. Security Incident Response

7.1 **Notification.** ClawOps will notify Client of any confirmed Security Incident without undue delay and in any event within 48 hours of becoming aware of the incident.

7.2 **Contents of Notification.** The notification will include, to the extent known:

(a) A description of the nature of the Security Incident, including categories and approximate number of Data Subjects and records affected

(b) The name and contact details of the point of contact at ClawOps

(c) A description of the likely consequences of the Security Incident

(d) A description of measures taken or proposed to address the Security Incident, including measures to mitigate potential adverse effects

7.3 **Cooperation.** ClawOps will cooperate with Client and take reasonable steps to assist in the investigation, mitigation, and remediation of the Security Incident.

7.4 **No Admission.** ClawOps' notification of a Security Incident does not constitute an acknowledgment of fault or liability.

---

## 8. Data Retention and Deletion

8.1 **Retention.** ClawOps will retain Personal Data only for as long as necessary to provide the Services and in accordance with the data retention schedule set forth in the Agreement or this DPA.

8.2 **Post-Engagement Deletion.** Upon termination or expiration of the Agreement, or upon Client's written request, ClawOps will:

(a) Return all Personal Data to Client in a commonly used, machine-readable format, or

(b) Delete all Personal Data (including copies) within 30 days

unless retention is required by applicable law. ClawOps will confirm deletion in writing upon Client's request.

8.3 **Retention Exceptions.** ClawOps may retain Personal Data beyond the standard retention period only where required by applicable law or regulation. ClawOps will inform Client of any such requirement and will limit processing to that which is necessary to comply with the legal obligation.

---

## 9. Data Transfers

9.1 **Transfer Locations.** Personal Data may be transferred to and processed in the jurisdictions identified in Annex 2 (Sub-Processor List).

9.2 **Transfer Safeguards.** Where Personal Data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or any jurisdiction with data transfer restrictions, ClawOps will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

(a) Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, or

(b) Other legally recognized transfer mechanisms under Applicable Data Protection Law

9.3 **Supplementary Measures.** Where required, ClawOps will implement supplementary technical and organizational measures to ensure the transferred Personal Data is afforded a level of protection equivalent to that in the originating jurisdiction.

---

## 10. Audits and Compliance

10.1 **Information Availability.** ClawOps will make available to Client all information reasonably necessary to demonstrate compliance with this DPA and Applicable Data Protection Law.

10.2 **Audit Rights.** Client (or an independent auditor appointed by Client) may conduct an audit of ClawOps' processing activities no more than once per 12-month period, with at least 30 days' written notice. Audits shall be conducted during normal business hours and shall not unreasonably interfere with ClawOps' operations.

10.3 **Costs.** Each party shall bear its own costs in connection with audits.

10.4 **Confidentiality.** Any information obtained during an audit is subject to the confidentiality provisions of the Agreement.

---

## 11. Data Protection Impact Assessments

11.1 ClawOps will provide reasonable assistance to Client in conducting data protection impact assessments and prior consultations with supervisory authorities, where required under Applicable Data Protection Law, taking into account the nature of processing and the information available to ClawOps.

---

## 12. General Provisions

12.1 **Conflicts.** In the event of a conflict between this DPA and the Agreement, this DPA shall prevail with respect to the processing of Personal Data.

12.2 **Amendments.** This DPA may be amended only in writing signed by both parties.

12.3 **Governing Law.** This DPA is governed by the same governing law as the Agreement.

12.4 **Severability.** If any provision of this DPA is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

12.5 **Term.** This DPA shall remain in effect for the duration of ClawOps' processing of Personal Data on behalf of Client.
