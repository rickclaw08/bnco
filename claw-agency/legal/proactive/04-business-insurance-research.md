## Essential Coverage Types

### 1. Technology Errors & Omissions (Tech E&O) - PRIORITY #1

**What it covers:**
- Claims of negligence or failure to deliver promised services
- Missed deadlines that cause client financial loss
- Errors in automation workflows that damage client operations
- Claims that your AI solution didn't perform as promised
- Defense costs even if the claim is baseless

**Why it's critical for us:** We're building automations that touch client revenue, data, and operations. If an automation breaks and costs a client money, this is our shield.

**Estimated cost:** $500 - $2,000/year for a small agency (<$500K revenue)

---

### 2. Cyber Liability Insurance - PRIORITY #1

**What it covers:**
- Data breaches affecting client data we handle
- Ransomware and cyberattack recovery
- Client notification costs after a breach
- Forensic investigation costs
- Regulatory fines and penalties
- Credit monitoring for affected individuals

**Why it's critical for us:** We handle client data, API keys, and access credentials. A breach could be catastrophic.

**Estimated cost:** $500 - $1,500/year for a small agency

---

### 3. General Liability Insurance - PRIORITY #2

**What it covers:**
- Bodily injury (someone gets hurt at your office/meeting)
- Property damage
- Libel, slander, advertising injury
- Medical payments

**Why it matters:** Basic business protection. Many co-working spaces and clients require proof of general liability.

**Estimated cost:** $300 - $600/year (often bundled in a BOP)

---

### 4. Business Owner's Policy (BOP) - RECOMMENDED BUNDLE

**What it covers:** Combines general liability + commercial property insurance at a discount.

**Estimated cost:** $500 - $1,000/year

---

### 5. Professional Liability (if not covered by Tech E&O)

**What it covers:** Similar to E&O but broader. Covers consulting advice that leads to client losses.

**Estimated cost:** Often bundled with E&O, $400 - $1,200/year standalone

## When to Buy

- **Before signing first client:** E&O and Cyber Liability
- **Before leasing office space:** General Liability / BOP
- **Before hiring employees:** Workers' Compensation (required by NY law)
- **As revenue grows:** Increase coverage limits, add umbrella policy

---

## Additional Considerations

### Workers' Compensation
- **Required in NY** once you have employees (even 1)
- Covers employee injuries/illness
- Cost varies by role and payroll

### Umbrella / Excess Liability
- Extra layer above other policies
- Consider when revenue exceeds $500K or working with enterprise clients
- Typically $1M+ in additional coverage for $300-$500/year

### Fidelity Bond
- Covers employee theft or fraud
- Important if employees have access to client financial systems
- $100-$300/year
