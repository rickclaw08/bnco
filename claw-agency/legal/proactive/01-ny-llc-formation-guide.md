## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Choose Your LLC Name
- Must be distinguishable from existing NY businesses
- Must include "LLC" or "Limited Liability Company"
- Search availability: https://apps.dos.ny.gov/publicInquiry/
- **Suggested:** "Claw Agency LLC" or "Claw Automation LLC"

### Step 2: Choose a County for Publication
- NY requires publishing ads in 2 newspapers for 6 consecutive weeks
- Pick a cheap county (Albany, not Manhattan) to save hundreds
- A registered agent with an address in a cheaper county enables this

### Step 3: Appoint a Registered Agent (Optional but Recommended)
- Person or company that receives legal documents on your behalf
- Options: Northwest ($39/yr), LegalZoom ($249/yr), or yourself (free but your address is public)
- **Recommendation:** Use a registered agent for privacy and to leverage a cheap publication county

### Step 4: File Articles of Organization
- File online with NY Division of Corporations: https://www.dos.ny.gov/corps/llcfaq.asp
- **Cost:** $200
- **Online filing:** Approved immediately
- **Mail filing:** 2-3 weeks
- Required info: LLC name, county, registered agent, organizer name/address

### Step 5: Create an Operating Agreement
- **Required by NY law** (even for single-member LLCs)
- Not filed with the state, kept internally
- Defines ownership, profit distribution, management structure
- Template will be drafted separately

### Step 6: Get an EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- Free from IRS: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
- Takes 5 minutes online
- Needed for bank accounts, taxes, hiring

### Step 7: Publish Newspaper Ads (Publication Requirement)
- Must be completed within 120 days of LLC formation
- Run ads in 2 newspapers (1 daily, 1 weekly) for 6 consecutive weeks
- The county clerk designates which newspapers to use
- Contact the county clerk's office where your LLC is filed

### Step 8: File Certificate of Publication
- After completing the 6 weeks of newspaper ads
- File with NY Division of Corporations
- **Cost:** $50
- **Deadline:** 120 days from formation
- **Penalty for missing deadline:** LLC can lose authority to conduct business in NY

### Step 9: Open a Business Bank Account
- Bring: Articles of Organization, EIN letter, Operating Agreement, photo ID
- Keep business and personal finances separate (protects LLC liability shield)

## Ongoing Requirements

- **Biennial Statement:** File every 2 years ($9) with NY Division of Corporations
- **NY State Taxes:** File IT-204 (partnership) or report on personal return (single-member)
- **NYC Unincorporated Business Tax:** If operating in NYC, 4% tax on income over $65,000
- **Annual report:** None (NY uses biennial statements instead)
