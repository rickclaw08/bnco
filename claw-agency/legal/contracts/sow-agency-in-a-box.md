## 1. Project Overview

Provider will deliver a white-label AI receptionist platform that Client can resell to its own customers under Client's brand. The package includes a branded platform, custom voice configuration, an agency management dashboard, per-seat deployment capability, training, and initial client deployments.

**Business Objectives:**
- Enable Client to offer AI receptionist services under its own brand
- Provide Client with a turnkey platform for deploying and managing AI receptionists at scale
- Generate recurring revenue for Client through per-seat resale
- Minimize Client's technical overhead through Provider-managed infrastructure

---

## 2. Scope of Work

### 2.1 Deliverables

**A. White-Label Branded Platform**
- AI receptionist platform branded with Client's logo, colors, and domain
- Custom landing page and client-facing portal under Client's brand
- White-label call recordings, transcripts, and reporting (no Provider branding visible to end users)
- Custom email notifications and communications from Client's domain

**B. Custom Voice Configuration**
- Selection and configuration of AI voice persona aligned with Client's brand identity
- Up to three (3) distinct voice profiles for different use cases or tiers
- Voice tone, pacing, and style customization
- Custom greeting and sign-off scripts per voice profile

**C. Agency Management Dashboard**
- Centralized dashboard for Client to manage all deployed AI receptionist seats
- Per-client analytics: call volume, lead captures, outcomes, and trends
- Seat provisioning: add, pause, or remove client deployments
- Billing visibility: usage tracking per seat for Client's invoicing purposes
- Role-based access control (admin, manager, viewer)

**D. Per-Seat Deployment Capability**
- Standardized deployment workflow for spinning up new AI receptionist instances
- Per-seat configuration: unique business info, scripts, FAQs, CRM connections, and call routing
- Self-service seat setup through the agency dashboard (with Provider support as needed)
- Estimated deployment time per new seat: 1-2 business days after Client provides end-client materials

**E. Initial Client Deployments (Up to 3)**
- Provider will deploy AI receptionist service for up to three (3) of Client's end customers as part of this SOW
- Each deployment includes: AI training on the end client's business, CRM integration, call routing, appointment scheduling, and SMS follow-up
- These initial deployments serve as proof-of-concept and training for Client's team

**F. Agency Training**
- Live training session(s) for Client's team covering:
  - Platform administration and dashboard usage
  - New seat deployment and onboarding workflow
  - Troubleshooting common issues
  - Best practices for selling and positioning AI receptionist services
- Up to four (4) hours of live training, delivered via video call
- Recorded training sessions provided for future reference

**G. Marketing Materials**
- White-label sales deck (editable, in Client's branding)
- One-page service overview / sell sheet
- Email templates for outreach and onboarding
- Sample case study template

### 2.2 Out of Scope

The following are not included in this SOW:
- End-client billing or payment collection (Client handles its own invoicing)
- Custom mobile app development
- Outbound calling or cold call campaigns
- Hardware of any kind
- Third-party software licensing fees (CRM, calendar, telephony, etc.)
- Legal review of Client's reseller agreements with its end customers
- Deployments beyond the initial three (3) included seats
- Services not explicitly described in Section 2.1

Out-of-scope work may be added via a separate SOW or written change order.

3.6. **Timely Feedback:** Responses to Provider requests within three (3) business days. Delays in Client feedback may extend the project timeline.

## 6. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

6.1. **Platform Uptime.** Provider guarantees 99.5% uptime for the white-label platform and all active AI receptionist seats, measured monthly. Uptime excludes scheduled maintenance (with at least 24 hours' notice) and downtime caused by Client's systems or third-party services.

6.2. **New Seat Deployment.** Provider will deploy new seats within two (2) business days of receiving complete end-client materials from Client.

6.3. **SLA Credits.** If platform uptime falls below 99.5% in any calendar month, Client is entitled to a service credit equal to:
- 99.0% - 99.4% uptime: 5% of that month's total seat fees
- 98.0% - 98.9% uptime: 10% of that month's total seat fees
- Below 98.0% uptime: 20% of that month's total seat fees

SLA credits are Client's sole remedy for uptime failures. Credits are applied to the next invoice and do not accumulate beyond one month's total seat fees. Client must request credits in writing within fifteen (15) days of the affected month.

6.4. **Exclusions.** The SLA does not apply to outages caused by: (a) Client's or end client's systems, networks, or phone providers, (b) third-party services outside Provider's control, (c) force majeure events, or (d) Client-requested changes during implementation.

## 7. White-Label Terms

7.1. **Branding.** The platform will display Client's branding exclusively. Provider's branding will not be visible to Client's end customers at any point.

7.2. **Resale Rights.** Client may resell the AI receptionist service to its own customers at prices determined by Client. Provider has no relationship with, and no obligations to, Client's end customers.

7.3. **Client Responsibility for End Customers.** Client is solely responsible for: (a) its own customer agreements and terms of service, (b) billing and collections from its end customers, (c) first-line support for its end customers, and (d) compliance with applicable laws in connection with its resale activities.

7.4. **Provider Support.** Provider will provide second-line technical support to Client (not directly to Client's end customers) via the channels specified in this SOW.

7.5. **Non-Compete.** During the term of this SOW, Provider will not directly solicit Client's end customers who were deployed through Client's white-label platform. This restriction does not apply to end customers who independently contact Provider or who were Provider's customers before this SOW.

## 9. Term and Termination

9.1. This SOW begins on the date of execution and continues for an initial term of twelve (12) months. After the initial term, it continues month-to-month.

9.2. Either party may terminate this SOW with thirty (30) days' written notice per the MSA.

9.3. Upon termination:
- (a) Provider will export and deliver all Client Data, end-client data, call recordings, and platform configurations within fifteen (15) business days.
- (b) Provider will maintain active seats for up to thirty (30) days during transition (at the standard per-seat rate) to allow Client to migrate end customers.
- (c) Client's white-label branding will be removed from the platform upon termination.
- (d) Client is responsible for fees through the end of the notice period plus any transition period usage.

## 10. Assumptions

- Client has or will obtain a domain name for the white-label platform portal.
- Client has the legal right to resell services to its end customers.
- Combined call volume across all active seats does not exceed 50,000 calls per month. Higher volumes may require custom infrastructure and pricing.
- All calls are within the United States. International call handling is available as an add-on.
- Client will provide first-line support to its end customers; Provider provides second-line support to Client.
