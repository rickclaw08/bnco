## 1. Definitions

**"Service"** means ClawOps' AI-powered telephone receptionist service, including call answering, call routing, appointment scheduling, and business hours configuration as described in Section 3.

**"Lifetime Access"** means access to the Service for as long as ClawOps continues to operate the Service commercially. This term is further defined and limited in Section 5.

**"Go-Live Date"** means the date on which the Member's AI receptionist is activated and operational.

## 2. Founding Member Fee

### 2.1 One-Time Payment

The Member agrees to pay a one-time Founding Member fee of **$1,997 USD** ("Founding Member Fee").

### 2.3 Payment

## 3. Included Services

### 3.1 What's Included

The Founding Member Fee includes the following for the lifetime of the Service:

- **One (1) dedicated phone number** - A local or toll-free phone number assigned to the Member's AI receptionist
- **One (1) custom greeting** - A personalized greeting script tailored to the Member's business
- **Call routing** - Intelligent routing of calls to designated team members based on configurable rules (by department, inquiry type, availability, or business hours)
- **Appointment booking** - Integration with the Member's calendar system (Google Calendar, Outlook, or Calendly) for real-time appointment scheduling during calls
- **Business hours configuration** - Configurable business hours with after-hours handling (voicemail, custom message, or emergency routing)
- **Basic call analytics** - Access to a dashboard showing call volume, call outcomes, and basic reporting
- **AI training updates** - Periodic updates to the AI model as ClawOps improves its platform (applied globally to all users)
- **Standard support** - Email support with response within one (1) business day

### 3.2 What's NOT Included

The following are **not** included in the Founding Member Fee and are available as paid add-ons:

| Add-On | Price |
|--------|-------|
| Additional phone numbers | $50/month per number |
| Custom integrations (CRM, proprietary systems) | Quoted per project |
| API access | Quoted per use case |
| Priority support (4-hour response, dedicated channel) | $150/month |
| Additional language support | $100/month per language |
| Custom reporting or analytics | Quoted per request |
| SMS follow-up packages | Quoted per volume tier |

Add-on pricing is subject to change with thirty (30) days' written notice.

## 5. Lifetime Access - Definition and Limitations

### 5.1 Duration

"Lifetime Access" means the Member retains access to the Service for as long as ClawOps continues to commercially operate the AI receptionist service. This is **not** a perpetual or irrevocable grant.

### 5.2 Service Continuity

ClawOps intends to operate the Service indefinitely. However, ClawOps reserves the right to discontinue the Service entirely if:

- The Service becomes commercially unviable
- Regulatory changes make the Service illegal or impracticable
- Third-party dependencies (AI providers, telephony infrastructure) become unavailable without a reasonable substitute

### 5.3 Discontinuation Notice

If ClawOps decides to discontinue the Service, it will provide Founding Members with at least **ninety (90) days' written notice** before termination. In the event of discontinuation within the first twenty-four (24) months after the Member's Go-Live Date, ClawOps will refund a pro-rata portion of the Founding Member Fee based on remaining months.

### 5.4 Feature Modifications

ClawOps reserves the right to modify, update, or change the features of the Service with **thirty (30) days' written notice** to Founding Members. ClawOps will use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain feature parity or provide equivalent functionality. Feature modifications will not result in a material reduction of the core services described in Section 3.1.
