## 1. Services

Agency agrees to provide AI automation services as described in individual Statements of Work (SOWs). Each SOW will specify:

- Project scope and deliverables
- Timeline and milestones
- Pricing and payment terms
- Specific acceptance criteria

## 2. Term and Termination

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice. Either party may terminate immediately if:

- The other party breaches a material term and fails to cure within 15 days of written notice
- The other party becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy
- Services under all active SOWs are completed

Termination does not affect payment obligations for work completed or in progress.

## 3. Fees and Payment

**Payment Terms:**
- Invoices are due within 15 days of receipt
- Late payments incur 1.5% monthly interest
- Agency may suspend services if payment is more than 30 days overdue

**Pricing Tiers:**
- Starter: $500 (one-time projects)
- Growth: $2,000/month (ongoing support)
- Enterprise: $5,000-$15,000/month (custom solutions)

Specific pricing will be detailed in each SOW. All fees are non-refundable except as explicitly stated in an SOW.

**Client IP:** Client retains all rights to their proprietary information, branding, and existing materials.

**Deliverables:** Upon full payment, Agency transfers ownership of custom work product created specifically for Client under an SOW. This includes:
- Custom automation workflows
- Integration configurations
- Documentation written for Client's systems

**Agency IP:** Agency retains ownership of:
- Pre-existing tools, templates, and frameworks
- General methodologies and processes
- Improvements to Agency's own systems
- Any work not specifically commissioned in an SOW

**License:** Client receives a perpetual, non-exclusive license to use any Agency IP incorporated into deliverables.

## 5. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to:
- Keep confidential information secret
- Use it only for the purposes of this Agreement
- Not disclose it without prior written consent

Confidential information does not include information that:
- Was publicly known before disclosure
- Becomes public through no fault of the receiving party
- Was independently developed
- Was rightfully received from a third party

Confidentiality obligations survive termination for 3 years.
