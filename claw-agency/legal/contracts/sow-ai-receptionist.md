## 1. Project Overview

Provider will deploy a custom AI-powered phone receptionist for Client's business. The AI receptionist will answer inbound calls, capture lead information, route calls to the appropriate team members, schedule appointments, and send automated SMS follow-ups, all trained on Client's specific business information, scripts, and FAQs.

**Business Objectives:**
- Ensure every inbound call is answered promptly and professionally
- Capture and qualify leads automatically
- Reduce missed calls and lost business opportunities
- Free up staff time by handling routine inquiries via AI

---

## 2. Scope of Work

### 2.1 Deliverables

**A. Custom-Trained AI Receptionist**
- AI voice agent trained on Client's business information, services, pricing, and FAQs
- Natural, conversational voice interaction (not robotic or menu-driven)
- Configurable greeting, tone, and call handling rules
- Support for call screening, message taking, and basic Q&A
- Multi-language support available upon request (additional fees may apply)

**B. CRM Integration**
- Integration with Client's existing CRM system (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel, or equivalent)
- Automatic creation of new lead records from inbound calls
- Call notes, transcripts, and caller details synced to CRM in real time
- Custom field mapping based on Client's CRM configuration

**C. Call Routing**
- Intelligent call routing to designated team members based on configurable rules
- Routing by department, inquiry type, availability, or business hours
- Warm transfer with context (AI briefs the team member before connecting)
- Fallback routing and voicemail handling for after-hours or unavailable staff

**D. Appointment Scheduling**
- Integration with Client's calendar system (Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly, or equivalent)
- AI schedules appointments directly during the call based on real-time availability
- Automatic confirmation sent to both Client and caller
- Rescheduling and cancellation handling

**E. SMS Follow-Up**
- Automated SMS sent to callers after the call (e.g., confirmation, next steps, links)
- Configurable SMS templates and triggers
- Opt-in compliance handling (TCPA)

**F. Dashboard and Reporting**
- Web-based dashboard showing call volume, lead captures, call outcomes, and trends
- Call recordings and transcripts accessible on demand
- Weekly summary reports delivered via email

### 2.2 Out of Scope

The following are not included in this SOW:
- Outbound calling campaigns or cold calling
- Custom mobile app development
- Hardware (phones, headsets, or other equipment)
- Third-party software licensing fees (CRM, calendar, etc.)
- Modifications to Client's existing phone system beyond what is needed for call forwarding
- Services not explicitly described in Section 2.1

Out-of-scope work may be added via a separate SOW or written change order.

## 6. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

6.1. **Uptime.** Provider guarantees 99.5% uptime for the AI receptionist service, measured monthly. Uptime excludes scheduled maintenance (with at least 24 hours' notice) and downtime caused by Client's systems or third-party services.

6.2. **Response Time.** The AI receptionist will answer inbound calls within three (3) rings under normal operating conditions.

6.3. **SLA Credits.** If uptime falls below 99.5% in any calendar month, Client is entitled to a service credit equal to:
- 99.0% - 99.4% uptime: 5% of that month's fee
- 98.0% - 98.9% uptime: 10% of that month's fee
- Below 98.0% uptime: 20% of that month's fee

SLA credits are Client's sole remedy for uptime failures. Credits are applied to the next invoice and do not accumulate beyond one month's fee. Client must request credits in writing within fifteen (15) days of the affected month.

6.4. **Exclusions.** The SLA does not apply to outages caused by: (a) Client's systems, networks, or phone provider, (b) third-party services outside Provider's control, (c) force majeure events, or (d) Client-requested changes during implementation.
