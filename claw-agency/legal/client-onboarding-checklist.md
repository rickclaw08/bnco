## Phase 1: Pre-Engagement (Before Any Work Begins)

### Agreements

- [ ] **Master Services Agreement (MSA) signed** - Both parties signed. Filed in project folder.
  - *Skip if Client is on Starter tier and using the combined Small Project Agreement.*
- [ ] **Statement of Work (SOW) signed** - Scope, deliverables, timeline, fees, and acceptance criteria defined and agreed.
- [ ] **Data Processing Addendum (DPA) signed** - Required if engagement involves any personal data or PII.
  - *If Client confirms no personal data is involved, document that confirmation in writing.*
- [ ] **Pre-Existing IP Schedule attached to SOW** - Lists ClawOps templates, tools, and frameworks that may be incorporated into deliverables.

### Shared Responsibility

- [ ] **Shared Responsibility Matrix reviewed with Client** - Walk through the matrix during kickoff. Ensure Client understands their responsibilities (data accuracy, consents, production monitoring, etc.).
- [ ] **Client acknowledges AI output disclaimer** - Client understands AI outputs require review before use and that ClawOps does not guarantee accuracy of AI-generated content.

### Data and Access

- [ ] **Client data types identified** - Document what data Client will provide (CRM records, emails, customer data, financial data, etc.).
- [ ] **Personal data assessment completed** - Determine if engagement involves personal data / PII. If yes, ensure DPA is signed.
- [ ] **Special category data check** - Confirm whether Client's data includes sensitive categories (health, biometric, financial, children's data). If yes, discuss additional safeguards.
- [ ] **Client consents confirmed** - Client confirms they have obtained all necessary consents from their end users / data subjects for ClawOps to process data.
- [ ] **Industry compliance noted** - Identify any industry-specific regulations Client must comply with (HIPAA, SOX, PCI-DSS, FERPA, etc.) and document any special handling requirements.

### Payment

- [ ] **Deposit invoice sent** - Per SOW payment schedule (typically 50% upfront for project engagements).
- [ ] **Deposit payment received** - Do not begin work until deposit is received.
- [ ] **Payment terms confirmed** - Client acknowledges 15-day payment terms and late payment policy.

## Phase 2: Engagement Kickoff

### Access and Credentials

- [ ] **Client access provided** - Client has granted access to all required systems, platforms, and accounts as identified in the SOW.
- [ ] **Credentials stored securely** - All client credentials (API keys, passwords, tokens) stored in encrypted vault (never in plaintext, email, or code repos).
- [ ] **Access documented** - List of all systems and accounts accessed is recorded in the project folder.

### Project Setup

- [ ] **Designated Client contact confirmed** - Name, email, and phone of the person authorized to make decisions and approve deliverables.
- [ ] **Communication channel established** - Agree on primary communication method (email, Slack, etc.) and expected response times.
- [ ] **Project folder created** - With appropriate access controls.
- [ ] **Data retention schedule communicated** - Client is informed of our retention schedule (30 days post-engagement for most data).
- [ ] **Sub-processor list shared** - Client has reviewed the list of sub-processors (OpenAI, Anthropic, Make.com, Zapier, Google).

---

## Phase 3: During Engagement

### Ongoing Compliance

- [ ] **Scope changes documented** - Any scope changes are captured in a written change order signed by both parties before work begins.
- [ ] **Client approvals obtained in writing** - Key decisions and approvals are documented via email or project management tool.
- [ ] **Data handling rules followed** - No client data stored in personal drives, unencrypted locations, or unauthorized systems.
- [ ] **AI outputs flagged for review** - All AI-generated content is clearly identified and submitted to Client for review before production use.

---

## Phase 4: Delivery and Acceptance

### Deliverable Handoff

- [ ] **All deliverables submitted** - Per SOW scope and timeline.
- [ ] **Documentation provided** - Written documentation of all automations, including setup instructions and troubleshooting guide.
- [ ] **Client review period initiated** - Client has 5 business days to review and accept or reject (with specific reasons).
- [ ] **Acceptance received** - Client has signed off on deliverables (or deemed accepted after review period).

### Final Payment

- [ ] **Final invoice sent** - Per SOW payment schedule.
- [ ] **Final payment received** - IP assignment takes effect upon full payment.

---

## Phase 5: Post-Engagement Closeout

### Data Cleanup

- [ ] **Client credentials deleted** - All API keys, passwords, and tokens removed from encrypted vault within 7 days of engagement end.
- [ ] **Client data deleted or returned** - All Client Data deleted or returned within 30 days of engagement end, per retention schedule.
- [ ] **Deletion confirmed in writing** - Send written confirmation to Client that data has been deleted (upon request or proactively for Enterprise clients).
- [ ] **AI logs auto-deleted** - Confirm that AI prompts and output logs have been purged (30-day auto-deletion).

### Knowledge Transfer

- [ ] **All documentation delivered** - Ensure Client has complete documentation for all automations.
- [ ] **Handoff call completed** - Walk Client through delivered automations, answer questions.
- [ ] **Support period communicated** - Client understands the duration and scope of post-delivery support (per SOW).

### Records Retention

- [ ] **Project communications archived** - Retained for 1 year post-engagement.
- [ ] **Invoices and financial records filed** - Retained for 7 years (tax/legal requirement).
- [ ] **Signed agreements filed** - MSA, SOW, DPA, and any change orders archived permanently.
