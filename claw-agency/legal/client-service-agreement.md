## 1. Overview of Services

ClawOps provides an AI-powered receptionist and call handling service designed for home service contractors. By signing this Agreement, you are engaging ClawOps to build, deploy, and maintain a custom AI voice agent for your business.

### 1.1 What You Get

- **Custom AI Voice Agent** - A voice agent trained specifically on your business, answering calls 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.
- **CRM Dashboard** - Full access to a customer relationship management platform including contacts, sales pipeline, calendar, and conversation history.
- **5 Automated Workflows:**
  1. New Lead Notification & Follow-Up
  2. Booking Confirmation
  3. Missed Call Recovery
  4. Review Generation
  5. No-Show Recovery
- **Dedicated Local Phone Number** - A local phone number for your business, managed by ClawOps.
- **Calendar Integration** - Synced with your existing scheduling tools.
- **Monthly Optimization & Prompt Tuning** - We continuously improve your AI agent's performance based on real call data.
- **Monthly Review Call** - A 15-minute call each month to review performance, discuss improvements, and align on priorities.
- **Priority Support** - Direct access to the ClawOps team for questions, issues, or requests.
