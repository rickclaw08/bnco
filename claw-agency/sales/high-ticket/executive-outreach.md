## Outreach Strategy Notes

**Timing:**
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 6am-9am or 1pm-3pm local time (highest open rates for B2B)
- Follow up 3 days later if no response (different subject: "Quick follow-up - [topic]")
- Third touch 5 days after that (different angle: case study or free resource)

**Personalization checklist before sending:**
- Replace [Company], [First Name], [specific detail]
- Reference recent funding, growth milestone, or job posting
- Adjust dollar figures based on company size
- Adjust tone based on industry (fintech = more formal, agency = more casual)

**Response handling:**
- "Send me more info" → Send 1-pager sprint package PDF + calendar link
- "What's the pricing?" → "Depends on scope, but most clients in your space pay $10K-$15K. Want to hop on a quick call to see if it makes sense?"
- "Not right now" → "Totally understand. Mind if I check back in 90 days?" (set reminder)
- No response after 3 touches → Move to nurture list, try again in 6 months

**Success metrics:**
- Target open rate: 40-60% (personalized subject lines)
- Target response rate: 10-20% (high-value, specific offers to qualified prospects)
- Target conversion (response → call): 60%+
- Target conversion (call → deal): 30-40%

**Math:**
- 100 emails sent → 15 responses → 9 calls booked → 3-4 deals closed
- At $10K-$15K average deal size, that's $30K-$60K from one email batch
- Send 20 emails/day (personalized) = 100 emails/week = steady pipeline

---

Key differentiators in messaging:
1. **Specific pain points** (not generic "we automate stuff")
2. **Time/cost quantification** (20 hours/week = $50K/year)
3. **Fast results** (1 week, not 3 months)
4. **Industry-specific examples** (not one-size-fits-all)
5. **Clear ROI** (payback in 2-3 months)
6. **Alternative framing** ("Before you hire that ops coordinator, let's talk")
