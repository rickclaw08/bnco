## Qualification Criteria

Before outreach, verify:
- **Revenue:** $5M-$50M (sweet spot for high-ticket deals)
- **Team size:** 10-200 people (enough complexity, not too big to move fast)
- **Tool spend:** Using Zapier, Make, HubSpot, or similar (signals automation awareness + budget)
- **Decision-maker access:** Can you find VP/Director of Ops on LinkedIn?
- **Recent funding or growth:** Series A/B companies are perfect (money to spend, urgency to scale)
