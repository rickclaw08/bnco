## Show Your Prospects the Money

*Use this document as a leave-behind or walk through it live on a call. Customize the numbers for each prospect's situation.*

## THE CORE EQUATION

```
Your Revenue = (Number of Clients) × (Your Monthly Price) 
Your Cost    = (Number of Clients) × $100/mo + $5,000 setup
Your Profit  = Revenue - Cost
```

That's it. Everything below is just scenarios.

## THE "JUST 10 CLIENTS" BENCHMARK

This is the number to anchor on. Most GHL agencies have 10-30 clients. Deploying to just 10 looks like this:

```
10 clients × $500/mo retail price          = $5,000/mo revenue
10 clients × $100/mo cost to us            = $1,000/mo cost
                                             ─────────────────
Monthly recurring profit                   = $4,000/mo
Annual recurring profit                    = $48,000/year
Setup investment                           = $5,000
Payback period                             = 1.25 months
First-year ROI                             = 860%
```

**$5,000 in → $48,000 out. That's the pitch.**

## CLIENT-LEVEL ROI (What Your Clients See)

Show your clients this math when selling them the service:

### For a Dental Practice:
- Average new patient value: $1,200/year
- Currently missing ~40% of calls
- Gets ~100 calls/month, missing ~40
- AI receptionist catches all 40 missed calls
- Converts 25% of those to booked appointments = **10 new patients/month**
- **Value of recovered calls: $12,000/year**
- **Cost of AI receptionist: $4,800/year ($400/mo)**
- **Client ROI: 150%**

### For an HVAC Company:
- Average service call value: $350
- Currently missing ~50% of calls (technicians are on jobs)
- Gets ~200 calls/month, missing ~100
- AI books 30% as service appointments = **30 new jobs/month**
- **Value of recovered calls: $10,500/month ($126,000/year)**
- **Cost of AI receptionist: $400/mo ($4,800/year)**
- **Client ROI: 2,525%**

### For a Law Firm:
- Average case value: $3,000-$10,000
- Currently missing ~35% of calls (staff at lunch, after hours, busy)
- Gets ~150 calls/month, missing ~52
- AI captures and qualifies all 52 missed calls
- 15% convert to consultations = **8 new consults/month**
- Even at conservative $3K/case: **$24,000/month in new revenue**
- **Cost of AI receptionist: $500/mo ($6,000/year)**
- **Client ROI: off the charts**

### The Universal Pitch:
> "What's one new customer worth to you? [$X]. And you're missing [Y] calls per month. If we capture even a fraction of those, this pays for itself multiple times over. The question isn't whether you can afford $400/mo for AI receptionist — it's whether you can afford NOT to have one."

## MARGIN ANALYSIS

### At $300/mo Retail (Budget Tier):
- Revenue per client: $300/mo
- Cost per client: $100/mo
- **Margin: $200/mo (67%)**
- Break-even on $5K setup: 25 client-months (e.g., 5 clients × 5 months)

### At $400/mo Retail (Standard Tier):
- Revenue per client: $400/mo
- Cost per client: $100/mo
- **Margin: $300/mo (75%)**
- Break-even on $5K setup: 17 client-months (e.g., 6 clients × 3 months)

### At $500/mo Retail (Premium Tier):
- Revenue per client: $500/mo
- Cost per client: $100/mo
- **Margin: $400/mo (80%)**
- Break-even on $5K setup: 13 client-months (e.g., 5 clients × 3 months)

### At $997/mo Retail (Enterprise/White-Glove):
- Revenue per client: $997/mo
- Cost per client: $100/mo
- **Margin: $897/mo (90%)**
- Break-even on $5K setup: 6 client-months (e.g., 3 clients × 2 months)
