## OUTREACH BEST PRACTICES

### Reddit:
- **Never** DM someone without first engaging with their post (comment something helpful publicly)
- Wait at least 30 minutes between your public comment and the DM
- Reference their specific situation — generic DMs get ignored
- Don't pitch in the subreddit directly (you'll get banned). Value first, DM second.
- Keep your Reddit profile active with helpful GHL content (not just promotion)

### LinkedIn:
- Personalize the connection request with something specific about their profile
- Don't pitch in the connection request — just establish relevance
- Wait 24-48 hours after they accept before sending the follow-up
- Engage with their content (like/comment) before DMing
- If they don't respond to the first follow-up, wait 5-7 days and send ONE nudge. Then stop.

### For Both Platforms:
- Your goal is to get them on a 15-min demo call. That's it.
- Don't sell in the DM. Create enough curiosity that they want to see it live.
- If they ask questions, answer briefly and redirect to a call.
- Track every outreach in a spreadsheet: username, date, platform, thread URL, response status.

## RESPONSE HANDLING

**If they say "Interesting, tell me more":**
> "The best way to see it is a quick demo — I'll call a number live and you'll hear the AI answer as a real business. Takes 15 min. When works for you this week?"

**If they say "What's the catch?":**
> "No catch. $5K setup, $100/mo per seat, no minimums, no long-term contracts. We make money when you deploy to more clients, so our incentives are aligned. Happy to walk through it on a quick call."

**If they say "Send me info":**
> "Sure, I'll send over a one-pager. But honestly, the thing that sells this is hearing it work. 15 minutes — I'll call the demo line, you listen to a real AI receptionist handle a call in a custom voice with live booking. When's good?"

**If they ghost:**
> Wait 5-7 days, then: "Hey [name], just bumping this. Totally understand if the timing isn't right — just wanted to make sure it didn't get buried. Let me know either way and I'll update my notes. 👍"
