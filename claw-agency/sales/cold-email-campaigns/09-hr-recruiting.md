Here are the three automations that drive the biggest results for recruiting firms:

1. **AI resume screening and ranking** - Every application gets scored against the job requirements automatically. Recruiters see a ranked shortlist with match explanations instead of reading through 200 resumes. Works with your existing ATS (Bullhorn, JobAdder, Greenhouse, Lever, etc.).

2. **Self-service interview scheduling** - Candidates pick their own interview slots based on real-time recruiter and hiring manager availability. Automated reminders, rescheduling, and confirmation. Eliminates the 5-8 emails it takes to schedule one interview.

3. **Automated candidate and client communication** - Status updates, rejection notices, offer letter workflows, and placement follow-ups all triggered automatically based on pipeline stage. Candidates and hiring managers stay informed without your recruiters writing individual emails.

Recruiting firms that implement these typically see 25-40% more placements per recruiter without increasing headcount.
