## SLIDE 4: CASE STUDY

### How a Distribution Company Eliminated 18 Hours/Week of Pricing Updates

**The situation:**
A mid-size distributor had a pricing coordinator who spent 18 hours per week manually updating prices across their product catalog. Every time a supplier changed pricing, the coordinator had to update hundreds of SKUs across multiple spreadsheets and their ordering system.

**What we built:**
- Automated supplier price feed ingestion
- Automatic catalog updates across all systems when pricing changes
- Exception alerts when price changes exceed normal thresholds (flagged for human review)
- Weekly pricing summary report delivered to management every Monday at 8 AM

**The results:**
- 18 hours/week of manual work reduced to 2 hours/week of oversight
- Price update errors dropped from 5-8% to under 0.5%
- The pricing coordinator now focuses on vendor negotiations and margin analysis
- **ROI: The automation paid for itself in 3 weeks**
