## PERSONALIZATION VARIABLES

- `{{OWNER_NAME}}` - Business owner first name (use "there" if unknown)
- `{{TRADE}}` - Their trade: HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical
- `{{COMPANY}}` - Company name

## USAGE NOTES

1. Always personalize the trade/industry
2. Subject line A is the strongest opener - use it as default
3. Keep the CTA simple: call the demo number or reply
4. The P.S. is the second-most-read part of any email - always include it
5. Send between Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9am local time for best open rates
