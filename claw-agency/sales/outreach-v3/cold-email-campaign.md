## Email 1: The Problem (Day 1)

**Timing**: Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9:00-10:00 AM local time (best open rates)

### Subject Line Options (A/B Test These):
1. "Quick question about [specific process] at [Company Name]"
2. "Spending hours on [manual task]? There's a better way"
3. "[Name], automation question for [Company Name]"
4. "How [similar company] saved 10 hours/week (without hiring)"

**Subject Line Rules**:
- Personalize with company name or recipient name
- Keep under 50 characters
- Avoid spam words (free, guarantee, limited time)
- Make it specific, not generic

## Email Best Practices

### Technical Setup:
- **Sender Domain**: Use custom domain (theclawops.com) not Gmail/iCloud if possible (better deliverability)
- **SPF/DKIM**: Ensure email authentication is set up
- **Warm-up Period**: Start with 10-15 emails/day, increase gradually
- **Sending Times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-10 AM or 1-2 PM local time
- **Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends

### Writing Rules:
- **Length**: 100-150 words max (shorter = higher response rate)
- **Paragraphs**: 1-3 sentences max per paragraph
- **Personalization**: Minimum 2 personalized elements per email
- **CTA**: One clear call-to-action only
- **Links**: Maximum 1-2 links (too many = spam filters)
- **Images**: None (plain text performs better)

### Subject Line Best Practices:
- **Length**: 6-10 words, under 50 characters
- **Personalization**: Include name or company name
- **Avoid**: ALL CAPS, excessive punctuation (!!!), spam words
- **Test**: A/B test 2-3 subject lines per campaign
- **Re: prefix**: Use in follow-ups to increase open rates

### Personalization Tokens:
- [First Name]
- [Company Name]
- [Industry]
- [Specific Tool/Process]
- [Similar Company]
- [City/Region]
- [Pain Point Signal]

## Legal & Compliance

### CAN-SPAM Compliance:
- ✓ Include physical address or P.O. Box in signature
- ✓ Clear sender identification (real name, not fake)
- ✓ Accurate subject lines (no deceptive subjects)
- ✓ Honor unsubscribe requests within 10 days
- ✓ Include unsubscribe link (or "reply STOP to unsubscribe")

### GDPR (if emailing EU prospects):
- Document legitimate interest basis for outreach
- Include clear unsubscribe mechanism
- Store prospect data securely
- Delete data upon request

### Unsubscribe Language:
