## Next Steps for Each Prospect

1. **Research deeper**: Find more specific pain points through their website, social media, reviews
2. **Personalize approach**: Reference specific post/signal, use their language
3. **Lead with value**: Time savings, cost savings, or revenue increase (whichever resonates most)
4. **Low-friction CTA**: 15-20 minute call, not "buy this project"
5. **Follow-up sequence**: 3-email sequence if no response (day 1, day 4, day 8)

## Prospecting Strategy Summary

**Where to Find More Prospects**:
1. LinkedIn posts/comments mentioning manual processes
2. Job postings for "data entry" or "administrative assistant"
3. Reddit r/smallbusiness, industry-specific subreddits
4. Twitter/X searches for "[industry] automation" or "manual process"
5. Company blogs discussing operational challenges
6. Review sites where businesses respond to operational complaints

**Qualifying Criteria**:
- 5-50 employees (sweet spot for automation ROI)
- Obvious manual process that's time-consuming
- Growing company (scaling pain = need for automation)
- Budget likely available ($2K-10K project size)
- Decision maker accessible (not massive enterprise)

**Success Metrics**:
- Target: 20% response rate to cold outreach
- Goal: 2-3 discovery calls per 10 prospects contacted
- Conversion: 30-40% of discovery calls to paid projects
- Average project: $3,500

10 prospects → 2 responses → 1 project = $3,500 revenue from 10 outreaches
