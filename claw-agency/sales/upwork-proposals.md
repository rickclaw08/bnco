## USAGE NOTES

1. **Always customize the bracketed sections** for each specific job posting. Generic proposals lose.
2. **Reference the specific job title and tools mentioned** in the posting.
3. **Pick the template closest to their need**, then adjust. Many projects overlap (document processing + data entry, CRM + workflow integration). Combine elements when needed.
4. **Keep the ROI framing.** Every template leads with the cost of the problem before presenting the solution. This is the trillion-dollar CRO approach: pain before pitch.
5. **The CTA is always a call.** Don't try to close on Upwork messages. Get them on a 10-minute call where you can do discovery and present a tailored solution.
6. **Follow up within 48 hours** if you don't hear back. A short message: "Hey [Name], just checking if you had a chance to review my proposal. Happy to answer any questions."
