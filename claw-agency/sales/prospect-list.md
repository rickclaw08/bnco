## Prospecting Scripts

### For Google Maps research:
1. Search "[trade] [city]"
2. Click on businesses with 50-300 reviews (sweet spot)
3. Check their website - do they have a chat widget? If no, they're a prospect.
4. Note their phone number - we'll mystery-call them later
5. Find owner name via: website About page, BBB, LinkedIn, or Google "[Company Name] owner"
6. Find email via: website contact page, Hunter.io, or the pattern firstname@company.com

### For Angi/HomeAdvisor:
1. Search by trade and market
2. Filter by "Top Rated" or "Elite" (these are established, revenue-rich businesses)
3. Cross-reference with Google Maps for review count

## Notes

- **Don't source more than 100 prospects at once.** Source 30-40, outreach them, learn what works, then source more.
- **Quality over quantity.** A personalized email to 30 perfect prospects beats a generic blast to 300.
- **The mystery call is our secret weapon.** Every prospect we can call before emailing gets 3x the response rate because Email 3 ("I called you yesterday") is devastating.
- **Track which trades respond best.** If plumbers respond at 25% and roofers at 5%, pivot hard toward plumbers.
