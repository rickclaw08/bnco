- Identify data quality issues, duplicates, and gaps

**Phase 2 - Pipeline Build & Integration (1-3 weeks)**
- Build automated extraction from all your source systems (APIs, databases, SaaS tools, spreadsheets)
- Implement data transformation, cleaning, and validation rules
- Set up the destination (data warehouse, BI tool, CRM, or wherever your clean data needs to live)
- Build error handling, retry logic, and alerting so you know immediately if something breaks

**Phase 3 - Reporting & Handoff (3-5 days)**
- Create automated dashboards and reports that update in real time
- Document the entire pipeline with runbooks for your team
- Provide 2 weeks of monitoring and support post-launch
