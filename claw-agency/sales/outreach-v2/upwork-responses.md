## UPWORK STRATEGY NOTES:

**Winning Elements:**
- Lead with specific outcomes (hours saved, error reduction %)
- Reference similar projects with real numbers
- Offer discovery call (builds trust, qualifies leads)
- Fixed-price > hourly (less scary for clients)
- Show you understand THEIR problem specifically

**Red Flags to Avoid:**
- Generic "I'm experienced" claims
- Hourly rate focus (makes clients worry about runaway costs)
- Over-promising ("I can automate everything!")
- No specific examples or results

**Follow-up:**
- If they reply but don't book a call, send ONE follow-up in 2-3 days
- If they go dark, move on (Upwork has plenty of leads)
- Track all responses in the outreach tracker
