## SEQUENCE STRATEGY:

**Connection Request:**
- Reference something SPECIFIC (their post, company news, mutual connection)
- Keep it casual and low-pressure
- No direct pitch

**Message 1 (Day 1):**
- Lead with value
- Mention specific pain point + outcome
- Soft CTA (offer Loom, not a "meeting")

**Message 2 (Day 7):**
- Share concrete case study with numbers
- Acknowledge they might have missed previous message
- Stronger CTA but still consultative

**Message 3 (Day 14):**
- Breakup email style
- Give them an easy out
- Ask a question to re-engage
- Leave door open

**CONVERSION TIPS:**
- If they reply at ANY stage, immediately pivot to discovery questions
- Don't send a calendar link immediately (ask 1-2 qualifying questions first)
- If they engage but don't want a call, offer async Loom demo
- Track ALL responses in outreach tracker CSV
