# ClawOps Outreach V2 - Direct Channels Package

**Created:** 2026-02-20
**Strategy:** Pivot from karma-gated Reddit/social to immediate-access channels (email, LinkedIn, Upwork)

## What's In This Package

### 1. Cold Email Templates (`cold-email-templates.md`)
10 industry-specific email templates designed for high reply rates:
- E-commerce (inventory/order management)
- Real Estate (lead routing)
- Professional Services (client onboarding)
- Healthcare/Dental (appointment management)
- Marketing Agencies (reporting automation)
- SaaS (lead qualification)
- Manufacturing (quote management)
- Financial Services (compliance/data entry)
- Hospitality (booking/guest communication)
- Construction (estimate follow-up)

**Key Features:**
- Under 100 words each
- Value-first, no hard sell
- Specific ROI numbers from case studies
- Personalization hooks
- No em dashes (per style guide)

### 2. LinkedIn Sequence (`linkedin-sequence.md`)
3-message sequence over 2 weeks:
- Connection request (personalized, low-pressure)
- Message 1 (Day 1): Value drop with soft CTA
- Message 2 (Day 7): Case study with numbers
- Message 3 (Day 14): Breakup/re-engagement

**Conversion Strategy:**
- Reference specific posts/activity
- Offer Loom demos before calls
- Give easy outs to maintain goodwill
- Track all responses

### 3. Upwork Responses (`upwork-responses.md`)
5 templates for common automation job posts:
- General business automation
- CRM/data entry automation
- E-commerce/Shopify automation
- Marketing/agency reporting
- Make/Zapier expert jobs

**Winning Elements:**
- Lead with outcomes (hours saved, error reduction)
- Fixed-price positioning
- Discovery call offers
- Real project examples with numbers

### 4. Lead Magnet (`lead-magnet-outline.md`)
**Title:** "5 Automations Every Small Business Should Set Up This Week"

**Content:**
- 10-12 page PDF guide
- 5 practical automations with step-by-step setup
- Time-to-setup and ROI for each
- Tools needed (mostly free/low-cost)
- Designed for DIY implementation

**Lead Capture:**
- Email gate on website
- Auto-delivery + follow-up sequence
- Positions ClawOps as automation experts
- Natural bridge to paid services

### 5. Outreach Tracker (`outreach-tracker.csv`)
Simple CSV template for tracking all outreach:
- Name, Company, Industry
- Channel (email/LinkedIn/Upwork)
- Date Sent, Follow-up Date
- Status, Notes, Next Action

**Usage:** Log every outreach attempt to avoid double-messaging and track conversion rates by channel.

### Risks & Mitigations:

**Risk:** Email deliverability (cold email can hurt domain reputation)
**Mitigation:** 
- Warm up agentclaw08@icloud.com domain slowly (start with 10/day, scale up)
- Use email verification tool (NeverBounce, ZeroBounce) before sending
- Keep sending volume under 50/day from single domain
- Consider secondary domain for cold outreach (clawops.io or similar)

**Risk:** LinkedIn account restrictions (too aggressive = ban)
**Mitigation:**
- Stay under 100 connection requests/week
- Personalize every request
- Don't use automation tools (do it manually or use LinkedIn Sales Nav approved features)
- If warned, pause for 2 weeks

**Risk:** Upwork saturation (lots of automation freelancers)
**Mitigation:**
- Lead with business outcomes, not tools
- Respond within 1 hour of job posting (speed matters)
- Include video intro (90% of proposals don't, instant differentiation)
- Build 5-star rating quickly with smaller projects first
