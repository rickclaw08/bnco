| Stage | Definition | Required Action |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Prospected** | Identified, contact info gathered | Send initial outreach within 24 hours |
| **Contacted** | Initial outreach sent | Follow up Day 2 if no reply |
| **Replied** | Prospect responded | Book AI Readiness Audit call within 24 hours |

| **Closed Lost** | Declined or went dark (7+ days after final follow-up) | Log reason, move to nurture list |

## Geographic Focus (Start Here)

**Warm markets (higher average job values, year-round demand):**
1. **Texas** (Austin, Houston, Dallas, San Antonio) - HVAC is essential, massive construction
2. **Florida** (Tampa, Orlando, Miami, Jacksonville) - Year-round AC demand
3. **Arizona** (Phoenix, Tucson, Scottsdale) - HVAC goldmine
4. **Georgia** (Atlanta metro) - Fast-growing, lots of new construction

**Why these markets:**
- Hot climate = HVAC is mission-critical (not seasonal)
- High population growth = constant demand for contractors
- Higher average home values = higher job values = more money to spend on systems
