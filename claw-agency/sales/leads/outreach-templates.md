## General Tips
- **Personalize the brackets** — company name, person's name, specific detail from their website/LinkedIn
- **Follow up** — Day 3 (bump), Day 7 (new angle/value), Day 14 (breakup email)
- **Subject lines** — keep them curiosity-driven or pain-point specific, never salesy
- **CTA** — always low-commitment ("15 minutes", "quick look", "no pitch")
