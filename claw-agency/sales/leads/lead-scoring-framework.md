# Lead Scoring Framework
*Generated: 2026-02-19*

Score leads 0–100. Prioritize 70+. Nurture 40–69. Deprioritize <40.

---

## Scoring Categories

### 1. Pain Intensity (0–25 pts)
How much does their current manual process hurt?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 25 | Actively complaining about it (social posts, reviews, job postings for admin roles) |
| 20 | Clear bottleneck visible (slow response times, bad reviews mentioning service) |
| 15 | Industry is known for manual processes but no direct signal |
| 10 | Some automation already in place, marginal gains available |
| 5 | Low pain — already well-optimized |

### 2. Budget Fit (0–20 pts)
Can they afford it?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 20 | Revenue $1M+, or funded startup, or professional services with high margins |
| 15 | Revenue $500K–$1M, healthy business, likely can budget $1K–$3K/mo |
| 10 | Revenue $100K–$500K, would need clear ROI proof |
| 5 | Pre-revenue, bootstrapped, or very tight margins |

### 3. Decision Speed (0–15 pts)
How fast can they say yes?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 15 | Owner-operator, single decision maker, small team |
| 10 | 2–3 stakeholders, but accessible |
| 5 | Committee decisions, procurement process, slow org |

### 4. Technical Readiness (0–15 pts)
Do they use tools we can plug into?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 15 | Already on modern stack (Slack, Shopify, HubSpot, Zapier, etc.) |
| 10 | Some digital tools, willing to adopt |
| 5 | Paper-based, resistant to change, legacy systems |

### 5. Engagement Signal (0–15 pts)
Have they shown interest?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 15 | Inbound inquiry, replied to outreach, booked a call |
| 10 | Opened emails, clicked links, engaged on social |
| 5 | No engagement yet but fits profile |
| 0 | Cold, no signal |

### 6. Strategic Value (0–10 pts)
Beyond this deal, what's the upside?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 10 | Great case study potential, referral network, recurring revenue likely |
| 5 | Solid client but limited expansion |
| 0 | One-off project, low visibility |

---

## Score Ranges

| Range | Action |
|-------|--------|
| **80–100** | 🔥 Hot — reach out TODAY, personalize heavily, offer a free audit |
| **70–79** | ✅ High priority — reach out this week, strong fit |
| **50–69** | 📧 Nurture — add to email sequence, follow on socials, check back monthly |
| **30–49** | 👀 Watch — keep on list, revisit quarterly |
| **0–29** | ❌ Deprioritize — not worth active effort right now |

## Process
1. Find leads matching profiles in `lead-list.md`
2. Score each using this framework
3. Log scores in a spreadsheet or CRM
4. Route 70+ to immediate outreach using `outreach-templates.md`
5. Route 50–69 to nurture sequence
6. Re-score monthly as signals change
