# Instagram DM Funnel: "DEMO" Trigger Word Sequence

## How It Works
1. @theclawops posts content about missed calls costing contractors money
2. CTA: "DM me DEMO to hear it yourself"
3. Lead DMs "DEMO"
4. Sequence below fires off

---

## DM #1: Immediate Response (within 2 minutes of "DEMO")

**Trigger:** Lead DMs "DEMO"

```
Hey [name]! Glad you want to hear it.

Here is the number: (888) 457-8980

Call it right now. It is a live AI receptionist that answers calls for contractors. Talk to it like you are a customer calling for a quote.

Let me know what you think after you call. I will be here.
```

**Why it works:** No friction. No qualifying. Just get them on the phone immediately. The product sells itself when they hear it.

---

## DM #2: Follow-Up After They Called (send 15-30 min after DM #1 if they respond positively)

**Trigger:** Lead responds with anything positive ("that was crazy," "wow," etc.)

```
Right? Most people call it and immediately think about all the calls they have been missing.

Quick question: what type of contracting do you do? And roughly how many calls does your business get per month?

Just want to make sure this is a good fit before we go further.
```

**Why it works:** They are excited. Now we qualify while the energy is high. Asking about call volume tells us deal size and urgency.

---

## DM #3: Follow-Up If They Did Not Call (send 4-6 hours after DM #1 with no response)

**Trigger:** No response after DM #1

```
Hey [name], just checking in. That number is still live if you want to try it: (888) 457-8980

Takes 30 seconds. Call it like you are a homeowner needing [HVAC service / a plumber / an electrician]. You will see exactly how it handles your calls.

Most guys who try it say they wish they had it months ago.
```

**Why it works:** Gentle nudge with a specific use case. Making it tangible removes the "I will do it later" excuse.

---

## DM #4: Booking Push (send 24 hours after DM #1 if still no booking)

**Trigger:** They showed interest but have not committed

```
Hey [name], I know you are busy running jobs. Here is the quick version:

Our AI answers your phone 24/7, qualifies the caller, and books them on your calendar. No hold music. No missed leads. No hiring a receptionist.

We have a few spots open for contractors this month at our founding rate ($1,997 one-time, no monthly fees).

If you want to learn more, fill this out and I will personally walk you through it: theclawops.com/book/

No pressure. Just want to make sure you see what this can do before the founding spots fill up.
```

**Why it works:** Scarcity (founding spots), concrete value prop, and a clear link to the booking page. The one-time price is easier to stomach in a DM than the monthly option.

---

## DM #5: Final Value-Add (send 3-5 days after DM #1 if no conversion)

**Trigger:** Lead went cold

```
Hey [name], last thing from me on this.

I did some quick math: if your business misses just 2 calls a week and each job is worth $500+, that is $4,000/month walking out the door. Over a year, that is almost $50K in lost revenue.

Our AI catches every single one of those calls.

The demo line is still live if you change your mind: (888) 457-8980

Either way, good luck with the business. Hope the season treats you well.
```

**Why it works:** Math makes it real. Ends on a positive note without being pushy. This is the "plant the seed" message. Some will come back weeks later.

---

## General DM Rules

1. **Never use em dashes.** Use commas, periods, or rewrite.
2. **Keep messages under 500 characters when possible.** Mobile DMs get cut off.
3. **Use their name when you have it.** Personalization matters.
4. **Do not pitch price until DM #4.** Let the product sell first.
5. **Always lead with the phone number.** The demo is our best closer.
6. **Match their energy.** If they are casual, be casual. If they are professional, match that.
7. **If they ask about price early:** "Depends on the setup, but our founding rate is $1,997 one-time. Most guys make that back in the first two weeks from calls they would have missed."
8. **If they say "too expensive":** "I get it. Let me ask this: how much is a missed call worth to you? If it is $500+ per job, the AI pays for itself after 4 caught calls."
