# Lead Qualification Criteria

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Green Light: Close This Deal

- **Business Type:** HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Roofing, General Contractor, or any home/commercial service business that relies on phone calls for leads
- **Monthly Call Volume:** 50+ calls per month (more calls = more value from the AI)
- **Current Setup:** No receptionist, using voicemail, missing calls on job sites, or paying for an answering service they hate
- **Revenue Per Job:** $500+ average ticket (makes the ROI math obvious)
- **Pain Level:** High. They know they are losing money. They have felt the sting of checking voicemail and finding 3 missed calls from the morning.
- **Decision Maker:** Owner or partner who can say yes without a committee
- **Budget:** Can handle $1,997 one-time or $2,500 + $497/mo without it being a stretch

### Qualification Questions (DM or Form)

1. "What type of contracting do you do?" (validates business type)
2. "How many calls does your business get per month?" (validates volume)
3. "What happens right now when you miss a call?" (validates pain and urgency)
4. "Do you currently have a receptionist or answering service?" (validates current solution gap)

### Scoring Model

| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| 100+ calls/month | +3 |
| 50-100 calls/month | +2 |
| Under 50 calls/month | +1 |
| No receptionist / using voicemail | +3 |
| Has receptionist but missing after-hours | +2 |
| Has answering service (unhappy with it) | +2 |
| Average job value $1,000+ | +3 |
| Average job value $500-$1,000 | +2 |
| Called the demo line | +3 |
| Owner/decision maker confirmed | +2 |
| Responded within 1 hour of DM #1 | +1 |
| Mentioned specific missed-call pain | +2 |

**Score Interpretation:**
- **12+ points:** Hot lead. Priority follow-up. Push for close.
- **8-11 points:** Warm lead. Nurture with DM sequence. Will likely convert.
- **5-7 points:** Cool lead. Stay in touch but do not force it. Add to email list.
- **Under 5 points:** Not a fit right now. Politely pass.

## Red Flags: Pass on These Leads

- **Startup with no revenue yet:** They cannot afford it and will churn. "Sounds great, let me get back to you when we launch" is a no.
- **Fewer than 20 calls per month:** Not enough volume for the AI to prove its value. The ROI story falls apart.
- **Just wants a chatbot / not interested in voice:** Wrong product. Redirect to main site or pass.
- **Multiple decision makers / "let me talk to my partner":** These stall out. If the person you are talking to cannot decide, offer to do a quick group call but do not wait around.
- **Focused only on price, not value:** If every question is "how much" and nothing about what it does, they are shopping on price and will churn at the first bill.
- **Already has a full-time receptionist they are happy with:** No pain = no sale. Unless they need after-hours coverage, this is not a fit right now.

## Objection Handling Quick Reference

### "How much does it cost?"
"Our founding rate is $1,997 one-time with no monthly fees. Standard pricing after that is $2,500 setup plus $497/month. Most contractors make the founding price back in the first week or two from calls they would have missed."

### "I need to think about it."
"Totally fair. While you think about it, how many calls do you think you missed this week? Each one of those is money someone else got. The founding spots are limited, so I would not wait too long."

### "I already have a receptionist."
"That is great. Who answers your phone at 7pm? On weekends? When she is on break or calls in sick? Most of our clients have receptionists too. The AI handles everything they cannot."

### "Sounds too good to be true."
"Call it right now: (888) 457-8980. Talk to it yourself. If it is not real, I owe you lunch."

### "I do not get that many calls."
"Even if you get 30 calls a month and miss 10% of them, that is 3 lost jobs. At $500 per job, that is $1,500/month in missed revenue. Over a year, $18,000. The AI costs less than one of those jobs."

### "Can I try it for free?"
"The demo line is free and live right now: (888) 457-8980. Call it and see exactly how it works. Setup is what costs money because we customize the AI for your business, your services, and your scheduling."

## Disqualification Script (Polite Pass)

For leads that do not fit:

```
Hey [name], appreciate your interest. Based on what you told me, I do not think our AI receptionist is the right fit for you right now. It works best for businesses that get 50+ calls per month and have a high per-job value.

Here is what I would suggest instead: [relevant alternative or general advice].

If things change down the road, you know where to find us. Good luck with the business.
```

Keep it professional. A polite pass today can become a referral tomorrow.
