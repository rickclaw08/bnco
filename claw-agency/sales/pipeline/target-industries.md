# Target Industries for ClawOps AI Automation

## 1. Real Estate Agencies
**Pain Points:**
- Manual lead qualification wastes 60%+ of agent time
- Inconsistent follow-up leads to lost deals
- Property matching is time-consuming and inefficient
- Client communication requires constant availability

**Automation Opportunity:** Lead scoring, automated follow-ups, property matching AI, chatbot for 24/7 inquiries

---

## 2. Legal Practices (Small to Mid-Size Firms)
**Pain Points:**
- Document review and analysis is labor-intensive
- Client intake processes are repetitive and slow
- Billing and time tracking is manual and error-prone
- Case research takes hours of attorney time

**Automation Opportunity:** Document analysis AI, intake chatbots, automated billing, legal research assistants

---

## Selection Criteria for These Industries

**Why These Work Best:**
1. **High repetitive task volume** - Clear ROI from automation
2. **Growing but understaffed** - Need leverage without hiring
3. **Price-appropriate** - Can afford $500-$5K/month and see value
4. **Immediate pain** - Problems they feel daily, not theoretical
5. **Low technical sophistication** - Not already automated, appreciate the help
