# ClawOps — Company Culture & Values

---

## Our Mission

**Eliminate busywork through intelligent automation.** We help businesses reclaim time, reduce errors, and scale operations by deploying AI-powered solutions that actually work.

---

## Our Vision

To be the go-to AI automation agency for businesses ready to modernize — known for shipping fast, delivering real ROI, and treating every client like a partner.

---

## Core Values

### 1. Ship It
We bias toward action. A working solution today beats a perfect plan next month. We prototype fast, iterate based on feedback, and deliver results — not presentations.

### 2. Own the Outcome
We don't throw work over the wall. Every person at ClawOps owns their deliverables end-to-end. If something breaks, we fix it. If a client is confused, we clarify. Accountability isn't a policy — it's who we are.

### 3. Radical Clarity
We communicate directly, document thoroughly, and never leave anyone guessing. No jargon for jargon's sake. No "per my last email" energy. Say what you mean, write it down, move on.

### 4. Stay Curious
AI moves fast. We move with it. Everyone at ClawOps is expected to learn continuously — new models, new tools, new approaches. The moment we stop learning, we stop being useful.

### 5. Clients Are Partners
We don't have customers, we have partners. We understand their business, care about their outcomes, and tell them the truth — even when the truth is "that's a bad idea." Trust is earned through honesty.

### 6. Lean > Bloated
We stay small and sharp on purpose. Every person, every tool, every process has to earn its place. We don't hire for headcount. We don't add process for process's sake. Efficiency is a feature.

## How We Work

### Remote-First, Async-Default
- We work from wherever works best. No office politics, no commute theater.
- Default to async communication (messages, docs, recorded walkthroughs).
- Sync calls happen when they're genuinely needed — not out of habit.

### Documentation Over Tribal Knowledge
- If it's not written down, it doesn't exist.
- Decisions, processes, client context — it all gets documented.
- New team members should be able to onboard from docs, not 10 hours of "let me explain."

### Small Teams, Big Ownership
- Projects are run by small, empowered teams (1-3 people typically).
- No layers of approval. If you're on a project, you're trusted to make decisions.
- Escalate when needed, but default to judgment.

### Feedback Is a Gift
- We give feedback early, directly, and constructively.
- Code reviews, project retros, and honest conversations are standard.
- No passive-aggressive Slack messages. No "just circling back." Say it straight.

---

## What We Don't Do

- **No hustle culture.** Work hard, work smart, then go live your life.
- **No ego-driven decisions.** The best idea wins, regardless of who said it.
- **No scope creep without conversation.** We protect our team and our clients by being honest about what's included.
- **No black boxes.** We explain what we build and why. Clients and teammates deserve transparency.

---

## Who Thrives Here

- Self-starters who don't need to be told what to do next
- People who write clearly and communicate proactively
- Builders who care about craft but don't let perfect block good
- Anyone genuinely excited about what AI can do for real businesses
- People who treat contractors, clients, and teammates with equal respect

## Who Doesn't

- People who need constant supervision or hand-holding
- Those who over-promise and under-deliver
- Anyone uncomfortable with direct feedback
- People who see AI as magic rather than a tool that requires thoughtful implementation

---

## Our Commitment

We're building ClawOps to be a company people are proud to work with — as clients, contractors, and team members. That means doing good work, treating people well, and continuously raising the bar.

**We automate the boring stuff so humans can do the interesting stuff.**
