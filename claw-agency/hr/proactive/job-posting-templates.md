## 1. AI Engineer

### About ClawOps
ClawOps builds AI-powered automation solutions that help businesses eliminate repetitive work, streamline operations, and scale intelligently. We're a lean, fast-moving agency that ships real solutions — not slide decks.

### The Role
We're looking for an **AI Engineer** to design, build, and deploy AI automation systems for our clients. You'll work across LLMs, APIs, data pipelines, and workflow tools to deliver production-grade solutions.

### What You'll Do
- Architect and implement AI-powered automation workflows for diverse client needs
- Integrate LLMs (OpenAI, Claude, open-source models) into business processes
- Build RAG systems, AI agents, and intelligent data pipelines
- Develop and optimize prompts for reliability and performance
- Deploy and monitor solutions in production environments
- Collaborate directly with clients to translate business problems into technical solutions
- Document everything — we ship solutions, not mysteries

### What You Bring
- **3+ years** software engineering experience (Python required, JS/TS a plus)
- **1+ year** hands-on experience with LLM APIs and AI application development
- Strong API integration skills (REST, webhooks, OAuth)
- Experience with at least one workflow automation platform (n8n, Make, Zapier)
- Comfortable with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Solid understanding of databases (SQL and/or NoSQL)
- Excellent written communication — we're async-first
- Self-directed with low need for hand-holding

### Bonus Points
- Experience with fine-tuning or training models
- Familiarity with vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, Chroma)
- Open source contributions in AI/ML space
- Prior agency or consulting experience
- Understanding of AI safety and responsible deployment
