**Pre-existing IP:** Contractor retains ownership of pre-existing tools/libraries but grants Company a perpetual, royalty-free license to use them within the delivered work.

## 5. Confidentiality & Non-Disclosure

Contractor agrees to hold in strict confidence all proprietary information including but not limited to:
- Client lists, business strategies, pricing models
- Technical architectures, AI models, automation workflows
- Internal processes, tools, and documentation

This obligation survives termination for **2 years**.

## 6. Non-Solicitation

During the term and for **12 months** after termination, Contractor will not directly solicit Company's clients for competing services.

---

## 7. Termination

Either party may terminate with **14 days written notice**. Company may terminate immediately for cause (breach, misconduct, failure to deliver). Upon termination:
- Contractor delivers all completed and in-progress work
- Company pays for work completed through termination date
