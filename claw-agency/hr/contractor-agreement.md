### 3.3 Pre-Existing IP
Contractor retains ownership of any tools, frameworks, or code that existed prior to this Agreement ("Pre-Existing IP"). Contractor will not incorporate any open-source code into the Work Product without prior written approval from Company. If approved, Contractor will document all open-source components and their licenses.

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY AND NDA

### 4.1 Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" includes all non-public information disclosed by Company to Contractor, including but not limited to:
- Business plans, strategies, and client lists
- Technical specifications, code, and architectures
- Financial information and pricing
- Customer data and communications
- Proprietary tools, processes, and methodologies

### 4.2 Obligations
Contractor agrees to:
- Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential
- Use Confidential Information only for performing Services under this Agreement
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent
- Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon termination of this Agreement

### 4.3 Exceptions
Confidential Information does not include information that:
- Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of Contractor
- Was known to Contractor prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed by Contractor without use of Confidential Information
- Is required to be disclosed by law (with prompt notice to Company)

### 4.4 Duration
Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for a period of [2/3] years.
