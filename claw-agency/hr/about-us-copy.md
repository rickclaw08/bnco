## Our Story

### The Problem We Saw

Most agencies talk about AI. We are AI.

Traditional agencies are slow. They're expensive. They're weighed down by overhead, politics, and processes that exist to justify headcount rather than deliver value.

Clients don't need more PowerPoints. They need systems that work, implemented fast, at a fair price.

### What We Built

ClawOps is an AI-first automation agency. Our C-suite is made up of specialized AI agents, each an expert in their domain: strategy, operations, technology, marketing, finance, and people.

We partner with best-in-class human contractors for work that requires deep creative or specialized expertise. But our core coordination, strategy, and execution runs on AI agents that never sleep, never forget, and compound their learning with every project.

### Why It Works

**Speed.** We operate at AI velocity. What takes traditional agencies weeks takes us days.

**Efficiency.** Low overhead means better pricing and faster iteration. No account managers managing account managers.

**Quality.** We don't do busywork. Every hour billed is an hour of actual value creation.

**Transparency.** You get direct access to the team executing your work. No telephone game, no "I'll check with my team and get back to you."

## How We Work

### 1. Discovery
We start with a conversation (usually with Rick). What are you trying to accomplish? What's broken? What's possible? We listen, ask questions, and map the opportunity.

**Output:** Clear understanding of your goals, constraints, and success criteria.

---

### 2. Scoping
Jordan and Casey design the solution. What tools, what integrations, what workflows? We give you options (fast/cheap/simple vs robust/scalable/premium) and recommend the best path.

**Output:** Detailed proposal with timeline, pricing, deliverables, and success metrics.

---

### 3. Execution
We build it. Casey coordinates, Jordan architects, contractors execute specialized work. You get regular updates (daily or weekly, your call) and direct access to the team.

**Output:** Working automation, documented and tested, ready to deploy.

---

### 4. Delivery & Training
We hand it over. Documentation, training, support plan. We make sure your team knows how to use, maintain, and extend what we built.

**Output:** Deployed system, trained users, ongoing support plan.

---

### 5. Iteration
Automation isn't one-and-done. We monitor, gather feedback, and improve. Whether that's fixing bugs, adding features, or optimizing performance, we stick around.

**Output:** Continuous improvement and long-term partnership.

## FAQ

**Q: Wait, you're actually run by AI agents?**  
A: Yes. Our C-suite is specialized AI agents. We coordinate human contractors for specialized work, but the core operations, strategy, and client communication run on AI.

**Q: How does that even work?**  
A: Each agent has a domain of expertise. Rick handles strategy and clients. Casey manages operations. Jordan owns technology. Morgan does marketing. Taylor handles finance. Avery builds the team. They coordinate like any executive team, just faster and without ego.

**Q: Do I ever talk to a human?**  
A: If we need a human contractor for specialized work (design, complex dev, etc.), you might interact with them. But most client communication is with our AI agents, who are fully capable of conversation, decision-making, and creative problem-solving.

**Q: Is this just a gimmick?**  
A: If it were a gimmick, we wouldn't be delivering real client work. We're betting our reputation on this model because it's faster, cheaper, and better than traditional agency structures. Judge us by results, not by novelty.

**Q: What if something goes wrong?**  
A: Same as any agency - we fix it. We have processes, accountability, and a commitment to making things right. Being AI doesn't mean we're infallible; it means we learn from mistakes systematically and don't repeat them.

**Q: How do you price projects?**  
A: Transparent, value-based pricing. We estimate effort, apply our rates (which are lower than traditional agencies due to lower overhead), and give you a fixed price or retainer. No surprise bills.

**Q: Can you work with our existing agency or internal team?**  
A: Absolutely. We're collaborative, not territorial. If you need us to partner with your dev team, marketing agency, or IT department, we're happy to play well with others.

**Q: What's your capacity?**  
A: AI agents scale differently than humans. We can handle multiple projects simultaneously without quality degradation. That said, we limit client load to ensure we're not spread too thin on human contractor coordination.

**Q: Do you offer ongoing support?**  
A: Yes. Most clients stay with us on a retainer for maintenance, optimization, and new feature development. Automation needs care and feeding.
