# Client Satisfaction Survey Template

## Purpose
This survey helps us understand how well we're serving clients, identify areas for improvement, and spot potential issues before they become problems. Use this at project completion and quarterly for ongoing retainer clients.

---

## Survey Metadata

**Survey timing:** Send within 48 hours of project delivery, or quarterly for retainer clients  
**Survey owner:** Casey (COO)  
**Distribution method:** Email with link to form (Typeform, Google Forms, or similar)  
**Target response time:** 5-7 minutes  
**Incentive:** Optional (small gift card or discount on next project)

## Post-Survey Actions

### Immediate (Within 24 Hours of Response)

**If NPS 9-10 (Promoter):**
- Casey sends personal thank you
- Request testimonial or case study (if Q19 = yes)
- Tag as reference account for future prospects
- Offer referral incentive

**If NPS 7-8 (Passive):**
- Casey or Rick follows up to understand "what would make it a 10?"
- Address any concerns raised
- Note for future improvement

**If NPS 0-6 (Detractor):**
- Rick reaches out immediately (same day if possible)
- Schedule call to understand issues
- Create remediation plan if relationship is salvageable
- Document lessons learned in `knowledge/mistakes/`

**If quality, communication, or budget rated "Poor" or below:**
- Rick and Casey review immediately
- Client call to discuss and address
- Internal retro with team to prevent recurrence

## Survey Metrics to Track

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

**Net Promoter Score (NPS):**  
Formula: % Promoters (9-10) minus % Detractors (0-6)  
Target: >50 (world-class), >30 (good), <0 (problem)

**Overall Satisfaction:**  
Formula: % "Very satisfied" + "Satisfied"  
Target: >90%

**Would Work Again:**  
Formula: % "Definitely" + "Probably"  
Target: >85%

**On-Time Delivery:**  
Formula: % "Yes" + "Mostly on time"  
Target: >90%

**On-Budget Delivery:**  
Formula: % "Yes" + "Minor overages"  
Target: >85%

**Quality Exceeds Expectations:**  
Formula: % "Exceeded expectations"  
Target: >40%

## Customization by Client Type

### Enterprise Clients
- Add questions about stakeholder alignment
