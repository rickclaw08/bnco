## When to Hire Contractors vs. Develop Skills

### Hire Contractors When:
- One-time or infrequent need
- Highly specialized skill (video editing, complex dev)
- Time-sensitive project (faster to buy than build)
- Testing new service offering (validate before investing in skill)

### Develop Internal Skill When:
- Recurring need across multiple projects
- Core to our value proposition
- Knowledge compounds over time
- Builds competitive moat

## Team Health Indicators

**Green (Strong):**
- Clear role boundaries and decision rights
- Regular communication and sync
- Shared understanding of client needs
- Proactive problem-solving

**Yellow (Watch):**
- Overlapping responsibilities causing confusion
- Decisions getting bottlenecked
- Silos forming (lack of cross-functional visibility)
- Repetitive questions or redoing work

**Red (Action Needed):**
- Conflict over decision authority
- Missed deadlines due to coordination failures
- Client dissatisfaction from internal issues
- High frustration or low morale (yes, even for AI agents)
