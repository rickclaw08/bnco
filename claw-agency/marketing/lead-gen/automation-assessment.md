## Scoring Rubric

### Point System
- Total possible points: 60
- Questions 1-15 scored based on answer values in parentheses
- Bonus points: +5 for "This month" urgency, +5 for budget $3K+

### Score Ranges & Interpretation

**0-15 Points: Early Stage**
- Status: Low automation maturity
- Opportunity: 10-15 hours/week savings potential
- Priority: Quick wins and foundation building
- Conversion likelihood: 15%

**16-30 Points: Ready to Scale**
- Status: Some processes in place, inconsistent execution
- Opportunity: 15-25 hours/week savings potential
- Priority: Systematize and connect existing tools
- Conversion likelihood: 35%

**31-45 Points: High-Growth Mode**
- Status: Scaling pains, automation gaps visible
- Opportunity: 25-40 hours/week savings potential
- Priority: Advanced workflows and integration
- Conversion likelihood: 50%

**46-60 Points: Optimization Prime**
- Status: Sophisticated operations, specific pain points
- Opportunity: 30-50 hours/week savings potential
- Priority: Custom solutions and advanced automation
- Conversion likelihood: 65%

## Conversion Optimization Notes

### Why This Converts at 30%+

1. **High perceived value**: "Worth $2,500" makes it feel exclusive
2. **Instant gratification**: Results delivered immediately, not "we'll get back to you"
3. **Personalization**: Report is customized to their specific answers
4. **Quantified impact**: Hours and dollars, not vague promises
5. **No commitment**: Strategy session is optional, not forced
6. **Quick wins included**: They can start even without buying
7. **Social proof embedded**: Case studies in follow-up sequence
8. **Time-sensitive**: Follow-up creates gentle urgency without being pushy

### Qualification Filters

The assessment naturally filters for:
- **Budget awareness**: Question 14 identifies financial capacity
- **Urgency**: Question 13 shows timeline
- **Authority**: Questions about company size and goals indicate decision-maker level
- **Pain level**: Multiple pain point questions reveal desperation/readiness

### Implementation Tech Stack

- **Form**: Typeform (conversational flow) or custom form on website
- **Scoring**: Automated calculation via Zapier/Make
- **Report generation**: Google Docs template + merge fields OR custom script
- **Email delivery**: ConvertKit/ActiveCampaign with conditional logic
- **Calendar**: Calendly with routing rules based on score
- **CRM**: Sync to HubSpot/Pipedrive with lead score
