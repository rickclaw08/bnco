## Template Usage Guide

### When to Create a Case Study

- After project completion + 30 days (let results stabilize)
- Client is happy and willing to participate
- Results are measurable and impressive
- Story is relatable to your target audience

### How to Gather Information

**Client Interview Questions:**

1. What was the biggest challenge before working with us?
2. What made you decide to move forward with automation?
3. What were you skeptical about?
4. What surprised you most during the project?
5. Can you quantify the impact? (time saved, costs reduced, revenue gained)
6. How has this changed day-to-day work for your team?
7. Would you recommend us? Why?
8. Anything you'd want other businesses considering this to know?

**Internal Data to Collect:**

- Time tracking: before/after comparisons
- Error rates: before/after
- Customer satisfaction scores: before/after
- Financial impact: costs, savings, revenue changes
- Project timeline and milestones
- Tools/technologies used

### Distribution Strategy

**Where to Use This Case Study:**

1. **Website:** Dedicated case studies page
2. **Sales Collateral:** PDF version to send prospects
3. **Social Media:** Break into multiple posts (tease results, share quotes, link to full story)
4. **Email Campaigns:** Send to prospects in similar industries
5. **Proposals:** Include relevant case studies in pitches
6. **LinkedIn Articles:** Republish as long-form content
7. **Sales Calls:** Reference during discovery/demo calls

**SEO Optimization:**

- Title includes target keywords (e.g., "logistics automation case study")
- Meta description with key results
- Alt text on any images/charts
- Internal links to service pages
- Schema markup (Article or Case Study schema)

## Design Tips for One-Pagers

**Visual Elements to Include:**

- **Before/After Comparison Chart:** Visual representation of time saved or errors reduced
- **ROI Graph:** Show investment vs. return over time
- **Quote Highlight Box:** Pull out the best client quote visually
- **Icons:** Use simple icons for each result metric (clock for time, dollar sign for savings, etc.)
- **Client Logo:** (with permission) Add credibility
- **Headshot:** Client testimonial with photo if possible

**Formatting for Readability:**

- Use bullet points and short paragraphs
- Bold key numbers (124 hours saved, 1,087% ROI)
- Color-code sections (challenge = red, solution = blue, results = green)
- Keep it scannable: busy prospects will skim first, read details later

**File Formats:**

- **PDF:** For email and download (one-page, print-friendly)
- **Web Page:** For SEO and easy sharing
- **Slide Deck:** For presentations and sales calls
