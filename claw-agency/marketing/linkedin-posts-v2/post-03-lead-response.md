By then, your prospect has already talked to three competitors, picked one, and forgotten your name.

So what happens when you automate lead response?

- Average response time: 6 hours

- Monthly revenue from inbound leads: ~$18K

After we built their automated lead capture and qualification system:
- Average response time: 47 seconds
- Booking rate: 34%

Same ad spend. Same team size. Same services. The only thing that changed was speed.

1. Lead comes in (form, call, text, email, doesn't matter)
2. AI qualifies them instantly with a personalized response
3. Qualified leads get booked directly onto the calendar
4. Unqualified leads enter a nurture sequence
5. The team only talks to people who are ready to buy
