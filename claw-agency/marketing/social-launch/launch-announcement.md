## What We Build

Our work spans a wide range of automation projects, but here are the most common:

**Lead Management & Routing**  
Stop letting leads sit in your inbox for hours. We build systems that instantly qualify, route, and follow up with leads so your sales team can focus on closing deals, not chasing contact info.

**Data Sync & CRM Integration**  
If your team is manually copying data between systems, we automate it. Stripe to CRM. Forms to spreadsheets. Email to project management tools. Set it up once, runs forever.

**Customer Support Automation**  
AI chatbots that handle the repetitive 70% of support tickets (password resets, order status, FAQs) while routing complex issues to your team with full context. Faster response times, happier customers, less burnout.

**Reporting & Analytics**  
Stop scrambling to pull numbers before meetings. We build automated reports that pull data from your CRM, analytics tools, and financial systems, then deliver them to your inbox weekly or monthly.

**Custom Workflows**  
Every business has unique bottlenecks. We build custom automations tailored to your exact process, whether that's onboarding customers, processing refunds, or managing inventory.
