## Production Notes (All Reels)

**Lighting:** Natural light or ring light. Clean, not overproduced. Authenticity > polish.

**Audio:** Clear voice audio is non-negotiable. Use a lav mic or phone in a quiet room. Background noise kills contractor content.

**Text overlays:** Use bold, clean font (Inter or similar). Green (#4ade80) for key numbers and CTAs. White for everything else. Dark background behind text if over video footage.

**Posting format:** Post as Reel (not Story). Add to "AI Receptionist" highlight after posting.

**Hashtag strategy:** Mix broad (#contractor, #smallbusiness) with niche (#AIreceptionist, #missedcalls). 8-12 hashtags per post. No banned or spammy tags.

**Music:** Use trending audio when it fits, but never let it overpower the voiceover. For demo call reels, no music during the actual call playback.
