## Key Principles

1. **One niche, one product, one CTA.** Every reel is about the AI receptionist for contractors. Every CTA is "DM DEMO." No split focus.

2. **The product demos itself.** Our biggest asset is (888) 457-8980. Every funnel step pushes people to call it. The AI receptionist is more convincing than any sales page.

3. **Math over hype.** Every reel ties back to a specific dollar amount. $5K/month in missed calls. $300-$500 per lost lead. Contractors think in numbers, not vibes.

4. **Founder-led content wins.** Brand on camera builds trust faster than polished graphics. Wyatt proved this. An 18-year-old on camera closing medical clinics. Brand can do the same for contractors.

5. **Consistency beats virality.** 3 reels/week, every week, for 90 days. The algorithm rewards consistency. One viral reel means nothing without a content engine behind it.
