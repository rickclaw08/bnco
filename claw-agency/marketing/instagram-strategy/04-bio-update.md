**Line 1: "AI receptionist for contractors."**
- Immediately tells visitors what we sell and who it's for

- Matches Wyatt's approach: he says "Scale Your Clinic," we say the contractor equivalent

**Line 2: "Answers every call. Books every job. 24/7."**
- Three benefits in one line
- Rhythmic, punchy, memorable
- "24/7" is the killer differentiator vs. human receptionists

**Line 3: "$5K/month in missed calls? Not anymore."**
- Specific dollar amount creates urgency
- Ties directly to Reel #1 content (the $5,000/month leak)
- Question format stops the scroll

**Line 4: 'DM "DEMO" to hear it live'**
- Clear, single CTA
- Matches the trigger word from every reel
- "hear it live" implies proof, not a pitch
