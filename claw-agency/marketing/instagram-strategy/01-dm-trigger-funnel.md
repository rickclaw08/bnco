# DM Trigger Word Funnel for @theclawops

**Created:** March 2, 2026
**Inspired by:** Wyatt Roderick's "Training" DM trigger funnel at @wyattroderick_

---

## Primary Trigger Word: **DEMO**

**Why "DEMO" over alternatives:**
- One syllable, zero friction to type
- Implies "see it in action," not "buy something"
- Directly ties to our biggest conversion tool: the live AI receptionist call at (888) 457-8980
- Contractors are action-oriented. "Demo" feels concrete, not salesy.

---

## The Full Funnel (5 Steps)

### Step 1: Reel Hook
Every reel ends with a verbal and text CTA:
> "DM me the word DEMO and I'll send you a free test call with our AI receptionist. Call it yourself, hear it live."

**Visual:** Text overlay in green (#4ade80) on dark background, "DM 'DEMO' for a free test call" with an arrow pointing toward the DM icon.

### Step 2: Auto-DM Response
When someone DMs "DEMO," send this automated reply (via ManyChat or Instagram's built-in automation):

```
Hey! Thanks for reaching out.

Here's your free demo of our AI receptionist:

Call (888) 457-8980 right now.

Sarah (our AI) will answer, qualify you like a real lead, and book an appointment on your calendar. Takes about 45 seconds.

After you call, let me know what you think. If you want one built for YOUR business, I'll walk you through it on a quick call.

Book your strategy call here: [Calendly/Booking Link]
```

### Step 3: Follow-Up DM (24 hours later, if no booking)
```
Hey, did you get a chance to call the demo line?

Most contractors I talk to are losing 5-10 calls a week because they're on a job site and can't answer. That's $2,000-$5,000 in lost revenue every month.

If you want to see how this works for YOUR specific trade, grab a time here: [Booking Link]

No pressure. Just a 15-minute call.
```

### Step 4: Strategy Call
- 15-minute Zoom or phone call
- Walk through their current call flow (who answers? voicemail? answering service?)
- Show them the math: missed calls x average job value = money left on the table
- Offer founding member pricing: $1,997 one-time (vs. $2,500 + $497/mo standard)
- Urgency: founding member spots are limited

### Step 5: Close or Nurture
- **If closed:** Onboard within 48 hours, get them live
- **If not ready:** Add to nurture sequence. Send 1 value DM per week (case study, tip, or result screenshot)

## Key Principle

Every single piece of content points to one action: DM "DEMO." No split CTAs. No "link in bio" competing with "DM me." The bio link is a backup for people who skip the DM. Primary path is always: **Watch reel -> DM DEMO -> Call the demo line -> Book a call -> Close.**
