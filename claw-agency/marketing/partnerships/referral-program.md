# Client Referral Program - "Share the Automation Love"

## Program Overview

**Goal**: Turn existing clients into active advocates who refer new business.

**Incentive**: $500 credit (toward future services) OR $500 cash per successful referral

**Target**: Generate 3-5 referrals per month from existing client base

**Why This Works**:
- Happy clients naturally recommend us (we just need to make it official)
- $500 is meaningful enough to motivate, small enough to maintain healthy margins
- Credit option creates stickiness (keeps clients engaged)
- Cash option appeals to clients who don't need ongoing services

## Incentive Structure

### Option 1: $500 Service Credit

**What it covers:**
- Applied to any future ClawOps service
- No expiration date (unlike typical referral programs)
- Stackable (refer 3 clients, get $1,500 in credits)
- Transferable (can gift to another business if you don't need it)

**Best for**: Clients on ongoing retainers or likely to need future work

---

### Option 2: $500 Cash

**How it's paid:**
- Check, PayPal, Venmo, or ACH (referrer's choice)
- Paid within 30 days of referred client's first payment to us
- Subject to 1099 reporting (if over $600/year)

**Best for**: One-time project clients or those who prefer immediate cash

---

### Bonus Tiers

**Tier 1: 1-2 referrals/year** - Standard $500/referral  
**Tier 2: 3-4 referrals/year** - $600/referral  
**Tier 3: 5+ referrals/year** - $750/referral + VIP status (priority support, early access to new services)

---

## Qualification Criteria

### What Counts as a "Successful Referral"?

The referred client must:
1. ✅ Be a NEW client (not already in our pipeline)
2. ✅ Be explicitly referred by you (tracked via unique link or intro)
3. ✅ Sign a contract worth at least $2,000 (project) or $500/month (retainer)
4. ✅ Complete their first payment to ClawOps

**Timeline**: Referral must convert within 90 days of initial introduction

---

### What Doesn't Count?

❌ Existing leads already in our CRM  
❌ Referrals that don't close within 90 days (though we'll revisit if they close later)  
❌ Friends/family referrals that get special discounts (below minimum threshold)  
❌ Referrals from agency partners (they're on a different commission structure)

### Accounting & Tax Considerations

**For credits:**
- Applied as account balance reduction (no 1099 required)
- Tracked as marketing expense (reduces revenue)

**For cash payments:**
- Tracked as referral commission expense
- 1099-NEC issued if total payments to one referrer exceed $600/year
- Paid monthly or quarterly (depending on volume)

**Tax note for referrers:**
- Credits: Generally not taxable (treated as discount)
- Cash: Taxable income (1099 issued)

## Objection Handling

### "I don't want to be pushy with my network"
**Response**: "You're not being pushy - you're being helpful. If someone is struggling with operations, you're doing them a favor by introducing them to a solution. Frame it as, 'Hey, this helped me - thought it might help you too.'"

### "I don't know anyone who needs this"
**Response**: "Fair enough. Just keep us in mind. Most referrals happen organically - you hear someone complain about manual work or operational chaos, and you think of us. No pressure to actively recruit people."

### "What if my referral has a bad experience?"
**Response**: "Then we'll fix it. And if we genuinely drop the ball, we'll refund them AND still pay your referral fee. Your reputation is on the line, so we take that seriously. Plus, you can stay looped in as much as you want."

### "Is there a cap on referrals?"
**Response**: "Nope. Refer 1 person or 100 people - we'll pay out on every single one. In fact, we have volume bonuses (refer 5+ per year, earn $750 per referral instead of $500)."

## Monitoring & Optimization

### Success Metrics (Track Monthly)

**Participation Rate:**
- % of clients enrolled in program (target: 60%+)
- % of enrolled clients who actively refer (target: 30%+)

**Referral Volume:**
- Total referrals received per month (target: 5-10)
- Qualified referrals (target: 60%+ of total)

**Conversion Rate:**
- Referral → client conversion (target: 40%+)
- Higher than standard lead conversion (trust factor)

**ROI:**
- Cost per referral acquisition (reward + admin)
- Referred client lifetime value
- Program profitability

---

### Quarterly Review

**What's working?**
- Which clients refer the most? (double down on activating similar clients)
- Which messaging/templates drive most referrals?
- Credit vs. cash preference?

**What's not working?**
- Low enrollment? (need better communication)
- Low referrals from enrolled clients? (need better templates/support)
- Low conversion? (might be wrong fit referrals - need better qualification guidance)

**Optimization:**
- Test higher reward amounts ($750 or $1,000 for limited time)
- Test exclusive bonuses (first referrer of the month gets $1,000)
- Improve marketing materials based on feedback
