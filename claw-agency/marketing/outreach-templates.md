## LinkedIn DM Templates

### DM Template 1: After They Engage With Your Content

Hey [Name], thanks for [liking/commenting on] my post about [topic].

Quick question: is [the problem discussed in the post] something your team deals with? Curious because we just built a system that handles it pretty well.

No pitch, genuinely curious if it's a real pain point at [Company].

### DM Template 2: Cold But Warm

Hey [Name], I've been following [Company Name] for a bit. Impressive growth.

Random thought: companies at your stage usually start hitting operational bottlenecks. Stuff that worked with 5 people breaks at 20. A lot of it is fixable with automation.

Would you be open to a 15-min workflow audit? I'll point out the 2-3 biggest time sinks and how to fix them. Free, no strings.

Either way, keep crushing it.

### DM Template 3: Mutual Connection / Referral

Hey [Name], [Mutual Connection] mentioned you might be dealing with [specific pain point].

We build AI automation systems that fix exactly that. Just helped a similar company [one-line result].

Would a quick call make sense, or should I send over a short breakdown of how it'd work for [Company]?
