## STORIES / REELS IDEAS (Bonus)

1. **"Watch our AI answer a real call"** - Screen recording of the AI receptionist in action. 30 seconds. Show the natural voice, the qualification flow, and the booking. This is the most powerful piece of content we can create.

2. **"The $19,000 voicemail"** - Quick story format. "This plumber left his phone on silent for one afternoon. Here's what it cost him." Walk through the math.

3. **"Day in the life: AI Receptionist"** - Timelapse of calls coming in over 24 hours, each one handled automatically. Dashboard view. Satisfying.

4. **"I asked a contractor how many calls he missed last week"** - Interview-style or text overlay format. Real conversation (or realistic recreation). Punchline: "He didn't know. That's the problem."

5. **"What your customer hears when you don't pick up"** - Play a standard voicemail greeting. Then play what they hear with ClawOps AI. Night and day difference.
