# Demo Call: Auto Repair Status Check
**Scenario:** Customer calls to check on their car that's been in the shop since yesterday morning. The shop is slammed and the service writer can't get to the phone. AI receptionist pulls up the status and gives a real update.

---

| # | Caller | AI Receptionist |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | *(phone rings)* | Thanks for calling Valley Auto Care. How can I help you? |
| 2 | Hey, I dropped my car off yesterday for some brake work and I haven't heard anything. Just wanted to check on it. | Absolutely. Let me pull that up. Can I get your last name or the make and model of the vehicle? |
| 3 | Last name is Ramirez. It's a 2019 Honda Accord. | Got it. I see your vehicle here, Maria. 2019 Honda Accord, dropped off Tuesday morning for a brake inspection. |
| 4 | Yeah, that's me. So what's going on with it? | Let me check the latest notes. *(brief pause)* Your vehicle has been inspected. The technician found that your front brake pads are worn down and the rotors need resurfacing. The rear brakes still have good life on them. |
| 5 | Okay. How much is that going to cost? | For front brake pads and rotor resurfacing on your Accord, you're looking at $385 for parts and labor. That includes ceramic pads and the rotor work. Want me to go ahead and authorize that? |
| 6 | $385? Yeah, that's about what I figured. Go ahead with it. | Great, I'll get that authorized for you right now. The tech should have it done by end of day today. We'll send you a text the moment it's ready for pickup. |
| 7 | Perfect. What time do you guys close? | We're open until 6:00 PM today. If the car is ready before 5:30, you'll have plenty of time. If it runs past that for any reason, we'll let you know and hold it for first thing tomorrow morning. |
| 8 | Sounds good. One more thing. My check engine light has been on for a while. Can they look at that too while it's there? | Sure. We can run a diagnostic scan. That's $95, and if it leads to a repair, we credit the diagnostic fee toward the work. Want me to add that? |
| 9 | Yeah, let's do that too. Might as well knock it out while it's there. | Smart call. I'll add the diagnostic to your ticket. So we've got front brakes at $385 plus the engine diagnostic at $95. I'll have the tech prioritize both so we can hopefully still get it all done today. |
| 10 | Great. You'll text me when it's done? | Yes, you'll get a text as soon as it's ready, along with a summary of everything that was done. If the diagnostic turns up anything significant, someone will call you to discuss it before doing any additional work. |
| 11 | Perfect. Thanks for the update. | You're welcome, Maria. We'll take good care of it. Talk to you soon. |

---

**What this demo shows:**
- Real-time vehicle status lookup by customer name
- Specific repair details relayed (not just "it's being worked on")
- Price quoted and work authorized without putting the caller on hold
- Upsell handled naturally (engine diagnostic added to existing visit)
- Clear communication about timeline, pickup, and next steps
- SMS notification promised when vehicle is ready
