# Demo Call: Dental Appointment Reschedule
**Scenario:** Patient calls to reschedule a cleaning appointment. Front desk is busy with a patient checkout. AI receptionist handles the entire rebooking.

---

| # | Caller | AI Receptionist |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | *(phone rings)* | Good morning, Bright Smile Dental. How can I help you today? |
| 2 | Hi, I need to reschedule my appointment. I've got a conflict that came up. | Of course. I can help with that. Can I get your name so I can pull up your appointment? |
| 3 | David Chen. | Thanks, David. I see you have a cleaning scheduled for this Thursday, March 5th at 2:00 PM with Dr. Patel. Is that the one? |
| 4 | Yep, that's the one. Work meeting got moved to that afternoon and I can't get out of it. | No problem at all. Let me check what we have open. Do you have a preference for morning or afternoon, and are there any days that work better for you? |
| 5 | Mornings are better for me. Any day next week except Monday. | Let me look at next week. *(brief pause)* I have a few options: Tuesday March 10th at 9:30 AM, Wednesday March 11th at 8:00 AM, or Friday March 13th at 10:00 AM. All with Dr. Patel. |
| 6 | Wednesday at 8:00 works. Early is good, I can still make it to the office on time. | Perfect. I'm moving you to Wednesday, March 11th at 8:00 AM with Dr. Patel for a standard cleaning. I'll send you a confirmation text right now and you'll also get a reminder the day before. |
| 7 | Sounds good. Actually, quick question. My wife needs to schedule a cleaning too. Can I do that while I'm on the phone? | Absolutely. What's her name? |
| 8 | Jennifer Chen. She's already a patient there. | Got it. I see Jennifer in our system. Would she like the same day, or a different day? |
| 9 | Same day would be great if you have something close to my time. | I have 8:45 AM right after your slot, or 9:30 AM. Either of those work? |
| 10 | 8:45. She can ride with me. | Done. Jennifer Chen, Wednesday March 11th at 8:45 AM with Dr. Patel. I'll send her a confirmation text as well. What's the best number for her? |
| 11 | 702-555-6102. | Got it. Both confirmations are on the way. Is there anything else I can help with, David? |
| 12 | Nope, that's everything. Thanks for making it easy. | You're welcome. We'll see you and Jennifer on Wednesday morning. Have a great day. |

---

**What this demo shows:**
- Instant appointment lookup by patient name
- Multiple availability options offered (not just "we'll call you back")
- Rebooking completed in under 2 minutes
- Handled a second booking in the same call (upsell moment)
- SMS confirmations sent to both patients automatically
- Friendly, efficient tone that respects the caller's time
