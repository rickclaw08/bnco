# Demo Call: Plumber Quote Request
**Scenario:** Homeowner calls asking about pricing for a clogged kitchen drain. AI receptionist gathers details, explains the process, and books an estimate visit.

---

| # | Caller | AI Receptionist |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | *(phone rings)* | Thanks for calling Westside Plumbing. How can I help you? |
| 2 | Hi, yeah. I've got a clogged kitchen drain and I wanted to get an idea on pricing before I have someone come out. | Sure thing. I can walk you through how we handle drain cleaning and get you a ballpark. Let me ask a few questions so I can give you an accurate range. Sound good? |
| 3 | Yeah, go ahead. | Great. First, is this a single kitchen sink, or a double basin? |
| 4 | Double basin. Both sides are backing up. | Okay. And when did you first notice it? Is it draining slowly or completely stopped? |
| 5 | It started draining slow about a week ago. Now it barely drains at all. I ran the garbage disposal and that didn't help. | Got it. That usually means the clog is past the disposal, deeper in the line. One more question: do you have a cleanout access point, or do you know if the house is on a slab or has a crawlspace? |
| 6 | Honestly I have no idea. It's a ranch-style house built in the 80s if that helps. | That's actually very helpful. Homes from that era often have accessible cleanouts, which makes the job straightforward. For a standard kitchen drain cleaning, our pricing starts at $175 for a cable/snake clearing. If it turns out we need hydro-jetting for a more stubborn blockage, that runs $350 to $450 depending on the line length. |
| 7 | Okay, that's reasonable. What if it's something bigger, like a broken pipe or something? | Good question. Our tech does a visual assessment when they arrive. If they suspect a line issue, we offer a camera inspection for $150, which gets credited toward any repair work. So you're not paying twice. |
| 8 | Alright. I think I just want to get someone out to look at it. How soon can you come? | Let me check the schedule. *(brief pause)* We have an opening tomorrow afternoon between 1:00 and 3:00 PM, or Thursday morning between 9:00 and 11:00. What works for you? |
| 9 | Tomorrow afternoon is great. | Perfect. Can I get your name and address? |
| 10 | Mike Torres. 1880 Desert Willow Lane, Las Vegas. | Got it, Mike. I'm booking you for tomorrow, Wednesday, between 1:00 and 3:00 PM. Our tech will call you about 30 minutes before they arrive. I'll also send you a confirmation text with the details. What's the best number? |
| 11 | This one works. 702-555-4489. | You're all set. Confirmation is on the way. Just so you know, there's no charge for the estimate visit. You'll only pay if you decide to move forward with the work. |
| 12 | That's great. Thanks for all the info. | Anytime, Mike. We'll get that drain sorted out. See you tomorrow. |

---

**What this demo shows:**
- Transparent pricing without needing a manager or callback
- Technical intake questions that help the technician arrive prepared
- Tiered pricing explained clearly (basic vs. hydro-jet vs. camera)
- Appointment booked with a service window (industry standard)
- No-charge estimate clearly stated to reduce friction
- SMS confirmation with pre-arrival call promise
