# Demo Call: HVAC Emergency
**Scenario:** Homeowner calls about a broken AC unit at 9:14 PM on a Tuesday in July. Business is closed but the AI receptionist picks up instantly.

---

| # | Caller | AI Receptionist |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | *(phone rings)* | Thank you for calling Comfort Zone Heating and Air. This is your after-hours assistant. How can I help you tonight? |
| 2 | Yeah, hi. My AC just completely stopped working. It's like 90 degrees in my house right now and I've got two kids trying to sleep. | I'm sorry to hear that. A dead AC in this heat is miserable, especially with kids in the house. Let me get some info so we can get a tech out to you as fast as possible. Can I start with your name? |
| 3 | Sarah Mitchell. | Got it, Sarah. And what's the address where the unit stopped working? |
| 4 | 4217 Oakwood Drive, Henderson. | Thank you. And just to confirm, this is a residential home? |
| 5 | Yes, single family home. The unit is on the side of the house, one of those big ones. Not a window unit or anything. | Understood, sounds like a central air system. Can you tell me what happened? Did it shut off all at once, or was it making any unusual noises before it stopped? |
| 6 | It was running fine this morning. I got home around 7 and the house was warm. Thermostat says it's set to 72 but it's reading 88 inside. The unit outside isn't running at all. No noise, nothing. | Okay, so the outdoor condenser is completely off and the thermostat isn't triggering it. That helps our tech know what to check first. Sarah, we do have an on-call technician available tonight. There is an after-hours service fee of $89 on top of the standard diagnostic. Would you like me to dispatch someone? |
| 7 | Yes, absolutely. How soon can they get here? | Let me check availability. *(brief pause)* I can get someone to you between 10:00 and 10:30 tonight. Does that work? |
| 8 | That's perfect. Thank you so much. | Great. I'm booking that now. I'll also send you a confirmation text with the technician's name and an ETA once they're en route. What's the best number for that text? |
| 9 | This number is fine. 702-555-3841. | Got it. You'll receive a confirmation text in just a moment. One last thing: is there a gate code or anything the tech should know about accessing the property? |
| 10 | No gate. The unit is on the right side of the house. Tan-colored fence, just push it open. | Perfect. So to confirm: Sarah Mitchell, 4217 Oakwood Drive in Henderson, central AC not running, tech arriving between 10:00 and 10:30 PM tonight. After-hours fee of $89 plus diagnostic. |
| 11 | That's right. | You're all set, Sarah. Try to stay cool in the meantime. If anything changes, just call this number back and I'll be here. Have a good night. |
| 12 | Thank you so much. You guys are a lifesaver. | Happy to help. Goodnight. |

---

**What this demo shows:**
- After-hours call handling (no voicemail, no missed lead)
- Empathetic tone that matches the urgency
- Structured info gathering (name, address, unit type, symptoms)
- On-call tech dispatch with transparent pricing
- SMS confirmation sent automatically
- Access instructions collected for the technician
