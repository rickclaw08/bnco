# Demo Call: Roofing Lead Qualification
**Scenario:** Homeowner calls about storm damage after a hailstorm hit their neighborhood. AI receptionist qualifies the lead, captures key details, and books a free inspection.

**What this demo shows:**
- High-value lead qualification (homeowner, primary residence, 12 years, storm damage)
- Insurance process explained proactively to reduce buyer anxiety
- Photo intake via text (leveraging the same number for multimedia)
- Urgency matched without being pushy
- Free inspection clearly positioned as no-obligation
- Practical interim advice (tarping) builds trust and demonstrates expertise
- Full appointment confirmation with SMS and pre-arrival call
