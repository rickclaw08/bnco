## Key Differences From Old Plan

### 1. AI Receptionist is the new "gateway drug"
The old Starter Sprint ($600) was a loss leader that attracted bad clients. The AI Receptionist ($1,500-$3K setup + $400/mo) is a profit center that creates recurring revenue AND a case study for more sales.

### 2. Recurring revenue actually counts
Under the old plan, recurring was an afterthought. Now, ~26% of our $100K comes from monthly recurring. This means by the time we hit $100K, we have a $20K+/mo recurring base to build on.

### 3. Vertical focus makes every deal faster
Instead of selling "AI automation" to anyone who'll listen, we're selling "AI receptionist for plumbers" and "revenue automation for law firms." The pitch is tighter, the objections are predictable, the case studies are transferable.

### 4. The funnel compounds
Each tier feeds the next:
- Audit ($500) -> 65% convert to Receptionist or Sprint
- Receptionist ($2,250 + $400/mo) -> referrals + proves value for Sprint
- Sprint ($10K) -> 45% convert to AaaS retainer
- Agent Dev ($15K) -> 50% convert to AaaS retainer
- AaaS ($3K/mo) -> sticky, growing, high-LTV

### 5. Delivery is scalable
The old plan required 610 hours of custom work. The pivot plan requires ~400 hours, and 50%+ of that is templateable AI Receptionist builds.

## What Could Go Wrong

### Scenario: We Only Close 60% of Target
- Revenue: ~$65K (not $100K)
- MRR at month 3: ~$4,800
- **Still better than the old model's conservative scenario ($33.5K in 6 months)**
- Corrective action: Double outreach volume, offer founding client discounts more aggressively

### Scenario: AI Receptionist Churn is 10% Instead of 5%
- Impact: ~$3K less recurring revenue
- Not catastrophic - one-time revenue still carries the load
- Corrective action: Monthly ROI reports, proactive optimization calls

### Scenario: Sprint Pipeline Doesn't Materialize
- Impact: $30K hole in projections
- Mitigation: Replace with 6 more AI Receptionist setups ($13.5K) + push AaaS harder
- Would extend timeline to 100-110 days instead of 90
