## 1. Starter Package - $600 (One-Time)

**Tagline:** "One deliverable. Done right. Done fast."

**Ideal Client:** Solo founders, early-stage startups, or small businesses that need one specific thing built, fixed, or set up.

**Turnaround:** 3-5 business days

### Exact Deliverables (pick one):

- **Landing Page Build:** Single responsive landing page with up to 5 sections, mobile-optimized, deployed to your hosting (Vercel, Netlify, or similar). Includes contact form integration and basic SEO meta tags.
- **Bot or Automation Setup:** One workflow automation (Zapier, Make, n8n, or custom script). Examples: lead capture to CRM, email notifications, data sync between two platforms. Includes testing and documentation.
- **AI Tool Integration:** Connect one AI service (OpenAI, Claude, etc.) to your existing workflow. Examples: AI-powered email responder, content generator, data classifier. Includes prompt engineering and basic error handling.
- **Codebase Audit:** Review of one repository (up to 10,000 lines). Deliverable is a written report covering architecture issues, security concerns, performance bottlenecks, and prioritized recommendations.
- **DevOps Setup:** CI/CD pipeline configuration for one project (GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, or similar). Includes automated testing, build, and deploy to one environment.

### What's Included:

- 1 discovery call (30 minutes)
- 1 round of revisions
- Delivered source code or configuration with documentation
- 30-minute handoff call with walkthrough
- 7 days of bug-fix support post-delivery

### What's NOT Included:

- Ongoing maintenance or hosting costs
- Content creation (copy, images, branding)
- Multi-page applications or complex backends
- Third-party service subscription costs

## 2. Growth Package - $2,000/month (Retainer)

**Tagline:** "Your dev team, without the overhead."

**Ideal Client:** Growing startups and SMBs that need consistent development capacity without hiring full-time.

**Commitment:** Month-to-month, 30-day cancellation notice

### Exact Deliverables:

**Hours:** 20 hours/month of development and technical work

**Scope of Work:**
- Feature development on existing applications
- Bug fixes and maintenance
- Code reviews and pull request management
- Technical documentation updates
- Infrastructure monitoring and minor adjustments
- Integration of third-party services and APIs

**Communication and Reporting:**
- Weekly async status update (written summary of work completed, hours used, priorities for next week)
- 1 live sync call per week (30 minutes)
- Slack or Discord access for async questions (response within 4 business hours)
- Monthly summary report with hours breakdown

**Response Times:**
- Critical bugs (production down): 4-hour response, same-day fix
- High priority: 1 business day response
- Normal requests: 2 business day response

**Rollover Policy:** Up to 5 unused hours roll over to the next month (max 25 hours in any month).

### What's Included:

- All work tracked and logged transparently
- Access to shared project board (Linear, Trello, or GitHub Projects)
- Quarterly tech health check (architecture review, dependency audit)
- Priority scheduling over non-retainer clients

### What's NOT Included:

- Work exceeding 20 hours/month (billed at $125/hour overage rate)
- Third-party service costs (hosting, APIs, licenses)
- Design work (UI/UX design, branding, graphics)
- Project management of external teams

**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks

## Pricing Philosophy

1. **No hourly billing for Starter or Sprint.** Fixed price, fixed scope. Clients know exactly what they pay and what they get.
2. **Transparent hours for retainers.** Growth and Enterprise clients see exactly where their hours go.
3. **Value over time.** We price based on the value of the outcome, not the hours it takes us.
4. **No discounts on published rates.** We offer "Founding Client" rates (10% off for first 3 months) only during the initial sprint period. After that, published rates hold.
