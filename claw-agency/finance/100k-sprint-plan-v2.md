## Contingency Plans

### If Sprint deals slip (highest risk, largest dollar amounts)

- **Trigger:** Fewer than 2 Sprint projects closed by end of Week 3
- **Action:** Offer "Quick Sprint" packages at $5,000 for smaller-scope projects (2-week delivery)
- **Action:** Bundle 2 Starter packages as a "Starter Pro" at $1,000 to increase volume
- **Action:** Increase outreach volume by 50% targeting Sprint-ready prospects

### If Enterprise deals stall (long sales cycle risk)

- **Trigger:** Zero Enterprise closes by end of Week 3
- **Action:** Offer a 30-day trial at $3,500 (reduced from $5,000) with full-price renewal
- **Action:** Pivot Enterprise outreach time toward closing more Growth retainers
- **Action:** Target 6 Growth retainers instead of 4 to compensate ($12,000 additional)

### If outreach conversion drops below 5%

- **Trigger:** Reply rates consistently under 5% for 2 weeks
- **Action:** Shift to warm channels only (LinkedIn, referrals, communities)
- **Action:** Publish 2-3 value-driven posts per week on LinkedIn/Twitter to generate inbound
- **Action:** Offer free 30-minute strategy sessions as lead magnets
- **Action:** Partner with complementary service providers for mutual referrals

### If collections lag (revenue recognized but not collected)

- **Trigger:** More than $10,000 in outstanding invoices past Net 15
- **Action:** Switch all new clients to upfront payment only
- **Action:** Offer 5% early payment discount on outstanding invoices
- **Action:** Implement automated payment reminders at Day 7, Day 12, and Day 15

### Nuclear option: If we hit Week 4 under $40K collected

- Offer a "Founding Client" deal: 3-month Growth retainer prepaid at $5,000 (save $1,000)
- Launch a limited "Build Week" - intensive 5-day sprint for $5,000 (lower barrier)
- Reach out to freelancer networks for subcontract overflow work at margin
