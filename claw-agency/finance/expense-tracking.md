## Weekly Tracking Routine

**Every Friday (15 minutes):**
1. Open Wave (or your tracker)
2. Review week's transactions
3. Categorize any uncategorized expenses
4. Snap photos of any paper receipts → upload
5. Note any upcoming expenses

**Monthly (30 minutes):**
1. Review P&L in Wave
2. Compare actual vs projected expenses
3. Identify any unnecessary spending
4. Update projections if needed
5. Set aside 30% of profit for taxes

## Rules

1. **Separate accounts from Day 1** — Personal and business money never mix
2. **No cash transactions** — Everything through the business account for paper trail
3. **Receipt for everything over $25** — Digital photo is fine
4. **Categorize weekly** — Don't let it pile up
5. **30% tax reserve** — Automatic transfer on every client payment
