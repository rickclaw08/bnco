## Key Assumptions
- **Client retention:** 90% monthly for Growth/Enterprise
- **Growth rate:** ~50% monthly client acquisition
- **Contractor costs:** Scale at ~20-25% of revenue as we grow
- **No office, no salary** — pure profit distribution initially
- **Starter clients** may convert to Growth (assume 20% conversion)

## Revenue Mix Shift
- Month 1: 100% one-time projects
- Month 6: 82% recurring revenue (Growth + Enterprise)
- **Recurring revenue is the engine.** Prioritize Growth and Enterprise sales by Month 3.
