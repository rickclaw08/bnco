## Pipeline Entry Protocol

When adding a new lead:

1. Assign the next available row number
2. Fill in every column. No blanks except Contact Info (if unknown at Lead stage)
3. Source must be one of: **Upwork, Discord, Cold Email, Referral, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Inbound Web, Reddit, Other**
4. Deal Value is your best estimate. Update it as scope clarifies
5. Stage starts at LEAD unless they came in pre-qualified
6. Next Action must be specific and time-bound (e.g., "Send proposal by 2/23" not "Follow up")
7. Update Last Updated every time you touch the row

## Stage Transition Rules

- **LEAD to QUAL:** Confirmed they have budget, timeline, and a real problem we solve
- **QUAL to PROP:** Proposal or SOW drafted and sent
- **PROP to NEGO:** They responded with questions, counteroffers, or revision requests
- **NEGO to WON:** Contract signed and first payment received or Net 15 invoice sent
- **Any to LOST:** No response after 3 follow-ups, explicit rejection, or disqualifying factor found. Log the reason

## Follow-Up Cadence

| Stage | Follow-Up Timing |
|-------|-----------------|
| LEAD | Within 24 hours of first contact, then every 48 hours (max 3 attempts) |
| QUAL | Within 24 hours of qualification call/chat |
| PROP | 48 hours after proposal sent, then 72 hours, then final follow-up at 7 days |
| NEGO | Same day for counter-responses, 24 hours for clarifications |

## Weekly Pipeline Review

Every Monday at 09:00 EST:

1. Move stale leads (no activity 7+ days) to LOST with reason "No Response"
2. Update all Deal Values based on latest scope discussions
3. Verify Next Actions are current and time-bound
4. Calculate total pipeline value and update the Revenue Dashboard
5. Identify top 3 deals most likely to close this week
