## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

**Must-haves for hitting 90-day targets:**
1. **Speed to first client:** Get paying client by Day 10
2. **Recurring revenue focus:** Prioritize monthly retainers over one-time projects
3. **Client satisfaction:** Happy clients refer, unhappy clients churn
4. **Pipeline discipline:** Always have 3x target in pipeline (if target is $15K, have $45K in active proposals)
5. **Time management:** 60% client work, 30% sales/marketing, 10% admin

**Avoid These Traps:**
- Spending weeks on "perfect" website/branding
- Taking every client (say no to bad fits)
- Underpricing to win deals (race to bottom)
- Ignoring sales while delivering client work
