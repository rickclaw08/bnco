# Break-Even Analysis

> ClawOps | Revenue Milestones

---

## Context
- **$0 startup cost** — no debt, no investment to recoup
- "Break-even" here means hitting target monthly income levels
- Expenses scale with revenue (contractors, tools)

---

## Target: $5,000/mo Net Profit

### Required Revenue: ~$6,000/mo (after expenses)

**Scenario A — Growth-Heavy (Fastest)**
| Clients | Type | Revenue |
|---------|------|---------|
| 2 | Growth ($2K/mo) | $4,000 |
| 2 | Starter ($500) | $1,000 |
| 0 | Enterprise | $0 |
| | **Total** | **$5,000** |
| | Expenses (~15%) | -$750 |
| | **Net** | **$4,250** |

Need 1 more Starter or Growth client → **3 Growth + 1 Starter = $7,500 gross → ~$5,500 net** ✓

**Timeline: Month 3** (per projections)

**Scenario B — Mixed**
| Clients | Type | Revenue |
|---------|------|---------|
| 1 | Growth ($2K/mo) | $2,000 |
| 4 | Starter ($500) | $2,000 |
| 0 | Enterprise | $0 |
| | **Total** | **$4,000** |

Not enough. Need recurring clients.

**Key insight:** You can't hit $5K/mo sustainably on Starter alone. You'd need 10+ one-time clients/month. Growth retainers are the path.

**Timeline: Month 4** (per projections)

---

## Pipeline Requirements

### To sustain $5K/mo:
- **Active clients:** 3 Growth + 1-2 Starter
- **Pipeline needed:** 5-8 leads/month (assuming 25-30% close rate)
- **Lead sources:** Cold outreach, content marketing, referrals

### To sustain $10K/mo:
- **Active clients:** 1 Enterprise + 3 Growth + 2-3 Starter
- **Pipeline needed:** 10-15 leads/month
- **Lead sources:** Add partnerships, LinkedIn thought leadership, case studies

### To hit $25K/mo (Month 6 target):
- **Active clients:** 2 Enterprise + 6 Growth + 5 Starter
- **Pipeline needed:** 15-25 leads/month
- **Lead sources:** All above + paid ads, webinars, speaking

---

## Conversion Funnel Assumptions

| Stage | Conversion Rate | Volume Needed (for $10K/mo) |
|-------|----------------|---------------------------|
| Cold outreach sent | — | 200/month |
| Responses | 5% | 10 |
| Discovery calls | 50% of responses | 5 |
| Proposals sent | 80% of calls | 4 |
| Closed deals | 50% of proposals | 2 new clients/month |

**Plus:** 1-2 inbound leads/month from content + referrals

---

## Churn Impact

| Monthly Churn | Effect on $10K/mo |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 0% | Steady growth — every new client adds |
| 5% (1 in 20) | Lose ~$500/mo, easily replaced |
| 10% (1 in 10) | Lose ~$1,000/mo, need constant acquisition |
| 20% | Unsustainable — fix delivery quality |

**Target churn: <10%.** Deliver results, communicate proactively, do quarterly reviews.

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **$5K/mo is achievable by Month 3** with focused sales effort
2. **$10K/mo by Month 4** requires landing at least 1 Enterprise client
3. **Recurring revenue (Growth tier) is everything** — one-time Starter work is supplementary
4. **Pipeline is a numbers game** — 200 outreach/mo → 2 clients at current stage
5. **Retention > Acquisition** — keeping a $2K client is easier than finding a new one
