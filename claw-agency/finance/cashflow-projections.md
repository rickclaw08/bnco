## Key Risks to Cash Flow

1. **Slow pipeline:** If outreach does not convert, revenue lags. Mitigate with consistent daily outreach.
2. **Client churn:** Losing a $2K Growth client hurts. Mitigate with excellent delivery and communication.
3. **Scope creep:** Unbilled work eats margins. Mitigate with clear contracts and change order processes.
4. **Late payments:** Net 30 terms can delay cash. Mitigate with Stripe auto-billing and upfront payments for Starter.
5. **Tax surprise:** Forgetting to reserve for taxes. Mitigate with 30% auto-transfer on every payment.
6. **Contractor dependency:** Scaling too fast with expensive contractors. Mitigate with automation-first approach.

## Action Plan by Scenario

**If tracking Conservative:**
- Double down on outreach volume (200+ per month)
- Offer Launch Package to close faster
- Focus on local businesses for quicker trust-building
- Consider partnerships with complementary agencies

**If tracking Moderate:**
- Stay the course, focus on Growth-tier conversions
- Start building case studies for Enterprise sales
- Invest in content marketing for inbound leads
- Begin contractor onboarding for scalability

**If tracking Aggressive:**
- Hire/contract immediately to maintain delivery quality
- Document all processes for delegation
- Consider forming LLC sooner
- Start building toward productized offerings
- Explore S-Corp election for tax savings
