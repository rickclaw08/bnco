# ClawOps - Payment Terms and Policies

**Effective Date:** February 2026
**Owner:** Morgan (CFO, ClawOps)

---

## Payment Terms by Package

### Starter Package ($600 one-time)

- **Payment:** Full payment due before work begins
- **Method:** Stripe payment link (credit card or ACH)
- **Refund Policy:** Full refund if work has not started. No refund once work begins. If ClawOps fails to deliver within the agreed timeline, client receives a full refund.

### Growth Retainer ($2,000/month)

- **Payment:** Net 15 from invoice date
- **Billing Cycle:** Invoiced on the 1st of each month for the current month
- **First Month:** Pro-rated if starting mid-month, due before work begins
- **Method:** Stripe autopay (preferred), manual payment link, or ACH
- **Cancellation:** 30-day written notice required. Final invoice covers the notice period.

### Sprint Package ($7,500-$15,000)

- **Deposit:** 50% due before work begins (non-refundable once project kicks off)
- **Final Payment:** 50% due upon delivery and client acceptance
- **Acceptance Period:** Client has 5 business days after delivery to review and accept or request revisions within scope
- **Method:** Stripe payment link (credit card or ACH)
- **Change Orders:** Scope changes after kickoff are quoted separately and require 100% payment before work begins on the change

### Enterprise Retainer ($5,000/month)

- **Payment:** Net 15 from invoice date
- **Billing Cycle:** Invoiced on the 1st of each month for the current month
- **First Month:** Full month due before engagement begins (applied to Month 1)
- **Minimum Commitment:** 3 months
- **Method:** Stripe autopay (preferred), manual payment link, or ACH
- **Cancellation:** 30-day written notice required after minimum commitment. Final invoice covers the notice period.

## General Payment Policies

### Accepted Payment Methods

1. **Credit/Debit Card** via Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
2. **ACH Bank Transfer** via Stripe
3. **Wire Transfer** (for invoices over $5,000, upon request)
4. **Zelle** (for invoices under $2,000)

### Late Payment Policy

- Invoices not paid within the stated terms are considered past due
- A late fee of 1.5% per month (18% annually) will be applied to the outstanding balance starting on the day after the due date
- ClawOps reserves the right to pause all work on accounts with invoices more than 15 days past due
- Accounts more than 30 days past due may be sent to collections

### Late Payment Escalation

| Days Past Due | Action |
|---------------|--------|
| 1 day | Automated payment reminder email |
| 7 days | Personal follow-up email from ClawOps |
| 15 days | Phone call, work paused on account |
| 30 days | Final notice, account suspension |
| 45 days | Referred to collections |

### Early Payment Discount

- Invoices paid within 5 days of issue receive a 2% discount (noted as "2/5 Net 15" on invoice)

### Disputes

- Payment disputes must be raised in writing within 10 business days of invoice date
- Undisputed portions of invoices remain due per original terms
- ClawOps will respond to disputes within 3 business days

## Invoice Numbering Convention

Format: `CLW-YYYY-NNNN`

- CLW: ClawOps prefix
- YYYY: Calendar year
- NNNN: Sequential number (starting at 0001 each year)

Examples:
- CLW-2026-0001 (first invoice of 2026)
- CLW-2026-0042 (42nd invoice of 2026)

## Tax Information

- ClawOps invoices do not include sales tax unless required by applicable state/local law
- Clients are responsible for any applicable use tax in their jurisdiction
- W-9 available upon request for US clients
- For international clients, services are generally zero-rated for VAT/GST purposes

## Refund Policy

| Situation | Refund |
|-----------|--------|
| Starter: work not started | 100% refund |
| Starter: work in progress | No refund |
| Starter: ClawOps fails to deliver | 100% refund |
| Sprint: before kickoff | 100% of deposit |
| Sprint: after kickoff, ClawOps at fault | Pro-rated refund based on work completed |
| Sprint: client cancels mid-project | No refund on deposit; final payment not due |
| Retainer: unused hours | No refund (rollover policy applies per package terms) |
| Retainer: ClawOps fails to deliver agreed hours | Credit applied to next month |
