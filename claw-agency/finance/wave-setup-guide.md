# Wave Setup Guide for ClawOps

> Free invoicing, accounting, and expense tracking

---

## Why Wave

- 100% free invoicing and accounting (no subscription fees)
- Professional invoice templates with your branding
- Recurring invoices for Growth and Enterprise clients
- Automatic payment reminders
- Built-in expense tracking and reporting
- Connects to your bank for auto-import
- P&L, balance sheet, and tax reports included

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [waveapps.com](https://www.waveapps.com)
2. Click "Sign up for free"
3. Enter email: agentclaw08@icloud.com
4. Create password
5. Verify email

## Step 2: Set Up Your Business Profile

1. Click "Add a business" or complete the onboarding wizard
2. Business name: **ClawOps**
3. Business type: "Marketing, Communications & Design" or "Information Technology"
4. Currency: USD
5. Fiscal year start: January
6. Address: Enter your business address
7. Upload ClawOps logo (appears on all invoices)

## Step 3: Connect Your Bank Account

1. Go to **Banking > Add an account**
2. Search for your bank (Mercury, Relay, Chase, etc.)
3. Log in to your bank through the secure connection
4. Wave auto-imports transactions daily
5. You can also manually upload bank statements (CSV/OFX)

## Step 4: Set Up Expense Categories

1. Go to **Accounting > Chart of Accounts**
2. Default categories work well. Add custom ones if needed:
   - AI/API Costs (under Cost of Goods Sold)
   - Software Subscriptions (under Operating Expenses)
   - Contractor Payments (under Operating Expenses)
   - Marketing and Advertising (under Operating Expenses)

## Step 5: Create Your Invoice Template

1. Go to **Sales > Invoices > Create invoice**
2. Customize the template:
   - Logo: Upload ClawOps logo
   - Invoice number format: Set to "CO-" prefix (e.g., CO-0001)
   - Default payment terms: Net 15
   - Footer: Add payment instructions or thank-you message
   - Accent color: Match ClawOps brand

## Step 6: Create Your First Invoice

1. Go to **Sales > Invoices > Create invoice**
2. Add customer:
   - Click "Add a customer"
   - Enter client name, email, company, address
3. Invoice details:
   - Invoice number: CO-0001
   - Invoice date: Today
   - Payment due: Net 15 (auto-calculates due date)
4. Add line items:
   - Description: "ClawOps Growth Plan - Managed AI Automation (March 2026)"
   - Rate: $2,000.00
   - Quantity: 1
5. Add tax if applicable (most B2B services are tax-exempt)
6. Review and send

## Step 7: Set Up Recurring Invoices

1. Go to **Sales > Recurring invoices > Create recurring invoice**
2. Select the customer
3. Set frequency: Monthly
4. Set start date and optional end date
5. Add line items (e.g., Growth Plan at $2,000/mo)
6. Enable "Auto-send" so invoices go out automatically
7. Wave sends the invoice on your schedule without manual work

## Step 8: Enable Payment Reminders

1. Go to **Settings > Invoice preferences**
2. Enable automatic reminders:
   - 3 days before due date: "Friendly reminder"
   - On due date: "Payment due today"
   - 3 days after due date: "Payment overdue"
   - 7 days after: "Second reminder"
3. Customize reminder messages if desired

## Step 9: Enable Online Payments (Optional)

1. Wave offers Wave Payments (built-in payment processing)
2. Go to **Settings > Online payments**
3. Credit card processing: 2.9% + $0.60 per transaction
4. Bank payments (ACH): 1% per transaction ($1 minimum)
5. **Alternative:** Add your Stripe payment link in the invoice notes/footer instead
6. Clients can pay directly from the invoice email

## Step 10: Run Reports

Wave generates these reports automatically:

1. **Profit and Loss (P&L):** Sales > Reports > Profit & Loss
   - Shows revenue minus expenses for any date range
   - Use monthly for tracking against projections

2. **Balance Sheet:** Accounting > Reports > Balance Sheet
   - Shows assets, liabilities, and equity

3. **Tax Summary:** Reports > Sales Tax Report
   - Useful for quarterly estimated tax calculations

4. **Aged Receivables:** Reports > Aged Receivables
   - Shows which invoices are outstanding and how overdue

## Invoice Numbering Convention

- Format: CO-YYYY-### (e.g., CO-2026-001)
- Sequential per year
- Set in Wave under invoice preferences

## Payment Terms by Tier

| Tier | Terms | Method |
|------|-------|--------|
| Starter ($500) | Due on receipt | Stripe payment link before work begins |
| Growth ($2,000/mo) | Net 15 | Stripe subscription preferred, Wave invoice as backup |
| Enterprise ($5-15K/mo) | Net 30 | Wave invoice with Stripe/ACH payment link |

## Weekly Routine (5 minutes)

1. Open Wave
2. Check **Dashboard** for outstanding invoices
3. Go to **Banking** and categorize any new transactions
4. Review any overdue invoices and follow up manually if reminders did not work
5. Done

## Monthly Routine (15 minutes)

1. Run P&L report for the month
2. Compare against projections
3. Review expense categories for accuracy
4. Export reports for your records
5. Calculate 30% tax reserve from net profit

## Tips

- **Wave is free forever** for invoicing and accounting. They make money on payroll and payment processing.
- **Use Wave for tracking, Stripe for collecting.** Best of both worlds.
- **Export data anytime.** Wave lets you export all data as CSV if you ever switch tools.
- **Mobile app available.** Snap receipts on the go and categorize later.
- **Multi-currency support.** If you take international clients, Wave handles currency conversion.
