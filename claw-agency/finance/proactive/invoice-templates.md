## Invoice Numbering Convention

- **CO-S-XXXX** — Starter tier (e.g., CO-S-0001)
- **CO-G-XXXX** — Growth tier (e.g., CO-G-0001)
- **CO-E-XXXX** — Enterprise tier (e.g., CO-E-0001)

Sequential within each tier. Reset annually or run continuously.

## Payment Terms Summary

| Tier | Terms | Late Fee | Auto-Pay Default |
|------|-------|----------|------------------|
| Starter | Net 15 | 1.5%/mo | Yes (card on file) |
| Growth | Net 15 | 1.5%/mo | Yes (card on file) |
| Enterprise | Net 30 | 1.5%/mo | Optional |
