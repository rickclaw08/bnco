## 1. Discount & Incentive Strategy

### Recommendation: YES — Offer a Limited "Founding Client" Incentive

**Why:** We have zero clients, zero testimonials, zero social proof. That is our biggest liability right now. The first sale isn't about margin — it's about breaking the seal. A discount framed as an exclusive founding offer creates urgency AND gives us a credible reason to lower the price without looking desperate.

### The Offer: "Founding 5 Program"

Only the first 5 clients get these perks. After that, full price forever.

| Tier | Standard Price | Founding Client Price | Discount | Incentive Sweetener |
|------|---------------|----------------------|----------|---------------------|
| **Starter Sprint** | $600 | **$397** | 34% off | + Free 30-min strategy call ($200 value) |
| **Growth Retainer** | $2,000/mo | **$1,497/mo** (locked for 3 months) | 25% off | + Free process audit ($500 value) + Rate locked for 6 months |
| **Automation Sprint** | $7,500 | **$4,997** | 33% off | + 60 days post-launch support (vs 30 standard) |
| **Enterprise** | $5,000/mo | **$3,497/mo** (locked for 3 months) | 30% off | + Dedicated Slack channel + Monthly strategy call |

### Why These Numbers Work

- **$397 Starter** — Impulse-buy territory. Under $400 removes the "I need to think about it" barrier. Most small biz owners will spend this on a nice dinner. It's a no-brainer.
- **$1,497/mo Growth** — Feels premium enough to be taken seriously, cheap enough to say yes fast. The "locked for 6 months" framing makes them feel like they're getting a steal.
- **$4,997 Sprint** — Psychologically under $5K, which is the threshold where many small businesses need partner/spouse/board approval. Staying under keeps it a solo decision.
- **$3,497/mo Enterprise** — Under the "I need to involve my accountant" threshold for many mid-size companies.

### Rules for the Discount

1. **It expires.** "This founding rate is available through Friday, Feb 28." (Give them a hard deadline slightly past our Wednesday target — this gives us breathing room for follow-up.)
2. **It's limited.** "We're only taking 5 founding clients at this rate." (Scarcity = urgency.)
3. **It requires a testimonial.** "In exchange for the founding rate, we ask for an honest case study / testimonial after 30 days." (This is the REAL currency we're buying.)
4. **Never say 'discount.'** Always say "founding client rate" or "early adopter pricing." Discounts = weakness. Founding rate = exclusivity.

## 2. "Close Within 24 Hours" Script

### The Mindset

When a prospect responds — via email, DM, Reddit, Upwork, anywhere — the clock starts. Every hour that passes, close probability drops ~10%. Our goal: **payment link clicked within 24 hours of first response.**

### Script: The Warm-Up (First Reply, within 15 minutes)

> Hey [Name]! Really glad you reached out.
>
> Quick question before we dive in — what's the #1 thing eating up your time right now that you wish just... handled itself?
>
> I ask because we specialize in exactly that, and I want to make sure I'm focused on what actually matters to you, not just throwing features at you.

**Purpose:** Get them talking about their pain. Pain is what we sell the solution to.

### Script: The Pivot to Value (After They Describe Pain)

> Got it. So [restate their pain in their own words]. That's costing you roughly [X hours/week], which is basically $[Y]/month in time you're burning.
>
> Here's the good news — that's one of the most common things we automate. We've built [brief relevant example] that cuts that down by 70-80%.
>
> I actually have a quick proposal I can send over right now if you want to see exactly what this would look like for you. Takes 2 minutes to read. Want me to send it?

**Purpose:** Anchor the cost of inaction. Make the solution feel easy and immediate.

### Script: The Close (After Sending Proposal)

> Just sent it over — take a look when you get a sec.
>
> Quick heads up: we're running a founding client program right now. First 5 clients get [their tier] at [founding price] instead of [standard price], plus [bonus]. We've got [X] spots left.
>
> If this looks good, there's a payment link right in the proposal — you can lock it in now and we'll kick off within 48 hours. 
>
> Any questions at all, I'm right here.

**Purpose:** Create urgency. Make paying frictionless. Remove the "schedule a follow-up call" delay.

### Script: The Nudge (If No Response After 4 Hours)

> Hey [Name], just checking — did you get a chance to look at the proposal? Happy to hop on a quick 10-minute call if you want to walk through it together. I've got [two specific times today/tomorrow] open.

### Script: The Final Push (If No Response After 24 Hours)

> Hey [Name], totally understand if the timing isn't right. Just wanted to let you know the founding client rate expires [Friday], and I'd hate for you to miss it.
>
> If budget is the concern, we also have a Starter Sprint at $397 that handles [their specific pain] as a quick win. No long commitment, just results.
>
> Either way, I'm here when you're ready. 🤙

**Purpose:** Offer the downsell. A $397 sale is infinitely better than a $0 sale.

### Key Rules During the Close Sequence

1. **Never say "no pressure."** That signals you don't believe in your own product.
2. **Always give a specific next step.** Not "let me know" → Instead "I can send the proposal right now."
3. **Match their energy and platform.** If they DM'd on Reddit, close on Reddit. Don't force them to email.
4. **Use their name.** People respond to personalization.
5. **Assume the sale.** Talk in terms of "when we start" not "if you decide to."

## 4. Minimum Viable Deal — The Floor

### The Question: What's the Absolute Minimum We Accept?

**Answer: $250 — but only under very specific conditions.**

### The Deal Tiers (What to Accept)

| Priority | Deal | Price | When to Accept |
|----------|------|-------|----------------|
| 🟢 **Ideal** | Starter Sprint at founding rate | $397 | Any qualified prospect. This is the target. |
| 🟡 **Acceptable** | Custom micro-project | $250–$350 | Prospect has a very specific, small need (1 workflow). They're a good testimonial candidate. |
| 🟠 **Last Resort** | Barter / Deeply Discounted | $150–$249 | Only if: (1) they're a PERFECT testimonial (well-known company, great story), AND (2) they commit IN WRITING to a video testimonial + case study + referral. |
| 🔴 **Never** | Free work / "exposure" / equity-only | $0 | NO. Not ever. Not even for the first client. Free work signals zero value. |

### Why $250 is the Floor (Not $0)

1. **Money = commitment.** Even $250 means they're invested. Free clients ghost, demand more, and never give testimonials.
2. **It validates the model.** Revenue — any revenue — proves someone will pay for this. That's the data point we need.
3. **It sets a precedent.** Your first price becomes an anchor. Start at $0, good luck ever charging $5K.
4. **It covers costs.** $250 covers API costs and a few hours of work. You're not losing money.

### The "Testimonial Trade" Script (For Minimum Deals)

> Look, I'm going to be straight with you. We're a new agency and we're building our client base. I want to do this project at a rate that makes it a no-brainer for you — [PRICE] instead of our usual [STANDARD PRICE].
>
> In return, here's what I'd ask: after we deliver, you give us an honest testimonial we can use on our site and in proposals. A short paragraph or a 60-second video — whatever's comfortable.
>
> Fair deal?

**Why this works:** It's honest, it's mutually beneficial, and it positions the discount as an exchange (not desperation).

## 💡 Final Notes from CFO

1. **Don't overthink it.** The first sale is ugly. Every business's first sale is ugly. What matters is it happens.
2. **Volume over perfection.** Send 50 proposals this week. If 2% convert, that's your first client. Send 100, that's two clients.
3. **The downsell is your friend.** If someone says the Growth Retainer is too much, don't argue — offer the Starter. If Starter is too much, offer the micro-project. Something > nothing.
4. **After the first sale, everything changes.** You have proof. You have a testimonial pipeline. You have momentum. You have a case study in progress. The second sale is 10x easier.
5. **Track every conversation.** Every DM, every email, every Reddit reply. Log it in the pipeline tracker. We need to know our conversion rates from day one.
