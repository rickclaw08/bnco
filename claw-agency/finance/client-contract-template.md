## 4. Confidentiality

### 4.1 Definition

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either party to the other in connection with this agreement, including but not limited to: business plans, customer data, technical specifications, pricing, financial information, trade secrets, and proprietary methodologies.

### 4.2 Obligations

Each party agrees to:

- Use Confidential Information only for purposes of performing under this agreement
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent
- Protect Confidential Information with the same degree of care used for its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care
- Limit access to Confidential Information to personnel who need it and who are bound by confidentiality obligations

### 4.3 Exclusions

Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that:

- Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving party
- Was already known to the receiving party before disclosure
- Is independently developed without reference to Confidential Information
- Is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order (with prompt notice to the disclosing party)

### 4.4 Duration

Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this agreement for a period of two (2) years.

## 6. Termination

### 6.1 Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate this agreement with fourteen (14) days written notice. In the event of termination for convenience:

- Client pays for all work completed through the termination date
- ClawOps delivers all completed and in-progress Work Product
- Any prepaid amounts for unperformed work will be refunded within fifteen (15) days

### 6.2 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other party:

- Materially breaches this agreement and fails to cure within seven (7) days of written notice
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy
- Fails to make payment within thirty (30) days of the due date

### 6.3 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:

- All outstanding invoices become due immediately
- IP ownership transfers only for fully paid deliverables
- Confidentiality obligations survive per Section 4.4
- Sections 3, 4, 7, and 8 survive termination
