# ClawOps ROI Calculator

**Show prospects exactly how much they'll save. Fill in their numbers. Let the math do the selling.**

---

## Step 1: Capture Their Current Cost

Ask the prospect these two questions during the discovery call:

| Input | Their Answer |
|---|---|
| Hours spent on manual/repetitive work per week | _____ hrs |
| Average fully-loaded hourly cost of that labor | $_____ /hr |

**Fully-loaded hourly cost** includes salary, benefits, taxes, and overhead. If they don't know, use these benchmarks:

| Role | Typical Fully-Loaded Rate |
|---|---|
| Administrative / Data Entry | $25-35/hr |
| Operations / Coordinator | $35-50/hr |
| Specialist / Analyst | $50-75/hr |
| Manager / Senior Staff | $75-100/hr |
| Director / VP | $100-150/hr |

---

## Step 2: Calculate Current Waste

| Metric | Formula | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Weekly cost of manual work | [Hours/week] x [Hourly rate] | $_____ /week |
| Monthly cost of manual work | Weekly cost x 4.33 | $_____ /month |
| Annual cost of manual work | Monthly cost x 12 | $_____ /year |

## How to Use This on a Call

1. **Ask the two input questions** during discovery
2. **Do the math live** (or estimate quickly): hours x rate x 4.33 = monthly cost of their problem
3. **Compare to the tier price**: "So you're spending $6,500/month on this. Our solution is $7,500 one time. That means you break even in 5 weeks, then save $6,500 every month after that. Forever."
4. **Let the numbers land.** Pause. Let them process.
5. **Then close:** "Want to get started?"
