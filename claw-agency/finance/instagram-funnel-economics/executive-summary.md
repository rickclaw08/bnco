## TL;DR

The Instagram Reels funnel is the right move. The unit economics work, the cost is near-zero, and it's infinitely better than the Reddit approach (45 posts, $0 revenue). But $20K in 30 days from a brand new account is best-case territory, not expected case.

## WHAT MUST HAPPEN FOR THIS TO WORK

1. **Post 2 reels per day, every day, for 30 days straight.** No exceptions. No "I'll catch up tomorrow."
2. **ManyChat set up BEFORE the first reel goes live.** Trigger word automation, qualification flow, booking link.
3. **Demo call must be impressive.** If the AI product isn't ready to show in a compelling way, the funnel breaks at this stage.
4. **Sales calls need a script and objection handling.** Winging it at $2K-$2.5K price points won't cut it.
5. **Content must be genuinely valuable.** Not "I built an AI system, DM me." Show the problem, show the solution, show the result.

## BIGGEST RISKS

1. Content quality doesn't stand out in a crowded AI agency niche
2. No case studies or testimonials to build trust (chicken-and-egg problem)
3. Demo call product isn't polished enough to wow
4. Sales skills aren't developed enough for high-ticket closing
5. Inconsistent posting kills algorithmic momentum

## RECOMMENDED NEXT STEPS

1. Buy ManyChat Pro ($15) today
2. Set up Instagram business profile with optimized bio
3. Build ManyChat flow (trigger word, qualification, booking link)
4. Script first 7 reels (research top-performing content in niche first)
5. Record and post first batch
6. Set up Calendly free for demo/sales bookings
7. Prepare demo call script and sales call script
