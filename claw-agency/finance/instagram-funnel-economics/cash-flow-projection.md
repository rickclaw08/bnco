## THE HONEST TRUTH

$20K in 30 days from a brand new Instagram account with zero followers, zero case studies, and zero social proof is in the "best case" territory. It's possible but requires:
1. Multiple reels hitting 5K-15K+ views
2. Tight DM automation converting at above-average rates
3. Strong closing on sales calls
4. Some luck with the algorithm

The expected path to $20K/month is more like 45-60 days. Month 1 is building the machine. Month 2 is when it starts producing consistently.

This is still dramatically better than Reddit (45 posts, $0 revenue, no clear path to revenue). Instagram gives you leverage that Reddit simply cannot.
