## CHANNEL COMPARISON: Instagram vs. Reddit vs. Paid

### Option A: Organic Instagram (Our Current Plan)

| Cost Category | Monthly Cost | Notes |
|--------------|-------------|-------|
| Instagram account | $0 | Free |
| ManyChat Pro (DM automation) | $15-65/mo | $15 for up to 500 contacts, scales with volume |
| Content creation tools (CapCut, Canva) | $0-25/mo | Free tiers available, Canva Pro is $13/mo |
| Scheduling tool (Later, Buffer) | $0-18/mo | Free tier covers 5 reels/week |
| Time cost (2 reels/day, 1-2 hrs each) | 60-90 hrs/month | This is the REAL cost. At $50/hr opportunity cost = $3,000-4,500/mo |
| **Total cash outlay** | **$15-108/mo** | |
| **Total including time value** | **$3,015-4,608/mo** | |

**Pros:**
- Near-zero cash cost
- Content compounds (old reels keep getting views)
- Builds brand authority and trust
- DM automation handles volume without extra effort
- Algorithm can produce outsized returns if content hits

**Cons:**
- Unpredictable reach (algorithm dependent)
- Slow ramp (2-4 weeks before meaningful traction)
- Requires daily content creation discipline
- No case studies = harder to make compelling content
- Competing with thousands of "AI agency" accounts

**Unit Economics with Paid Ads (Expected Case):**

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Ad spend per close | $300-500 |
| Revenue per close (blended) | $2,198 |
| Gross margin on acquisition | 77-86% |
| ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) | 4.4-7.3x |

**When to activate paid ads:**
- NOT until we have at least 2-3 case studies / testimonials
- NOT until we've validated the organic funnel converts
- NOT until we've closed at least 3-5 clients organically
- Ideal timing: Month 2-3, once we have proof the funnel works

**Pros:**
- Predictable, scalable lead flow
- Can target exact demographics (contractors, specific trades, geography)
- Speed up the funnel dramatically
- Test and optimize messaging with data

**Cons:**
- Requires budget we may not have yet
- Needs creative that converts (testing costs money)
- Can burn cash fast if targeting/messaging is wrong
- Instagram ad costs have been rising YoY

### Option C: Reddit-Only Approach (Current Reality)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Posts/replies made | 45 |
| Revenue generated | $0 |
| Time invested | ~15-20 hours estimated |
| Leads generated | Unknown/minimal |
| Cost per lead | Infinite (no leads closed) |

**Why Reddit hasn't worked:**
1. Reddit users are skeptical of self-promotion
2. Contractor subreddits have low tolerance for "selling"
3. Comments get buried, no algorithmic amplification
4. No visual proof of product (text-only medium)
5. One-to-one interaction (each reply reaches one person)
6. Reddit audience skews younger/tech-savvy, not necessarily contractor decision-makers

**Reddit's role going forward:**
- Keep it as a supplementary channel, not primary
- Use it for market research and understanding pain points
- Genuine helpful answers (no pitching) build slow credibility
- Link to Instagram content when relevant and natural
- Time allocation: max 2-3 hours/week, not more

## HEAD-TO-HEAD COMPARISON

| Factor | Organic IG | Paid IG Ads | Reddit Only |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| Cash cost/month | $15-108 | $1,015-3,565 | $0 |
| Time cost/month | 60-90 hrs | 15-25 hrs | 15-20 hrs |
| Time to first lead | 2-3 weeks | 3-5 days | Unknown (hasn't produced one yet) |
| Time to first close | 4-6 weeks | 2-3 weeks | Unknown |
| Scalability | Medium (limited by content output) | High (limited by budget) | Low (one-to-one) |
| Reach per unit of effort | High (one reel = thousands of views) | Very high (pay for reach) | Low (one reply = one person) |
| Trust building | High (video builds rapport) | Medium (feels like an ad) | Medium (text credibility) |
| Compounding effect | Strong (old reels keep working) | None (stops when you stop paying) | Weak (posts get buried) |
| Risk | Low (just time) | Medium (can waste money) | Low (just time, but high opportunity cost) |

---

## BOTTOM LINE

Reddit is a dead end as a primary channel. 45 posts, zero revenue. The effort-to-result ratio is terrible for direct sales. Keep it for research and credibility, not lead generation.

Instagram organic is the right primary channel. Near-zero cash cost, massive reach potential per piece of content, visual medium (critical for demonstrating AI), and the DM funnel is proven by hundreds of agencies.

Paid ads come later. Month 2-3 at the earliest, after we have case studies and proven the funnel converts organically.

ManyChat Pro is the single best $15/month we can spend. Non-negotiable.
