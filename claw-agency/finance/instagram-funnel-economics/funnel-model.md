## 1. FUNNEL STAGE BREAKDOWN

### Stage 1: Instagram Reels (Top of Funnel)

**Estimated Views Per Reel - New Account, Organic Only:**

| Scenario | Views/Reel | Basis |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Worst Case | 200-500 | Brand new account, no followers, no momentum. First 2-3 weeks. |
| Expected Case | 800-2,000 | After 2-3 weeks of consistent posting (1-2/day), some reels catch. Average across all reels. |
| Best Case | 5,000-15,000 | 1-2 reels per week go semi-viral in niche. Happens after algorithm recognizes your content pattern. |

**Reality check:** New accounts on Instagram Reels in 2025-2026 face a crowded landscape. The algorithm favors accounts that post consistently (5-7x/week minimum), use trending audio, and generate watch time + saves. B2B content typically gets LOWER views than entertainment/lifestyle content. Contractor/small business content is niche, which helps targeting but hurts raw volume.

**Key assumption:** We're modeling 5 reels per week to start. Some will flop (200 views), some will perform (2K+), a rare one might pop (10K+).

### Stage 2: View-to-DM Conversion (Trigger Word in CTA)

**How it works:** Reel ends with "Comment [TRIGGER WORD] and I'll send you the demo."

| Scenario | View-to-Comment Rate | Comment-to-DM Rate | Effective View-to-DM Rate |
|----------|---------------------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| Worst Case | 0.3% | 60% | 0.18% |
| Expected Case | 0.8% | 75% | 0.60% |
| Best Case | 2.0% | 85% | 1.70% |

**Notes:**
- Average Instagram engagement rate for business accounts: 0.5-1.5% (likes + comments combined)
- Comment-specific rate is a fraction of total engagement
- Trigger word CTAs convert better than "DM me" because the barrier is lower (just type one word)
- ManyChat auto-reply eliminates friction between comment and DM
- B2B service content tends to get lower engagement rates than B2C
- These rates assume the content is actually good, hooks are strong, and the offer is clear

---

### Stage 3: DM-to-Demo-Call Rate

Once someone DMs (via trigger word), they enter an automated sequence that qualifies and books them.

| Scenario | DM-to-Demo-Call Rate | Basis |
|----------|---------------------|-------|
| Worst Case | 10% | Poor qualification sequence, tire-kickers, people just curious |
| Expected Case | 20% | Decent automation with 2-3 qualifying questions, clear value prop |
| Best Case | 35% | Tight sequence, strong social proof in DM, urgency element |

**Notes:**
- DM leads from content are warmer than cold outreach but cooler than referrals
- Many people who comment a trigger word are mildly curious, not buying-ready
- Qualification in DMs is critical to filter out non-contractors, broke prospects, and time-wasters
- Demo call = short 10-15 min Zoom showing the AI system in action

---

### Stage 4: Demo-Call-to-Booking Rate

Demo call leads to booking a full sales call (or closes on the spot for founding members).

| Scenario | Demo-to-Booking Rate | Basis |
|----------|---------------------|-------|
| Worst Case | 25% | Demo doesn't wow them, they ghost, scheduling friction |
| Expected Case | 40% | Demo is solid, clear ROI shown, easy booking link |
| Best Case | 60% | Demo blows their mind, immediate "when can we start?" energy |

---

### Stage 5: Booking-to-Close Rate

Full sales call to signed deal.

| Scenario | Close Rate | Basis |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Worst Case | 15% | No-shows, price objections, "need to think about it" |
| Expected Case | 25% | Solid sales process, good objection handling, social proof |
| Best Case | 40% | Killer closer, strong urgency, proof-heavy presentation |

**Notes:**
- High-ticket B2B close rates (services $2K-$5K) typically range 15-35%
- No-show rate for booked calls: 20-40% (this is baked into the close rate above)
- Founding member pricing ($1,997 one-time) should close easier than standard ($2,500 + $497/mo)
- Close rate improves dramatically with case studies and testimonials (which we don't have yet)

### How Many Reels Per Week to Hit $20K in 30 Days?

| Scenario | Close Rate (end-to-end) | Closes Needed | Reels Needed (30 days) | Reels Per Week |
|----------|------------------------|---------------|----------------------|----------------|
| Worst Case | 0.001% of views | 10 closes at $1,997 | ~150+ reels | 35+/week (impossible organically) |
| Expected Case | 0.012% of views | 9 closes at $2,198 | ~64 reels | ~15/week (aggressive but doable: 2-3/day) |
| Best Case | 0.14% of views | 7 closes at $2,800 avg | ~12 reels | ~3/week |

### REALITY CHECK ON $20K IN 30 DAYS:

**Worst Case: Not happening.** New account, no followers, no social proof, no case studies. You'd need 150+ reels in 30 days AND have them all perform. The math doesn't work.

**Expected Case: Extremely unlikely in first 30 days.** Here's why:
- Week 1-2: Algorithm is still learning your content. Most reels get 200-500 views.
- Week 3-4: Some reels start getting traction IF content is good and consistent.
- First DMs start coming in around week 2-3.
- First demo calls around week 3.
- First close: optimistically week 4-5.
- $20K requires 9 closes. Getting 9 closes from a brand new account in 30 days requires either paid traffic or going viral.

**Best Case: $3K-$8K in first 30 days.** If you post 2/day, nail the content formula early, get 1-2 semi-viral reels, and close well, you might land 2-4 founding members. That's $4K-$8K. Still significant, but not $20K.

**Honest projection for first 30 days:**

| Scenario | Revenue |
|----------|---------|
| Worst Case | $0 (no closes, still building audience) |
| Expected Case | $2,000-$4,000 (1-2 founding member closes) |
| Best Case | $6,000-$10,000 (3-5 closes, one reel went viral-ish) |

## 3. KEY ASSUMPTIONS AND RISKS

### What Must Be True for This to Work:
1. Content quality must be exceptional from day 1 (hooks, editing, value density)
2. Posting frequency of 1-2 reels/day minimum
3. ManyChat or manual DM response within 5 minutes of trigger comment
4. Demo call must be genuinely impressive (working AI system, clear ROI)
5. Sales process must be tight (objection handling, follow-up sequence)

### What Could Kill the Funnel:
1. Instagram shadowban or restricted reach (new accounts sometimes get throttled)
2. Content that looks/sounds like every other "AI agency" pitch on IG
3. Slow DM response time (kills conversion rate)
4. No case studies or testimonials yet (biggest trust deficit)
5. Demo call quality (if the AI product isn't ready to show, this all falls apart)
6. Sales skills (high-ticket closing is a learned skill)

### The Wyatt Roderick Factor:
- Wyatt built his audience over months, not days
- He has social proof, testimonials, and a proven system to demonstrate
- He likely posts 2-3x daily and has been doing it for a while
- Replicating his results in 30 days from zero is not realistic
- What IS realistic: building the same machine and letting it compound over 60-90 days
