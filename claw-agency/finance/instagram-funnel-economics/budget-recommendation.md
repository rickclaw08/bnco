# 30-Day Budget Recommendation
## ClawOps - Morgan (CFO) Analysis
### Date: 2026-03-02

---

## RECOMMENDED 30-DAY BUDGET: $80-$130 TOTAL

We're bootstrapping. Every dollar needs to produce leverage. Here's exactly what to spend and what to skip.

---

## MANDATORY SPEND (Do This Immediately)

### 1. ManyChat Pro - $15/month
**Priority: CRITICAL**
- This is the backbone of the DM funnel
- Automates trigger word detection, DM sequences, qualification
- Without this, you're manually responding to every DM (doesn't scale, kills response time)
- Free tier is too limited for a real funnel
- ROI: One founding member close ($1,997) pays for 11 YEARS of ManyChat

### 2. Calendly Free or Cal.com Free - $0
**Priority: CRITICAL**
- Demo call and sales call booking
- Free tier is fine for our volume
- Eliminates back-and-forth scheduling
- Integrates with ManyChat for direct booking from DM flow

### 3. Zoom Free Tier - $0
**Priority: CRITICAL**
- Demo calls and sales calls
- 40-minute limit on free tier is fine (demo = 15 min, sales call = 30 min)
- Upgrade to Pro ($13/mo) only if call volume exceeds 3-4/day

---

## RECOMMENDED SPEND (High ROI)

### 4. Canva Pro - $13/month
**Priority: HIGH**
- Thumbnail creation, reel text overlays, brand consistency
- Free tier works but Pro gives access to templates, brand kit, background remover
- Skip if budget is truly zero; upgrade when first revenue comes in

### 5. CapCut Pro - $0 (free) or $8/month
**Priority: MEDIUM**
- Video editing for reels
- Free version has everything you need (auto-captions, effects, transitions)
- Pro removes watermarks and adds advanced features
- Start free, upgrade if watermarks become an issue

### 6. Content Research - $0
**Priority: HIGH**
- Study top-performing reels in the AI agency / contractor space
- Free tools: Instagram search, TikTok (for trending formats), YouTube (for content strategy)
- Time investment: 2-3 hours in week 1, then 30 min/day

---

## OPTIONAL SPEND (Nice to Have)

### 7. Loom Pro - $0-13/month
**Priority: LOW**
- For recording personalized video DMs (higher conversion)
- Free tier gives 25 videos, 5 min each
- Only upgrade if personalized video DMs prove to convert significantly better

### 8. Instagram Creator/Business Account Tools - $0
**Priority: MEDIUM**
- Switch to Business or Creator account (free)
- Unlocks analytics, insights, contact button, category label
- No reason not to do this immediately

## WHERE TO INVEST TIME (More Important Than Money)

The real budget is your time. Here's how to allocate it in month 1:

### Week 1: Foundation (25-30 hours)
- Set up ManyChat flows (trigger words, qualification, booking link) - 4 hrs
- Set up Calendly/booking page - 1 hr
- Research 20 top-performing AI agency / contractor reels - 3 hrs
- Script and record first 14 reels (batch 2-3 at a time) - 15 hrs
- Set up Instagram business profile, bio, highlights - 2 hrs
- Prepare demo call script and presentation - 3 hrs

### Week 2: Content Machine (20-25 hours)
- Record and post 14 reels - 14 hrs
- Respond to DMs and engagement (even if manual supplement to ManyChat) - 3 hrs
- Analyze reel performance, adjust hooks and formats - 2 hrs
- Practice demo call and sales call scripts - 3 hrs
- Engage with contractor accounts (comment, follow, build community) - 3 hrs

### Week 3: Optimize and Close (20-25 hours)
- Record and post 14 reels - 12 hrs (faster now with proven formats)
- Run demo calls - 3-5 hrs
- Run sales calls - 2-4 hrs
- Refine ManyChat sequences based on data - 2 hrs
- Create Instagram highlights (testimonials, demos, FAQ) - 2 hrs

### Week 4: Scale What Works (20-25 hours)
- Record and post 14 reels (double down on top-performing format) - 12 hrs
- Demo and sales calls - 5-8 hrs
- Follow up with pipeline (people who didn't close) - 3 hrs
- Plan month 2 content calendar - 2 hrs

**Total time investment in Month 1: 85-105 hours**
That's roughly 3-3.5 hours/day, 7 days a week. Non-trivial but manageable.

---

## FINANCIAL DECISION FRAMEWORK

### When to increase spend:
- After first 2 closes: Add Canva Pro, any quality-of-life tools
- After first 5 closes: Consider $500-1,000 ad test budget
- After first 10 closes: Scale ad spend to $2,000-3,000/mo
- After $10K month: Invest in better equipment, possibly VA for DM management

### When to cut losses:
- If 30 days pass with 56+ reels and zero DMs: Content strategy needs complete overhaul
- If 20+ demo calls produce zero closes: Product or sales process is broken
- If engagement rate stays below 0.3% after 4 weeks: Account may be shadowbanned or content is fundamentally off

### Break-even analysis:
- Monthly costs at $28-80: One founding member close covers 25-71 months of operating costs
- This is an absurdly capital-efficient funnel compared to any paid acquisition channel

---

## FINAL RECOMMENDATION

Spend $15 on ManyChat Pro. Use free tools for everything else. Invest 85-105 hours of focused time. That's the budget.

The bottleneck isn't money. It's content quality, posting consistency, and sales skills. Those are free to develop but require discipline.

If month 1 produces even one close ($1,997), reinvest into Canva Pro and start saving for a month 2 ad test budget. If it produces 3+ closes, you're on track for $10K+ months by month 3.

The Instagram funnel is the right bet. The numbers work. The risk is almost entirely in execution, not capital.
