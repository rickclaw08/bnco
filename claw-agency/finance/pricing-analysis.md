## Value Justification by Tier

### Starter ($500)
- Average workflow automation saves 5-20 hours/month of manual work
- At $30/hr employee cost = $150–$600/mo savings
- **ROI: Pays for itself in 1-3 months, then pure savings**

### Growth ($2,000/mo)
- 2-3 automations saving 20-60 hours/month
- Employee cost equivalent: $600–$1,800/mo
- Plus: ongoing optimization, monitoring, new automations
- **ROI: 1-2x return minimum, improving monthly**

### Enterprise ($5,000–$15,000/mo)
- Custom AI systems (chatbots, data pipelines, process automation)
- Replacing or augmenting 1-3 full-time positions
- Employee equivalent: $15,000–$45,000/mo
- **ROI: 3-5x return vs. hiring**

## Pricing Psychology

1. **Anchor high, deliver value** — Starter at $500 feels accessible vs. $5K+ alternatives
2. **Monthly recurring = predictable revenue** — Growth tier is the bread and butter
3. **Enterprise is custom-quoted** — $5K is the floor, but flex up based on scope
4. **No hourly billing** — Value-based pricing, not time-based. We get faster, we keep the margin.

## Discounting Strategy
- **Annual prepay:** 2 months free (Growth: $20K/yr vs $24K)
- **Referral:** 1 month free for referrer
- **Bundle:** Starter → Growth conversion: waive Starter fee, apply to first month
- **Never discount Enterprise.** Custom = premium.
