# Communication Templates

All templates for client-facing communication during onboarding and delivery.

---

## 1. Welcome Email

**Subject:** Welcome to ClawOps - Let's Build Something Great

---

Hi [Client First Name],

Welcome aboard! We're excited to start working with you on [Project Name].

Here's what happens next:

1. **Kickoff Call** - We'll schedule a 30-minute kickoff within the next 48 hours. You'll receive a calendar invite shortly with the agenda.

2. **What We'll Need From You** - During the kickoff, we'll walk through a short checklist of access, credentials, and context we need to hit the ground running.

3. **Communication** - You'll get weekly status updates every Friday. For day-to-day questions, the fastest way to reach us is [Slack channel / email].

**Your project lead is:** [Project Lead Name] ([email])
**Your point of contact for anything else:** Harper ([email])

If you have any questions before our kickoff, don't hesitate to reach out.

Looking forward to it,

[Project Lead Name]
ClawOps

## 2. Kickoff Call Agenda

**Subject:** Kickoff Call Agenda - [Project Name]

---

Hi [Client First Name],

Looking forward to our kickoff on [Date] at [Time]. Here's what we'll cover:

**Agenda (30 minutes)**

1. Introductions (2 min)
2. Confirm project scope and deliverables (5 min)
3. Walk through the project timeline and milestones (5 min)
4. Review "What We Need From You" checklist (5 min)
5. Set communication expectations and cadence (5 min)
6. Q&A and next steps (8 min)

**Please come prepared with:**
- Access credentials for any platforms we'll be working in
- The name and contact info for your single point of contact (if not you)
- Any examples, references, or inspiration for the project

Talk soon,

[Project Lead Name]
ClawOps
