## AUTOMATION NOTES

**Platform:** Use SendGrid, Postmark, or similar with webhook triggers  
**Variables Required:**
- [First Name]
- [Project Name]
- [PM Name] + Contact Info
- [Timeline / Dates]
- [Milestone Names]
- [Invoice Details]
- [Portal URL]
- [Slack Channel Link]

**Tracking:**
- Open rates
- Click-through rates
- Reply rates
- Deliverability

**A/B Testing:**
- Subject lines
- Send times
- CTA placement
- Personalization level

**Follow-Up Rules:**
- If no reply to milestone delivery within 48 hours, PM manual follow-up
- If invoice unpaid 3 days before due date, automated reminder
- If no response to referral request, no additional follow-up (do not spam)

---

Every email is a touchpoint. Make it count. Be clear, be fast, be human.
