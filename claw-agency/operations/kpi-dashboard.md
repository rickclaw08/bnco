## Definitions

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|-----------|
| **Pipeline Value** | Total dollar value of all open opportunities (proposals sent, in negotiation) |
| **Weighted Pipeline** | Pipeline value multiplied by probability of close (e.g., $10K deal at 50% = $5K weighted) |
| **Conversion Rate** | Proposals won divided by proposals sent, trailing 30-day window |
| **Average Deal Size** | Total revenue divided by number of deals closed, trailing 90-day window |
| **Utilization Rate** | Billable hours divided by total available hours (40 hours/week standard) |
| **First-Pass Quality Rate** | Percentage of deliverables accepted by client without requiring revisions |
| **Budget Adherence** | Actual hours used divided by estimated hours, expressed as percentage |
| **NPS (Net Promoter Score)** | "How likely are you to recommend ClawOps?" on 1-10 scale |
| **Churn Rate** | Clients lost in period divided by total clients at start of period |
| **Revenue Per Client** | Total revenue divided by number of active clients, trailing 90 days |
| **Reuse Rate** | Percentage of new project work that leverages existing templates, code, or workflows |
| **Mean Time to Resolve** | Average time from incident report to resolution, in business hours |
