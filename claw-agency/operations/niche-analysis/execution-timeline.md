## Key Dates to Watch

- **Late May / Early June:** First major heat waves in southern markets. HVAC call volumes spike 3-5x. We MUST have proven product and testimonials before this window. This is our "peak season" too.
- **Late September / October:** Fall shoulder season. Good time for "get ready for winter" messaging push.
- **Late November / December:** Furnace breakdown season. Second major demand spike.

The HVAC business runs on weather. Our marketing calendar runs on weather. Every piece of content, every outreach push, every ad campaign should be timed to these cycles.

---

## Risk Factors

1. **ServiceTitan builds this in-house.** Mitigation: move fast, build HVAC-specific depth they won't match in their v1, and establish brand loyalty before they ship.
2. **Generic AI answering services add HVAC features.** Mitigation: our niche specificity IS the moat. They would need to rebuild their entire product for one vertical. They won't.
3. **Slow beta customer acquisition.** Mitigation: offer first 3 customers free for 30 days. The data and testimonials are worth more than the revenue right now.
4. **HVAC owners are tech-skeptical.** Mitigation: lead with ROI math, not technology. They don't care how AI works. They care that it books calls and makes them money.
