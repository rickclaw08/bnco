# Project Timeline Templates by Tier
**Claw Agency — Operations**
*Created: 2026-02-19*

---

## 🟢 Starter Tier

**Scope:** Essential digital presence — social media management, basic content, light strategy.
**Typical Duration:** Month-to-month, rolling.

| Week | Milestone | Deliverables |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| **1** | Onboarding & Audit | Intake complete, brand assets collected, basic audit done |
| **2** | Strategy & Content Planning | Content calendar (Month 1), tone/voice guide drafted |
| **3** | Production Begins | First batch of content created, scheduled, or posted |
| **4** | Review & Report | Month 1 performance snapshot, adjustments noted |
| **Ongoing** | Monthly Cycle | Content calendar → production → posting → monthly report |

**Cadence:** Weekly async check-in (email/message). Monthly performance report.

---

## 🔵 Growth Tier

**Scope:** Expanded services — social media, paid ads, email marketing, SEO basics, content strategy.
**Typical Duration:** 3–6 month engagements.

| Week | Milestone | Deliverables |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| **1** | Onboarding & Deep Audit | Full intake, competitive analysis, platform audit |
| **2** | Strategy Development | Comprehensive strategy doc, content pillars, ad plan |
| **3** | Content & Campaign Setup | Content calendar (Month 1–2), ad account setup, email sequences drafted |
| **4** | Launch | First campaigns live, content publishing begins |
| **5–8** | Optimize & Scale | A/B testing, audience refinement, SEO implementation |
| **9–12** | Performance Review & Expand | Quarterly report, strategy refresh, upsell discussion |

**Cadence:** Weekly live check-in (15–30 min). Bi-weekly status report. Monthly performance report.

---

## 🟣 Enterprise Tier

**Scope:** Full-service — brand strategy, multi-channel campaigns, PR, advanced analytics, dedicated team.
**Typical Duration:** 6–12 month retainer.

| Week | Milestone | Deliverables |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| **1** | Onboarding & Stakeholder Alignment | Exec kickoff, full intake, access & permissions secured |
| **2** | Comprehensive Audit | Full digital audit (social, SEO, paid, email, website, competitors) |
| **3** | Strategy & Roadmap | Multi-channel strategy doc, 90-day roadmap, KPI dashboard setup |
| **4** | Creative & Campaign Development | Brand refresh elements, campaign concepts, content systems built |
| **5–6** | Phased Launch | Campaigns go live in waves, tracking confirmed |
| **7–12** | Execution & Optimization | Continuous optimization, weekly reporting, monthly strategy calls |
| **13 (Q2 Start)** | Quarterly Strategic Review | Full performance report, roadmap update, budget reallocation |
| **14–26** | Scale & Evolve | New channel expansion, advanced testing, thought leadership |

**Cadence:** Weekly live check-in (30–45 min). Weekly status report. Monthly executive summary. Quarterly strategic review.

---

## General Notes

- All timelines start from **contract signed date**
- Timelines assume client provides assets/access within 48 hours of request
- Delays in client feedback shift downstream milestones accordingly (document in writing)
- Use these as starting templates — customize per client needs
