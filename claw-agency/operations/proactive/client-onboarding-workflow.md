# Client Onboarding Workflow
**Claw Agency — Standard Operating Procedure**
*Created: 2026-02-19*

---

## Phase 1: Contract & Admin Setup (Day 0–1)

- [ ] Signed contract received and filed in client folder
- [ ] Invoice sent for initial payment / deposit
- [ ] Payment confirmed
- [ ] Client added to project management tool (board/workspace created)
- [ ] Client added to communication channel (Slack, Discord, or email thread)
- [ ] Internal team notified of new client + tier level
- [ ] Assign project lead / point of contact

## Phase 2: Discovery & Intake (Day 1–3)

- [ ] Send **Client Intake Questionnaire** (brand info, goals, assets, logins, preferences)
- [ ] Schedule kickoff call
- [ ] Collect all brand assets (logos, fonts, colors, style guides)
- [ ] Collect platform access / credentials (social accounts, hosting, analytics)
- [ ] Review any existing content, campaigns, or prior agency work
- [ ] Document client's KPIs and success metrics
- [ ] Identify key stakeholders and approval contacts

## Phase 3: Strategy & Planning (Day 3–5)

- [ ] Conduct kickoff call — review goals, expectations, timeline, communication cadence
- [ ] Audit current digital presence (website, socials, SEO, ads)
- [ ] Draft initial strategy document based on tier
- [ ] Build project timeline (use tier-specific template)
- [ ] Define deliverables, milestones, and review dates
- [ ] Set recurring check-in schedule (weekly)

## Phase 4: Internal Alignment (Day 5–7)

- [ ] Brief all team members assigned to account
- [ ] Share strategy doc, timeline, and brand assets internally
- [ ] Set up internal task tracking with deadlines
- [ ] Confirm all tools/access are working (analytics, ad accounts, CMS)

## Phase 5: Project Kickoff (Day 7)

- [ ] Send client a **Welcome Packet** summarizing:
  - Point of contact & team
  - Communication plan (how/when we communicate)
  - Timeline overview
  - What to expect in week 1
- [ ] First deliverables enter production
- [ ] First weekly check-in scheduled and confirmed

---

## Post-Kickoff Checklist

- [ ] 30-day check-in: Review progress against KPIs, adjust strategy if needed
- [ ] 60-day review: Performance report, gather client feedback
- [ ] 90-day strategic review: Renewal/upsell conversation prep

---

> **Target: Contract signed → Kickoff in ≤ 7 business days.**
