# Post-Project Review Template

> **After Every Client Engagement**
> **Owner:** ClawOps Operations
> **Last Updated:** 2026-02-23
> **When to Complete:** Within 5 business days of project handoff
> **Time Required:** 30-45 minutes

---

## Overview

Every completed project gets a post-project review. No exceptions. This is how we get better, extract reusable assets, and turn happy clients into referral sources.

Inspired by Amazon's Correction of Error (COE) process and Google's blameless post-mortems, adapted for client services. The goal is not blame; the goal is learning.

## Part 7: Referral Ask

Timing matters. Ask for referrals when satisfaction is highest, which is right after a successful delivery.

### Referral Decision

- [ ] Client scored 9-10 on NPS: Ask for referral now
- [ ] Client scored 7-8 on NPS: Ask for referral after addressing improvement feedback
- [ ] Client scored 6 or below: Do NOT ask. Focus on resolving concerns first.

### Referral Ask Template (for 9-10 NPS clients)

```
Subject: Quick Question

Hi [Client Name],

It's been great working with you on [project]. I'm glad we were able to
[key result/outcome].

If you know anyone who could benefit from similar work, I'd really
appreciate an introduction. We're selectively taking on new clients and
a warm intro goes a long way.

No pressure at all. And if there's ever anything else we can help with
on your end, just reach out.

Thanks again for the partnership.

Best,
[Name]
ClawOps
```

## Part 8: Case Study Extraction

Every successful project is a potential case study for marketing and sales. Extract the story while it's fresh.

### Case Study Qualification

- [ ] Client achieved measurable results
- [ ] Client is willing to be referenced (ask permission)
