## How to Use

1. Pick the right template based on the prospect's industry and the stage
2. Customize every bracket `[like this]` with real information
3. Never send a template without personalizing it
4. Follow the timing: Initial > Day 3 Follow-Up > Day 7 Follow-Up
5. Log every email sent in the Pipeline Tracker

## Rules

- Personalize or don't send. Generic emails get ignored.
- Keep subject lines under 50 characters when possible.
- No attachments on cold emails (triggers spam filters). Save attachments for proposals.
- Send from a warmed-up domain. Start with 10/day and ramp to 25-50/day over 2 weeks.
- Plain text outperforms HTML for cold outreach. Skip the fancy formatting.
- Always include an easy opt-out ("let me know if you'd like me to stop reaching out").
