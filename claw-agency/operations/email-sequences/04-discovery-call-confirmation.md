# Discovery Call Confirmation

**When to send:** Immediately after a lead agrees to a call and a time is booked.
**Tone:** Professional, warm, set expectations.

---

## Template

**Subject:** Confirmed: Our call on [Day], [Date] at [Time]

Hi [First Name],

Great connecting with you. Looking forward to our call.

**Details:**
- Date: [Day, Date]
- Time: [Time] [Time Zone]
- Duration: 15 minutes
- Link: [Zoom/Google Meet link]

Here's what we'll cover:
1. Your current workflow and where the bottlenecks are
2. Quick overview of how we've solved similar problems
3. Whether there's a fit (and if not, I'll tell you honestly)

No prep needed on your end. Just show up and we'll have a good conversation.

If something comes up and you need to reschedule, just reply to this email. No worries at all.

Talk soon,
[Your Name]
ClawOps
