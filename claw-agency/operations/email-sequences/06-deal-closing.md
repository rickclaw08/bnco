# Deal Closing / Payment Link

**When to send:** After the prospect confirms they want to move forward (verbally or via email).
**Tone:** Clear, professional, frictionless. Make paying easy.

---

## Template A: They Said Yes

**Subject:** Let's make it official - agreement + payment link

Hi [First Name],

Awesome, let's do this!

Here's everything you need to get started:

**1. Agreement:** [Link to contract / agreement document]
Please review and sign at your convenience. It covers scope, timeline, and terms we discussed.

**2. Invoice / Payment:** [Payment link - Stripe, PayPal, or wire instructions]
Amount: $[Amount] (50% deposit to kick off)

Once both are complete, we'll schedule the kickoff call and hit the ground running.

**Expected Kickoff:** Within [X] business days of payment
**Estimated Delivery:** [Date or timeframe]

If you have any questions about the agreement, just reply here. Happy to clarify anything.

Excited to build this for you.

[Your Name]
ClawOps

---

## Template B: Nudge After Verbal Yes (2 days, no payment yet)

**Subject:** Quick check-in on getting started

Hi [First Name],

Just following up on our conversation. I sent over the agreement and payment link on [date]. Wanted to make sure everything looked good and there weren't any questions holding things up.

Here are the links again in case they got buried:
- Agreement: [Link]
- Payment: [Link]

We've got a slot opening up [this week / next week] for your project, so getting the deposit in by [date] would let us start on schedule.

Let me know if anything needs adjusting.

[Your Name]

## Template C: Final Nudge (5 days, no payment)

**Subject:** Still want to move forward?

Hi [First Name],

I know things get busy. Just wanted to check: are you still looking to move forward with the [project name] project?

If so, I'm holding a spot for [Company Name] through [date]. After that I'll need to open it up to other clients.

No pressure either way. Just let me know where your head's at and I'll work with whatever timeline makes sense.

[Your Name]

---

## Payment Options to Offer

- **Stripe invoice** (credit card, ACH) - lowest friction
- **PayPal invoice** - widely trusted
- **Wire transfer** - for larger deals ($5K+)
- **Crypto** - only if client requests it

Always send a proper invoice with line items, not just a payment link.
