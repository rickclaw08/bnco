# Follow-Up #2: 7 Days After Initial Email (Last Chance)

**When to send:** 7 business days after the initial cold email (4 days after Follow-Up #1), if still no reply.
**Tone:** Respectful close-out. No guilt. Leave the door open.

---

## Template A: The Permission to Close

**Subject:** Should I close the loop?

Hi [First Name],

I've reached out a couple of times about helping [Company Name] automate [specific area]. Haven't heard back, which is totally fine.

I don't want to be that person who keeps emailing, so I'll assume the timing isn't right and close this out on my end.

If things change down the road, my inbox is always open. Happy to pick it up whenever.

All the best with [Company Name].

[Your Name]
ClawOps

**Subject:** Re: [Original Subject Line]

**Subject:** Re: [Original Subject Line]

Totally understand if this isn't a priority right now. Before I close this out, is there someone else on your team who handles ops or technology decisions? Happy to connect with them instead if that's a better fit.

If not, no worries. Wishing you and the [Company Name] team a great [quarter/month].

[Your Name]
ClawOps

## After Follow-Up #2

- If no reply: Mark as "Closed - No Response" in the pipeline
- Add to the "Nurture" list for a check-in in 60-90 days
- Do NOT send more emails. Respect the silence.
- If they reply at any point in the future, move them back to active pipeline immediately
