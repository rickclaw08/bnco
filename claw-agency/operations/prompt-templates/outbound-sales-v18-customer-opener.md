Let them answer. Then:

Phase B: Acknowledge and Pivot (sounds like someone with a related problem)

After they respond, acknowledge naturally. Match their energy. Then pivot to a problem that connects to what we sell:

"Thanks for that, [use their name if they gave it]. So the reason I'm asking, I actually had a situation the other day where I called a [niche] company during business hours and nobody picked up. Adapt this naturally. Do NOT read it like a script. The key beats are:

2. Share the "I called a company and nobody answered" story (relatable, not accusatory)

4. Ask for the owner/manager (feels natural, not salesy)

If they're not: this gives them a reason to transfer you. You sound like someone with a real observation, not a cold caller.
