## Contract Essentials

Use DocuSign or PandaDoc with these key terms:

### Must-Have Clauses

1. **Scope of Work**: Reference the proposal, list deliverables
2. **Timeline**: Start date, milestone dates, delivery date
3. **Pricing and Payment**: Amount, schedule, late payment terms
4. **Intellectual Property**: Client owns all deliverables upon final payment
5. **Confidentiality**: Both parties protect each other's confidential information
6. **Revisions**: [X] rounds of revisions included, additional at hourly rate
7. **Termination**: Either party can terminate with 14 days written notice; client pays for work completed
8. **Liability**: Limited to total contract value
9. **Support**: 14 days post-delivery bug-fix support included
10. **Independent Contractor**: ClawOps operates as an independent contractor, not an employee
