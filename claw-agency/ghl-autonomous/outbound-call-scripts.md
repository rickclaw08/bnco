# Outbound Call Scripts - Niche-Specific

> ClawOps Autonomous Outbound Campaign
> These scripts are for AI Voice agents making OUTBOUND calls to leads.
> Different from inbound receptionist mode - these are proactive sales calls.
> Tone: Friendly, consultative, NOT pushy. We're offering a free demo, not hard-selling.

---

## Universal Outbound Framework

Every outbound call follows this structure:

1. **Opener** (10 sec) - Identify yourself, state the company name, ask if it's a good time
2. **Hook** (15 sec) - Niche-specific pain point that resonates immediately
3. **Bridge** (10 sec) - Connect pain to solution (Voice AI receptionist)
4. **Proof** (15 sec) - Quick credibility / what other companies in their niche are doing
5. **CTA** (10 sec) - Book a 30-min demo call, or offer to call back at a better time
6. **Fallback** - If not interested, be gracious. If voicemail, leave a tight message.

**Key Rule:** This call IS the demo. The AI calling them demonstrates the product. If they engage in conversation, point that out: "By the way, you're talking to our AI right now. This is exactly what your callers would experience."

## Outbound Call Rules

1. **Never call before 9 AM or after 6 PM** in the lead's local time zone
2. **Best calling windows:** Tue-Thu, 10 AM - 12 PM and 2 PM - 4 PM local time
3. **Max 3 call attempts** per lead before moving to email/SMS follow-up
4. **Spacing:** Attempt 1 on Day 1, Attempt 2 on Day 3, Attempt 3 on Day 6
5. **Always leave voicemail** on attempts 2 and 3 (skip on attempt 1 - call back looks more natural)
6. **If they answer and say "not interested":** Thank them, don't push, mark as "Declined" in CRM
7. **If they answer and show interest but can't talk:** Book a specific callback time, don't leave it vague
8. **Post-call:** Automatically update contact record with call outcome, notes, and next action
