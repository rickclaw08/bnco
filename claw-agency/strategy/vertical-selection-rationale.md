# ClawOps Vertical Selection: HOME SERVICES
**Date:** February 24, 2026  
**Decision:** Go all-in on Home Services (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Roofing, Landscaping, Junk Removal)

---

## Why Home Services Over Legal and Healthcare

### The Data (from Atlas's report)

**Home Services wins on every axis that matters for ClawOps right now:**

| Factor | Home Services | Legal | Healthcare |
|--------|--------------|-------|------------|
| **Pain urgency** | 10/10 - Losing $5K-$50K/mo to missed calls RIGHT NOW | 8/10 - Need triage but can hire paralegals | 7/10 - No-shows hurt but manageable |
| **Decision speed** | Fast - Owner decides alone, no committee | Medium - Managing partner, sometimes board | Slow - Compliance, HIPAA, committee decisions |
| **Technical sophistication** | Low - They want it to "just work" | Medium - Some tech-savvy firms | High - EMR integration requirements |
| **Regulatory burden** | Almost none | Moderate (bar association rules, confidentiality) | Heavy (HIPAA, patient data, consent requirements) |
| **Willingness to pay** | High - Already spending $3K-$10K/mo on answering services (Ruby, Smith.ai) | High - But expects more sophistication | Medium - Budget-conscious, insurance-dependent |
| **TAM (reachable SMBs)** | Massive - 3M+ home service businesses in the US | Moderate - ~400K law firms, most are small | Large but complex - compliance gates reduce TAM |
| **Sales cycle** | 1-2 weeks | 2-4 weeks | 4-8 weeks (compliance review) |
| **Case study buildability** | Easy - Clear before/after metrics (missed calls → captured leads) | Moderate | Hard - HIPAA limits what you can share |
| **Competition from incumbents** | Weak - Smith.ai and Ruby are expensive and not AI-native | Moderate - Clio, Lawmatics exist | Strong - Established health tech vendors |
| **Repeat/referral potential** | Extremely high - Contractors talk to each other constantly | Moderate - Bar association networks | Low - Siloed practices |

### The Killer Argument

Home service business owners are:
1. **Non-technical** - They don't want to build it themselves. They NEED us.
2. **Losing money every day** they don't solve this. Every missed call during a job is a lost $500-$5,000 job.
3. **Already paying $3K-$10K/mo** for inferior human answering services. We undercut that AND perform better.
4. **Easy to find** - They're on Google Maps, Yelp, HomeAdvisor, Angi. No LinkedIn detective work needed.
5. **Decision-makers are owners** - One person says yes. No procurement, no legal review, no committee.
6. **Talk to each other** - Plumbers know electricians. HVAC guys know roofers. One happy client = 3 referrals.

### Why NOT Legal

Legal is the second-best option, but:
- Higher sophistication expectations (they'll grill you on confidentiality, data handling)
- Slower decision cycles (managing partner needs to "discuss with the partners")
- The $5,400 Australian deal from the report is real, but it required Salesforce + WhatsApp + complex logic. That's a custom build, not a productized service.
- HIPAA-adjacent concerns around client data

### Why NOT Healthcare

Healthcare is the worst fit for us right now:
- HIPAA compliance is a real barrier. One mistake and you're liable.
- Integration with EMR/EHR systems (Epic, Cerner, Athena) requires certifications and partnerships we don't have.
- Sales cycle is 2-4x longer than home services.
- We'd need a compliance officer or lawyer review before shipping anything.

### The Play

We become "the AI company for contractors." Every home service business owner within 60 days should hear about ClawOps through cold email, referral, or content. We own that niche.

**Target profile:**
- Home service business (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping, junk removal, pest control, cleaning)
- Revenue: $500K-$10M/year
- 3-50 employees
- Owner-operated or small management team
- Currently using: pen and paper, basic CRM (ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro), or nothing
- Pain: Missing calls on job sites, slow follow-up, no review generation, manual scheduling

---

## Bottom Line

Home services has the shortest path to revenue, the simplest sales process, the highest referral velocity, and the clearest ROI pitch. We can build ONE productized solution (AI Receptionist + Lead Capture) and sell it 100 times with minimal customization. That's how you scale an agency into a machine.
