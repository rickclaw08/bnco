## Pricing Psychology (Updated)

1. **Lead with the Audit** - $500 is low enough to be an impulse buy for a business owner, high enough to filter out time-wasters
2. **AI Receptionist is the wedge** - Monthly recurring revenue, obvious ROI, easy to say yes to
3. **Sprints are the expansion play** - Once they trust us, we rebuild their operations
4. **Retainer is the lock-in** - Sticky revenue. Once their systems depend on us, switching cost is high.
5. **Custom AI is the upmarket play** - For when a client says "can you build something that doesn't exist yet?"
6. **Always frame as ROI, never as cost** - "This costs $300/month" is wrong. "This captures $8,000/month in leads you're currently losing" is right.
