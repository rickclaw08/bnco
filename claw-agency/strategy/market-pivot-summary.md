## Revenue Model

**Path to $50K MRR by Q4 2026:**

The AI Receptionist is the engine. Each client = $300-$500/mo recurring. Stack 60 clients = $18K-$30K MRR from recurring alone. Add sprints and retainers on top.

| Month | Receptionist Clients | MRR (Receptionist) | Sprint Revenue | Total Monthly |
|-------|---------------------|--------------------:|---------------:|--------------:|
| Mar 2026 | 2-3 | $600-$1,500 | $2,000-$3,000 | $2,600-$4,500 |
| Apr 2026 | 5-8 | $1,500-$4,000 | $5,000-$7,500 | $6,500-$11,500 |
| May 2026 | 10-15 | $3,000-$7,500 | $7,500-$15,000 | $10,500-$22,500 |
| Jun 2026 | 18-25 | $5,400-$12,500 | $10,000-$15,000 | $15,400-$27,500 |

Each closed Receptionist deal also unlocks:
- Revenue Ops Sprint upsell (60% of clients upgrade within 90 days)
- Automation-as-a-Service retainer (30% convert within 6 months)
- Referrals (contractors talk to each other - target 1 referral per happy client)

## Key Insight from Atlas's Research

The market for "AI automation" is flooded. But the market for "AI receptionist for plumbers" is wide open. We're not competing with 10,000 YouTube-course graduates offering generic automation. We're solving a specific, painful, expensive problem for a specific type of business owner. That's how you win.
