# Brand Guidelines

## Version: 0.1.0

## Brand Overview

**Company:** ClawOps
**What we do:** Build AI agent systems for businesses. Practical automation that works today.
**Who we serve:** Small to mid-size businesses, agencies, and consulting firms that want to automate operations without hiring a full engineering team.
**Our promise:** We build AI agents that actually do the work, not just chatbots that answer questions.

## Brand Personality

### We Are
- **Builders.** We make things that work
- **Pragmatic.** We care about results, not buzzwords
- **Direct.** We say what we mean
- **Honest.** We admit what we do not know
- **Technical.** We understand the tech deeply
- **Approachable.** We explain things clearly

### We Are Not
- Hype machines
- Corporate robots
- Vaporware peddlers
- Jargon factories
- Condescending experts

## Voice Guidelines

See content-creation/references/brand-voice.md for the complete voice guide.

### Quick Rules
1. No em dashes. Ever
2. No corporate jargon
3. Active voice preferred
4. Be specific, not vague
5. Sound like a person, not a brand
6. Keep sentences under 25 words

## Messaging Framework

### Primary Message
"We build AI agents that run your business operations, so you can focus on the work that matters."

### Supporting Messages
- "Practical AI automation for small teams."
- "From manual workflows to automated systems in weeks, not months."
- "AI agents that do the work, not just answer questions."
- "Built by people who understand both the tech and the business."

### Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
"ClawOps builds AI agents for businesses. Not chatbots. Actual agents that handle client onboarding, reporting, outreach, and operations. We work with small teams that want automation without the overhead of building it themselves. Most clients go from manual processes to automated workflows in 2-4 weeks."

## Differentiation

### What Makes Us Different
1. **We build agents, not chatbots.** Our systems take action, not just respond to prompts
2. **We focus on operations.** Client onboarding, reporting, outreach, project management
3. **We are small and fast.** No enterprise sales cycles. Start in days, not months
4. **We are honest about limitations.** AI is powerful but not magic. We set realistic expectations

### Competitor Positioning
- vs. Big consulting firms: We are faster, cheaper, and more hands-on
- vs. DIY tools: We handle the complexity so you do not have to
- vs. Other AI agencies: We focus on operations, not just marketing or content

## Content Guidelines

### Topics We Cover
- AI agents and automation (practical, not theoretical)
- Business operations and efficiency
- Small business technology
- Build logs and technical deep-dives
- Lessons learned from real projects
- Industry trends (with our pragmatic take)

### Topics We Avoid
- AI hype and predictions about AGI
- Political or controversial topics
- Competitor bashing
- Anything we cannot back up with evidence

## Approval Process

1. All public content must pass brand voice check (see brand-management skill)
2. Blog posts: reviewed before publishing
3. Social posts: reviewed before posting (batch review weekly is fine)
4. Client communications: use templates, review if custom
5. Press or media: always get approval before responding
