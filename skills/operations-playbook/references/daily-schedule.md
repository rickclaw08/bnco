# Daily Schedule

## Version: 0.1.0

## Standard Daily Schedule

This is the default daily structure. Adjust based on meetings, deadlines, and priorities.

---

### Morning Block: 8:00 - 10:00 AM

| Time | Activity | Duration | Notes |
|------|----------|----------|-------|
| 8:00 | Communication review | 15 min | Email, Slack, Discord, client messages |
| 8:15 | Daily standup prep | 10 min | Review yesterday, identify blockers |
| 8:25 | Outreach block 1 | 30 min | Reddit/Discord: find and respond to threads |
| 8:55 | Daily planning | 15 min | Prioritize tasks, block time, check calendar |
| 9:10 | Client work | 50 min | Highest priority deliverable |

### Midday Block: 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM

| Time | Activity | Duration | Notes |
|------|----------|----------|-------|
| 10:00 | Client work | 120 min | Focused execution on deliverables |
| 12:00 | Outreach block 2 | 30 min | Post content, engage with replies |
| 12:30 | Content check | 15 min | Review content pipeline, coordinate marketing |
| 12:45 | Lunch / break | 15 min | Step away from screen |

### Afternoon Block: 1:00 - 5:00 PM

| Time | Activity | Duration | Notes |
|------|----------|----------|-------|
| 1:00 | Deep work | 120 min | Most important task, no interruptions |
| 3:00 | Follow-ups | 30 min | Reply to messages, check delegated tasks |
| 3:30 | Outreach block 3 | 30 min | Check replies, engage new threads |
| 4:00 | Admin and docs | 30 min | Update tracking, document decisions |
| 4:30 | End of day wrap | 30 min | Log activities, prep tomorrow |

### Evening Block: 5:00 - 7:00 PM (Optional)

| Time | Activity | Duration | Notes |
|------|----------|----------|-------|
| 5:00 | Light outreach | 15-30 min | Quick scroll, reply to engagement |
| 5:30 | Wrap-up | 15 min | Final messages, update memory file |

## Priority Framework

When choosing what to work on:

1. **P0 - Urgent and Important:** Client blockers, system outages, deadline-day deliverables
2. **P1 - Important:** Client deliverables, active project work, time-sensitive responses
3. **P2 - Scheduled:** Outreach, content creation, status reports, meetings
4. **P3 - Maintenance:** Documentation, internal process improvements, learning

If unsure, ask: "Will someone be waiting on this tomorrow morning?" If yes, it is P1 or higher.

## Meeting Days

- **Monday:** Planning, sprint starts
- **Wednesday:** Midweek check-ins
- **Friday:** Status reports, retrospectives
- **Batch meetings** on 2-3 days to protect deep work on other days
