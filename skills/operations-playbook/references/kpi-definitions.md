# KPI Definitions

## Version: 0.1.0

## How to Use

Track these KPIs weekly. Review trends monthly. Adjust targets quarterly based on growth stage.

---

## Marketing KPIs

### Reddit Karma Growth
- **Definition:** Net change in Reddit karma for the RickClaw_Dev account over the reporting period
- **Measurement:** Check profile karma at start and end of week
- **Target:** +50 karma per week (Phase 1), +100 per week (Phase 2+)
- **Why it matters:** Indicates community engagement and credibility building

### Reddit Engagement
- **Definition:** Total upvotes and comments received on our posts and comments
- **Measurement:** Manual tracking via outreach log
- **Target:** Average 5+ upvotes per comment, 10+ per post
- **Why it matters:** Higher engagement means better content quality and community fit

### Content Published
- **Definition:** Total content pieces published across all platforms
- **Measurement:** Count from content calendar
- **Targets:**
  - Blog posts: 2 per month
  - Social posts: 3-5 per week
  - Newsletter: 2 per month
  - Reddit posts/comments: 8-15 per week
- **Why it matters:** Consistent publishing builds audience and SEO authority

### Inbound Leads
- **Definition:** Number of people who reach out to ClawOps expressing interest in services
- **Measurement:** Count from email, DMs, form submissions, referrals
- **Target:** 2-5 per month (early stage), 10+ per month (growth stage)
- **Why it matters:** Direct measure of marketing effectiveness

## Sales KPIs

### Leads in Pipeline
- **Definition:** Total number of active leads at any stage of the sales process
- **Measurement:** Count from pipeline tracker
- **Target:** 5-10 active leads at any time
- **Why it matters:** Pipeline health predicts future revenue

### Proposals Sent
- **Definition:** Number of formal proposals or quotes sent to prospects
- **Measurement:** Count from proposal tracker
- **Target:** 2-4 per month
- **Why it matters:** Conversion from lead to proposal indicates qualification quality

### Deals Closed
- **Definition:** Number of signed contracts in the reporting period
- **Measurement:** Count from contract tracker
- **Target:** 1-2 per month (early stage)
- **Why it matters:** Direct revenue generation

### Revenue Booked
- **Definition:** Total contract value of deals closed in the reporting period
- **Measurement:** Sum of signed contract values
- **Target:** Varies by stage. Set based on operating costs + growth goals
- **Why it matters:** Financial health indicator

### Average Deal Size
- **Definition:** Mean contract value across all closed deals
- **Measurement:** Total revenue / number of deals
- **Target:** Trending upward over time
- **Why it matters:** Indicates if we are attracting larger, more valuable projects

## Delivery KPIs

### Active Projects
- **Definition:** Number of client projects currently in progress
- **Measurement:** Count from project tracker
- **Target:** 2-4 concurrent (based on team size)
- **Why it matters:** Workload management

### Projects On Track
- **Definition:** Percentage of active projects with green status (on time, on budget)
- **Measurement:** (Green projects / Total projects) x 100
- **Target:** 80%+ green at all times
- **Why it matters:** Delivery quality and client satisfaction

### Sprint Velocity
- **Definition:** Number of work items completed per sprint
- **Measurement:** Count from sprint tracker
- **Target:** Consistent or improving week over week
- **Why it matters:** Predictability of delivery

### On-Time Delivery Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage of milestones delivered on or before the planned date
- **Measurement:** (On-time milestones / Total milestones) x 100
- **Target:** 90%+
- **Why it matters:** Client trust and reputation

### Client Satisfaction
- **Definition:** Qualitative assessment of client happiness based on feedback
- **Measurement:** Feedback from meetings, emails, surveys
- **Scale:** Happy / Neutral / Concerned
- **Target:** 100% Happy or Neutral
- **Why it matters:** Retention, referrals, reputation

---

## Operations KPIs

### Team Utilization
- **Definition:** Percentage of available hours spent on billable client work
- **Measurement:** (Billable hours / Available hours) x 100
- **Target:** 70-85%
- **Why it matters:** Below 50% = underutilized. Above 85% = burnout risk

### Response Time
- **Definition:** Average time to respond to client messages during business hours
- **Measurement:** Track from message receipt to first reply
- **Target:** Under 4 hours for standard messages, under 1 hour for urgent
- **Why it matters:** Client experience and trust

### Documentation Completeness
- **Definition:** Percentage of active projects with up-to-date documentation
- **Measurement:** Audit quarterly
- **Target:** 100%
- **Why it matters:** Knowledge management and handoff quality

---

## Tracking Cadence

| Frequency | What to Track |
|-----------|--------------|
| Daily | Outreach activity, client messages, blockers |
| Weekly | All KPIs above, status reports |
| Monthly | Trends, target adjustments, strategy review |
| Quarterly | Full KPI review, target recalibration, strategy pivot if needed |
