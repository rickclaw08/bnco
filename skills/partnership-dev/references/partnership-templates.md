# Partnership Templates

Outreach and proposal templates for different partnership types.

---

## Template 1: Initial Outreach (Referral Partnership)

```
Subject: Partnership idea - [Their Company] + RickClaw AI

Hi [Name],

I run RickClaw AI - we build AI automations and custom AI solutions
for businesses. I've been following [Their Company]'s work in
[their space] and think there's a natural partnership opportunity.

Here's what I'm thinking:

Your clients likely need [specific AI capability relevant to their
clients]. That's exactly what we build. Rather than you turning
those requests away or trying to build AI capabilities in-house,
we could handle that work and pay you a referral commission for
every client you send our way.

The structure is simple:
- You introduce us to clients who need AI/automation help
- We handle the sales, delivery, and support
- You earn [10-15%] commission on every deal that closes
- Zero work on your end beyond the introduction

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to explore this?

Best,
[Name]
RickClaw AI
```

## Template 3: Initial Outreach (White-Label Partnership)

```
Subject: Add AI services to [Their Company]'s offering

Hi [Name],

What if [Their Company] could offer AI automation services to your
clients without hiring an AI team?

I run RickClaw AI, and we partner with agencies like yours to provide
white-label AI development. Here's how it works:

1. You sell AI services under your brand
2. We build and deliver behind the scenes
3. You keep 30-40% margin on every project
4. Your clients see it as YOUR capability

This means you can:
- Say "yes" to AI requests instead of referring them out
- Add a new revenue stream with zero hiring
- Differentiate from competitors who can't offer AI

We handle everything technical. You handle the client relationship.

Worth a conversation? I'm available [suggest 2-3 times].

Best,
[Name]
RickClaw AI
```

## Template 4: Partnership Proposal (After Initial Interest)

```
PARTNERSHIP PROPOSAL
[Their Company] + RickClaw AI

Date: [Date]

---

THE OPPORTUNITY

[Their Company] and RickClaw AI serve overlapping clients with
complementary capabilities. By partnering, we can:

1. [Benefit 1 - e.g., "Offer complete digital + AI solutions"]
2. [Benefit 2 - e.g., "Generate new revenue from existing clients"]
3. [Benefit 3 - e.g., "Win larger deals through combined expertise"]

PARTNERSHIP STRUCTURE

Type: [Referral / Co-Selling / White-Label]

How It Works:
1. [Step 1 - how referrals or projects flow]
2. [Step 2 - how we collaborate on delivery]
3. [Step 3 - how revenue is shared]

FINANCIAL TERMS

[For Referral:]
- Commission: [X]% on referred deals
- Payment: Within 15 days of deal close
- Attribution: 90-day window from introduction

[For Co-Selling:]
- Revenue split: [X]% [Their Company] / [Y]% RickClaw AI
- Based on: Who brings the client and delivery responsibilities
- Payment: Per project, upon client payment

[For White-Label:]
- Wholesale pricing: [X]% below retail
- Your margin: [Y]%
- Volume discounts available for 3+ projects/quarter

WHAT WE PROVIDE
- Full AI development and delivery
- Priority support for partner-referred clients
- Partner collateral (one-pager, talking points)
- Monthly partnership check-ins
- Performance reporting

WHAT WE NEED FROM YOU
- Introductions to clients who need AI services
- Client context when referring (their problem, budget range)
- Feedback on our service quality
- [For white-label: Client communication and project management]

TRIAL PERIOD

Let's start with a 3-month trial:
- No exclusivity, no long-term commitment
- Evaluate results after 3 months
- Formalize or adjust based on outcomes

NEXT STEPS
1. Review this proposal
2. 30-minute call to align on details
3. Agree on trial terms
4. Start referring / collaborating!

---
RickClaw AI
[contact info]
```

## Tips for Partnership Outreach

1. **Research first** - know their business before reaching out
2. **Lead with THEIR benefit** - what do they gain?
3. **Keep it simple** - complex partnerships don't get started
4. **Start small** - trial period before commitment
5. **Be specific** - vague pitches get ignored
6. **Follow up once** - don't badger potential partners
7. **Track everything** - referrals, commissions, communications
