# Referral Structure

Commission structures, payment terms, and program rules for RickClaw AI's referral program.

---

## Referral Commission Table

| Referred Deal Tier | Deal Value Range | Commission Rate | Commission Range | Payment Timing |
|-------------------|-----------------|-----------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Starter | $500 - $1,500 | 10% | $50 - $150 | On payment receipt |
| Professional | $2,500 - $7,500 | 15% | $375 - $1,125 | On payment receipt |
| Enterprise (retainer) | $10K - $25K/mo | 10% of first 3 months | $3,000 - $7,500 | Monthly for 3 months |
| Custom | $25K+ | 10% of initial project | $2,500+ | On first payment |

---

## Commission Tiers for Volume Partners

Partners who send multiple referrals get upgraded rates:

| Referrals Per Quarter | Starter | Professional | Enterprise | Custom |
|----------------------|---------|-------------|-----------|--------|
| 1-2 referrals | 10% | 15% | 10% (3 mo) | 10% |
| 3-5 referrals | 12% | 17% | 12% (3 mo) | 12% |
| 6+ referrals | 15% | 20% | 15% (3 mo) | 15% |

Volume tiers reset quarterly. Commission rate applies to all referrals in that quarter once the tier is reached (retroactive within the quarter).

## Referral Attribution Rules

### How Referrals Are Tracked

1. **Direct introduction:** Partner emails/messages an introduction. We log the referral.
2. **Referral code/link:** Partner shares a unique code or tracked link.
3. **Client mentions partner:** New client says "[Partner] sent me." We verify and log.

### Attribution Window

- Referral credit is valid for **90 days** from introduction
- If the client signs within 90 days, the partner gets commission
- If the client signs after 90 days, no commission (unless re-referred)

### Disputes

- If attribution is unclear, we ask the client who referred them
- In ambiguous cases, the first verifiable referrer gets credit
- Partner disputes are resolved within 7 business days

## Payment Terms

### Payment Method
- Stripe payout (preferred)
- PayPal
- Wire transfer (for commissions over $1,000)
- Check (last resort)

### Payment Schedule
- Commissions calculated at end of each month
- Payment issued within 15 business days of month close
- Minimum payout: $50 (smaller amounts roll to next month)

### Clawback Policy
- If a referred client requests a refund within 30 days, commission is reversed
- If a referred enterprise client cancels within the first month, commission for that month is reversed
- Clawbacks are communicated immediately and deducted from next payout

## Referral Program Rules

### Partner Obligations
1. Refer only leads that are a genuine fit for our services
2. Do not make promises about pricing, features, or timelines on our behalf
3. Disclose the referral relationship to the prospect if asked
4. Do not engage in spam or aggressive tactics using our name

### Our Obligations
1. Treat all referred leads with priority
2. Communicate referral status to partners (qualified, proposal sent, won/lost)
3. Pay commissions on time per the schedule above
4. Provide partners with up-to-date service descriptions and collateral

### Exclusions
- Self-referrals do not earn commission
- Existing clients in our pipeline cannot be "referred" for commission
- Referrals from employees or team members follow a different program
- One referral fee per client per 12-month period
