# Contract Template

Standard service agreement for RickClaw AI engagements.

---

## Simple Service Agreement

```
SERVICE AGREEMENT

Between: RickClaw AI ("Provider")
And: [Client Name / Company] ("Client")
Date: [Date]
Project: [Project Name]

---

1. SCOPE OF WORK

Provider agrees to deliver the following:

[Paste deliverables from proposal]

- Deliverable 1: [Description]
- Deliverable 2: [Description]
- Deliverable 3: [Description]

Out of scope (unless separately agreed):
- [Exclusion 1]
- [Exclusion 2]

2. TIMELINE

- Start date: [Date]
- Estimated completion: [Date]
- Milestones:
  - [Milestone 1]: [Date]
  - [Milestone 2]: [Date]
  - [Final delivery]: [Date]

3. PAYMENT

Total fee: $[Amount]

Payment schedule:
- [Payment 1]: $[Amount] due [When]
- [Payment 2]: $[Amount] due [When]

Payment method: Stripe (link provided separately)
Late payment: Invoices unpaid after 15 days incur 1.5% monthly interest

4. REVISIONS

- Included revisions: [1-2 rounds, per tier]
- Additional revisions: Billed at $150/hour
- Revision requests must be submitted within 14 days of delivery

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

- All custom work product becomes Client's property upon full payment
- Provider retains the right to use general techniques and methodologies
- Provider may reference the project type (not client name) in portfolio
  unless Client grants written permission

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary information
shared during this engagement. This includes but is not limited to:
business data, technical specifications, customer information,
and financial details.

7. SUPPORT

- Support period: [30/60 days] from final delivery
- Support includes: Bug fixes, minor adjustments
- Support excludes: New features, scope changes, platform changes
- Response time: Within [24/48] hours on business days

8. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate with [7/14/30] days written notice.

Upon termination:
- Client pays for all work completed to date
- Provider delivers all completed work product
- Unused prepayment is refunded pro-rata

9. LIABILITY

Provider's total liability is limited to the total fees paid under
this agreement. Provider is not liable for indirect, consequential,
or lost-profit damages.

10. AGREEMENT

By replying "I agree" via email or signing below, both parties
accept these terms.

Provider: RickClaw AI
Signature: ___________________________
Date: _______________

Client: [Name]
Signature: ___________________________
Date: _______________
```

## Usage Notes

### When to Use Full Contract vs Email Confirmation

| Tier | Agreement Type |
|------|---------------|
| Starter ($500-$1,500) | Email confirmation is sufficient ("Reply 'confirmed' to proceed") |
| Professional ($2,500-$7,500) | This service agreement (email "I agree" is fine) |
| Enterprise ($10K+/mo) | This agreement + SLA addendum |
| Custom ($25K+) | This agreement + IP addendum + SLA + detailed scope document |

### SLA Addendum (Enterprise/Custom)

Add this section for Enterprise and Custom tiers:

```
SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT ADDENDUM

Response Times:
- Critical issues: 4-hour response
- Standard issues: 24-hour response
- General inquiries: 48-hour response

Availability:
- Business hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM ET
- Emergency support: Available via [channel]

Uptime (if applicable):
- Target: 99.5% monthly uptime for hosted solutions
- Scheduled maintenance: Communicated 48 hours in advance
```

### IP Addendum (Custom Tier)

Add for Custom tier when IP ownership needs clarity:

```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ADDENDUM

1. Custom Code: Ownership transfers to Client upon full payment
2. Pre-existing IP: Provider retains ownership of tools, libraries,
   and frameworks developed prior to this engagement
3. Open Source: Any open-source components used remain under their
   respective licenses
4. AI Models: Fine-tuned model weights are [Client property / Licensed]
5. Data: Client retains full ownership of all their data at all times
```
