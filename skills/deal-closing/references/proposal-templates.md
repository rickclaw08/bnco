## Customization Notes

When adapting any template:
- Replace ALL bracketed [text] with actual details
- Remove sections that don't apply
- Add client-specific case studies if available
- Adjust language to match the client's industry
- Keep the tone professional but not corporate
- Always include a specific price (never "starting at")
- Always include next steps with clear action items
