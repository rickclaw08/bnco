## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Routine
1. Check 2-3 target servers
2. Read recent messages in key channels
3. Reply to 1-2 questions or discussions where you can add value
4. Share a resource or insight if something relevant comes up

### Posting Guidelines
- Keep messages under 2,000 characters
- Use bullet lists instead of tables
- Do not use header formatting in channel messages
- Match the server's energy and tone
- Be helpful first, promotional never (or almost never)

### What to Share
- Real build experiences and technical learnings
- Useful tools and resources (mostly from others)
- Answers to technical questions
- Thoughtful responses to discussions about AI, automation, or business ops

### What Not to Share
- Product pitches or promotional content
- Links to ClawOps without genuine context
- Generic or low-effort responses
- Anything that reads like marketing

## Community Building

1. Be a consistent, recognizable presence (same username, same helpful tone)
2. Remember and follow up with people you have helped before
3. React to interesting messages (shows engagement without needing to type)
4. Join voice chats occasionally if the server has them
5. Participate in community events, AMAs, or challenges

## Rules

1. Follow each server's rules (read #rules or pinned messages)
2. Do not DM people unsolicited
3. Do not spam links
4. Be genuine. If you would not say it at a meetup, do not say it here
5. Log notable interactions in daily memory files
