# Follow-Up Sequences

Structured follow-up sequences for each pipeline situation.

---

## Sequence 1: After Initial Outreach (No Response)

### Follow-Up 1 (Day 2-3)

```
Hi [Name],

Wanted to make sure my message came through. I'm still interested in
helping with [specific problem from original message].

Happy to answer any questions or jump on a quick call whenever works.

Best,
[Name]
```

### Follow-Up 2 (Day 5-7)

```
Hi [Name],

Quick thought on [their problem]: [One specific insight or tip that
demonstrates expertise, even if they never respond].

If [their project] is still on your radar, I'd love to chat. If the
timing's not right, no worries at all.

Best,
[Name]
```

### Follow-Up 3 (Day 10-14)

```
Hi [Name],

Last note from me. I know [their role/industry] keeps you busy.

If you need help with [their problem] down the road, feel free to
reach out anytime. I'll be here.

All the best with [Company]!

[Name]
```

After Follow-Up 3 with no response: Move to Lost (Ghosted).

## Sequence 2: After Proposal Sent

### Follow-Up 1 (Day 3)

```
Hi [Name],

Just checking in - did you get a chance to review the proposal I
sent over on [date]?

Happy to walk through any part of it or answer questions. I know
[specific aspect of the proposal] might need more detail.

Let me know what you think!

Best,
[Name]
```

### Follow-Up 2 (Day 7)

```
Hi [Name],

Following up on the proposal. I wanted to mention something I
didn't include originally:

[Add a new piece of value - additional feature idea, relevant case
study, industry insight, or ROI calculation specific to them]

This could make the project even more impactful for [Company].
Let me know if you'd like to discuss.

Best,
[Name]
```

### Follow-Up 3 (Day 10)

```
Hi [Name],

Just a heads up - I'm getting busy with a few other projects
and wanted to make sure [Company]'s project doesn't lose its
spot in my schedule.

The proposal is valid for another [X] days. If you're still
interested, let's find 15 minutes to finalize the details.

If the timing isn't right, totally understand. Just let me know
either way so I can plan accordingly.

Best,
[Name]
```

### Follow-Up 4 (Day 14) - Final

```
Hi [Name],

Last follow-up on this. I don't want to be that person who keeps
emailing, so this will be my final note.

If [project topic] becomes a priority for [Company], my door is
always open. Just reply to this thread whenever you're ready.

Wishing you and the team all the best!

Best,
[Name]
```

After Follow-Up 4 with no response: Move to Lost.

## Sequence 3: After Discovery Call

### Follow-Up 1 (Same Day or Next Day)

```
Hi [Name],

Great chatting today! Here's a quick recap of what we discussed:

- Problem: [Their core challenge]
- Solution: [Our proposed approach]
- Timeline: [What we discussed]
- Next step: [What was agreed]

I'll have [proposal/scope document/next deliverable] ready by
[date]. Let me know if I missed anything.

Looking forward to working together!

Best,
[Name]
```

### Follow-Up 2 (If No Response After 3-5 Days)

```
Hi [Name],

Following up on our conversation from [date]. I've been thinking
about [specific aspect of their problem] and had an idea:

[New insight or approach that adds value]

Ready to move forward whenever you are. Let me know if you have
any questions about [the next step we agreed on].

Best,
[Name]
```

## Sequence 4: After Negotiation Stalls

### Nudge 1 (Day 3-5)

```
Hi [Name],

Wanted to circle back on our discussion about [specific negotiation
point]. I've given it some thought and here's what I can do:

[Specific concession, alternative, or creative solution]

Does that work for [Company]? I want to find something that
makes sense for both of us.

Best,
[Name]
```

### Nudge 2 (Day 7-10)

```
Hi [Name],

I understand these decisions take time. Just wanted to check:

1. Is there anything else you need from me to move forward?
2. Is there a concern I haven't addressed?
3. Has the timeline or priorities changed?

Any answer is fine. Just want to make sure I'm not missing
something on my end.

Best,
[Name]
```

## Sequence 5: Post-Win (Relationship Building)

### Check-In 1 (Day 3 of Project)

```
Hi [Name],

Quick check-in - how's everything going so far? Wanted to make
sure [first deliverable/initial work] meets your expectations.

Any feedback or adjustments needed?

Best,
[Name]
```

### Check-In 2 (At Delivery)

```
Hi [Name],

Everything's delivered! Here's a summary of what's included:

[List deliverables]

Support period starts today and runs for [30/60] days. Don't
hesitate to reach out if anything needs adjusting.

It's been great working with [Company]. I'd love to keep the
momentum going - let me know if there are other areas where
AI could help.

Best,
[Name]
```

### Testimonial Request (Day 30)

```
Hi [Name],

It's been about a month since we wrapped up [project]. How's
everything working?

If you're happy with the results, I'd really appreciate a quick
testimonial or review. Even 2-3 sentences would be amazing for
our portfolio.

No pressure at all - and I'm always here if you need anything!

Best,
[Name]
```

### Upsell Check-In (Day 60-90)

```
Hi [Name],

Hope [project deliverable] is still running smoothly!

I've been working on some new [relevant capability/feature type]
that reminded me of [Company]. Thought it might be interesting
for your [department/use case].

Want to hop on a quick call to explore? No commitment, just
wanted to share the idea.

Best,
[Name]
```

---

## Follow-Up Rules

1. **Always add value** in follow-ups (insight, resource, new idea)
2. **Never guilt-trip** ("I put a lot of work into that proposal")
3. **Keep it short** (under 100 words per follow-up)
4. **Vary the channel** if possible (email, then LinkedIn, then call)
5. **Respect silence** after 3-4 attempts
6. **Track every follow-up** in the pipeline tracker
7. **Set calendar reminders** for each scheduled follow-up
8. **Best times to send:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM their time zone
