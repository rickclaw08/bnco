# Outreach Templates

Message templates for each prospecting channel. Always customize before sending.

---

## Upwork Proposal Templates

### Template 1: Direct Problem-Solver

```
Hi [Client Name],

I read your posting about [specific problem from their description]. I've built
exactly this type of [automation/chatbot/pipeline] before, and I can have it
working for you in [timeline].

Here's my approach:
1. [Step 1 - addressing their specific need]
2. [Step 2 - how you'd build it]
3. [Step 3 - delivery and testing]

[One sentence about a relevant past project or capability]

I'd love to hop on a quick call to discuss the details, or I can put together
a more detailed plan if you prefer.

Best,
RickClaw AI
```

### Template 2: Value-First with Insight

```
Hi [Client Name],

Interesting project. Based on what you've described, I'd suggest [specific
technical approach] rather than [common but suboptimal approach]. Here's why:
[1-2 sentences explaining the advantage].

I specialize in [relevant AI capability] and can deliver this within [timeline].
My approach would be:

- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- [Deliverable 3]

Happy to share more details or answer any questions.

Best,
RickClaw AI
```

### Template 3: Quick and Confident

```
Hi [Client Name],

This is right in my wheelhouse. I build [type of solution] for businesses
like yours, and I can have [specific deliverable] ready in [timeline].

Key things I'd include:
- [Feature 1]
- [Feature 2]
- [Feature 3]

Let's connect and I'll walk you through my approach.

Best,
RickClaw AI
```

### Upwork Proposal Tips
- Keep cover letter under 200 words
- Reference something specific from their post (proves you read it)
- Include a timeline estimate
- Don't quote exact price in cover letter (discuss in chat)
- Ask one thoughtful question to start a conversation
- Never copy-paste without customizing

## Reddit Response Templates

### Template 1: Helpful Comment (No Pitch)

```
Great question! Here's how I'd approach [their problem]:

1. [Step 1 with brief explanation]
2. [Step 2 with brief explanation]
3. [Step 3 with brief explanation]

The key thing most people miss is [insight that shows expertise].

[Optional: "I've built something similar - happy to answer more questions
if you want to dig deeper."]
```

### Template 2: DM After Engagement (Only When Appropriate)

```
Hey [username], saw your post about [topic]. My comment covered the basics,
but I wanted to share a bit more detail since I work in this space.

[Specific insight or resource related to their problem]

If you're looking for hands-on help with this, I build AI automations
professionally. No pressure at all - just figured I'd mention it since
our conversation was relevant.

Either way, happy to answer any follow-up questions about [topic].
```

### Reddit Engagement Rules
- NEVER pitch in your first comment. Help first.
- Only DM if they express interest or ask for recommendations
- Build karma and reputation in the subreddits over time
- Share genuine expertise, not marketing fluff
- One helpful comment > ten salesy ones

## LinkedIn Message Templates

### Template 1: Connection Request (Under 300 Characters)

```
Hi [Name], I noticed your work at [Company] in [industry/area]. I help
businesses automate [relevant process] with AI. Would love to connect
and exchange ideas.
```

### Template 2: First Follow-Up After Connection

```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]!

I saw your recent [post/company update] about [topic]. Really interesting
approach to [specific aspect].

I've been working on AI automation solutions that help companies like
[Company] [specific benefit - save time, reduce costs, scale operations].

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute chat? I'd love to learn more about
what you're working on and share some ideas that might be useful.

No pitch, just a conversation. Let me know what works for your schedule.

Best,
[Name]
```

### Template 3: Value-Add Message (No Pitch)

```
Hi [Name],

Came across an [article/tool/resource] that reminded me of [something
they posted or their company does]:

[Link or brief description]

Thought you might find it useful. How's [relevant project/initiative] going?

Best,
[Name]
```

### Template 4: Soft Pitch (After 2-3 Interactions)

```
Hi [Name],

Enjoyed our conversation about [topic]. It got me thinking about how
[their company] could [specific benefit] using [specific AI approach].

I've helped companies in [similar industry] achieve [specific result].
For example, [one sentence case study or example].

Would it be helpful if I put together a quick overview of how this
could work for [Company]? No commitment, just some ideas on paper.

Let me know!

Best,
[Name]
```

### LinkedIn Outreach Rules
- Never pitch on the first message
- Always personalize (reference their posts, company, or industry)
- Build rapport over 2-3 interactions before pitching
- Share value (articles, insights, resources) before asking for anything
- Keep messages under 150 words

## Cold Email Templates

### Template 1: Problem-Focused

```
Subject: [Specific problem] at [Company]?

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] is [specific observation - e.g., "scaling your
customer support team" / "launching new products monthly"]. A lot of
companies in [industry] are using AI to [specific solution] and seeing
[specific result].

Quick example: [One sentence about a relevant project or result]

Would a 15-minute call make sense to explore if something similar
could work for [Company]?

Best,
[Name]
RickClaw AI
```

### Template 2: Referral-Based

```
Subject: [Mutual connection] suggested I reach out

Hi [Name],

[Mutual connection] mentioned you're looking into [AI/automation] for
[specific use case]. I specialize in exactly that.

I recently helped [similar company type] [specific result]. Took [timeline]
to set up and they're seeing [specific benefit].

Worth a quick conversation? I can share some ideas specific to [Company].

Best,
[Name]
RickClaw AI
```

### Cold Email Rules
- Keep under 100 words
- One clear call-to-action
- Personalize the first line (never generic)
- Never attach files to cold emails
- Follow up once after 3-5 days, then stop
- Always include an easy opt-out

## Follow-Up Templates

### Follow-Up 1: Gentle Nudge (2-3 Days After Initial Outreach)

```
Hi [Name],

Just wanted to make sure my message didn't get buried. I'd love to
chat about [specific topic] whenever you have a few minutes.

No rush - just reply when it's convenient.

Best,
[Name]
```

### Follow-Up 2: Add Value (5-7 Days)

```
Hi [Name],

Thought of something that might be useful for [Company]:

[Specific insight, tip, or resource related to their business]

Still happy to chat about [original topic] if you're interested.
Either way, hope this is helpful!

Best,
[Name]
```

### Follow-Up 3: Final Check (10-14 Days)

```
Hi [Name],

Last ping from me on this - I know you're busy.

If [original topic] ever becomes a priority, feel free to reach out
anytime. I'll be here.

Wishing you and [Company] all the best!

Best,
[Name]
```
