# Budget Thresholds

Alert levels and spending limits for RickClaw AI operations.

---

## Overall Profitability Thresholds

| Metric | Green (Healthy) | Yellow (Watch) | Red (Action Required) |
|--------|----------------|----------------|----------------------|
| Gross Margin | >70% | 50-70% | <50% |
| Net Margin | >50% | 30-50% | <30% |
| Monthly Revenue Growth | >10% | 0-10% | Negative |
| Burn Rate Trend | Decreasing | Stable | Increasing |

---

## Per-Category Spending Limits

### API & AI Models
- **Monthly budget:** $500 (adjust as revenue grows)
- **Yellow alert:** Over $400/month
- **Red alert:** Over $600/month or >20% of revenue
- **Action:** Switch to cheaper models, optimize prompts, cache responses

### Infrastructure
- **Monthly budget:** $50
- **Yellow alert:** Over $40/month
- **Red alert:** Over $75/month
- **Action:** Audit services, eliminate unused resources

### Tools & Subscriptions
- **Monthly budget:** $100
- **Yellow alert:** Over $80/month
- **Red alert:** Over $150/month
- **Action:** Audit subscriptions, cancel unused tools, find free alternatives

### Marketing & Outreach
- **Monthly budget:** $200 (or 10% of revenue, whichever is higher)
- **Yellow alert:** Over budget with no measurable ROI
- **Red alert:** Over 20% of revenue with poor conversion
- **Action:** Cut underperforming channels, double down on what works

### Subcontractors
- **Monthly budget:** 30% of project revenue (for the project they work on)
- **Yellow alert:** Subcontractor costs >40% of project revenue
- **Red alert:** Subcontractor costs >50% of project revenue
- **Action:** Renegotiate rates, bring work in-house, reprice the project

---

## Per-Project Thresholds

| Tier | Max Cost (% of Revenue) | Min Margin | Max Hours |
|------|------------------------|------------|-----------|
| Starter | 20% | 80% | 6 hours |
| Professional | 25% | 75% | 25 hours |
| Enterprise/month | 30% | 70% | 80 hours |
| Custom | 40% | 60% | Scoped |

If a project exceeds these thresholds:
1. Stop and assess why
2. Determine if scope crept
3. Discuss with client if additional payment is needed
4. Document lessons learned for future pricing

---

## Revenue Concentration Limits

- **Single client max:** 30% of total revenue
- **Yellow:** One client is 30-40% of revenue
- **Red:** One client is >40% of revenue
- **Action:** Actively diversify client base, prioritize new client acquisition

---

## Cash Flow Thresholds

- **Minimum cash reserve:** 2 months of fixed costs
- **Yellow:** Cash reserve under 3 months of fixed costs
- **Red:** Cash reserve under 1 month of fixed costs
- **Action:** Pause non-essential spending, accelerate invoicing, follow up on unpaid invoices

## Scaling Thresholds

When to adjust budgets upward:

| Revenue Milestone | Action |
|------------------|--------|
| $5K/month | Increase API budget to $1,000 |
| $10K/month | Consider paid tools and marketing budget increase |
| $25K/month | Budget for part-time subcontractor |
| $50K/month | Full budget review and restructure |
| $100K/month | Hire or outsource financial tracking |

## Alert Response Protocol

### Yellow Alert
1. Note in budget report
2. Identify root cause
3. Monitor daily for 1 week
4. Implement optimization if trend continues
5. Inform CEO in next update

### Red Alert
1. Immediately flag to CEO
2. Diagnose the specific problem
3. Present 2-3 remediation options
4. Implement emergency fix (cheapest model, pause spending)
5. Review in 48 hours
6. Post-mortem after resolution
