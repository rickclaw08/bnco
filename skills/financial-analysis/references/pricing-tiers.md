# Pricing Tiers Reference

All pricing tiers for RickClaw AI services.

## Tier 1: Starter / Quick Win

**Price:** $500 - $1,500 (one-time)
**Target Client:** Small businesses, solopreneurs, early adopters
**Delivery Time:** 1-3 days

### What's Included
- Single AI automation or workflow
- Basic setup and configuration
- One round of revisions
- 30-day email support
- Documentation/handoff guide

### Example Deliverables
- AI chatbot for customer support (basic)
- Automated email responder
- Content generation workflow
- Simple data extraction pipeline
- Social media scheduling automation

### Cost to Deliver
- Time: 2-6 hours
- API costs: $5-$20
- Tools: Included in existing subscriptions
- **Total cost basis:** $50-$150
- **Target margin:** 80-90%

### When to Recommend
- Client is testing the waters
- Simple, well-defined problem
- Quick turnaround needed
- Building relationship for upsell

---

## Tier 2: Professional / Project-Based

**Price:** $2,500 - $7,500 (one-time or monthly retainer)
**Target Client:** Growing businesses, marketing agencies, mid-size companies
**Delivery Time:** 1-2 weeks

### What's Included
- Multi-step AI automation system
- Custom integrations (2-3 tools/platforms)
- Testing and QA
- Two rounds of revisions
- 60-day support with bug fixes
- Training session (30 min)

### Example Deliverables
- Full lead generation pipeline (scrape + qualify + outreach)
- Content marketing automation suite
- Customer onboarding automation
- Internal knowledge base with AI search
- Reporting dashboard with AI insights

### Cost to Deliver
- Time: 10-25 hours
- API costs: $20-$100
- Tools/integrations: $0-$50/month
- **Total cost basis:** $200-$600
- **Target margin:** 75-85%

### When to Recommend
- Client has a specific business problem
- Multiple systems need to talk to each other
- ROI is clearly measurable
- Client has budget and urgency

---

## Tier 3: Enterprise / Retainer

**Price:** $10,000 - $25,000/month (retainer)
**Target Client:** Established companies, funded startups, enterprise teams
**Delivery Time:** Ongoing engagement

### What's Included
- Dedicated AI strategy and implementation
- Unlimited automation builds (within scope)
- Priority support (same-day response)
- Weekly check-ins and reporting
- Custom model fine-tuning (if needed)
- Full documentation and knowledge transfer
- Quarterly strategy reviews

### Example Deliverables
- Company-wide AI transformation roadmap
- Multiple department automations
- Custom AI agents for specific workflows
- Data pipeline and analytics infrastructure
- Ongoing optimization and new feature development

### Cost to Deliver
- Time: 40-80 hours/month
- API costs: $200-$1,000/month
- Tools/infrastructure: $100-$500/month
- **Total cost basis:** $2,000-$5,000/month
- **Target margin:** 70-80%

### When to Recommend
- Client wants ongoing partnership
- Multiple departments or use cases
- High-value business with clear ROI
- Client values reliability and priority access

---

## Tier 4: Custom / Strategic Partnership

**Price:** $25,000+ (project-based or equity + cash)
**Target Client:** Funded startups, enterprises with specific AI needs
**Delivery Time:** 4-12 weeks for initial build

### What's Included
- Custom AI product or platform build
- Full architecture and design
- Dedicated team allocation
- IP ownership (negotiable)
- Ongoing maintenance agreement
- Performance guarantees (SLA)
- Executive reporting and strategy alignment

### Example Deliverables
- White-label AI product
- Custom LLM application
- Full SaaS feature powered by AI
- Industry-specific AI platform
- AI-powered internal tool suite

### Cost to Deliver
- Time: 100-300+ hours
- API costs: $500-$5,000+
- Infrastructure: $200-$2,000/month
- Potential subcontractor costs: $2,000-$10,000
- **Total cost basis:** $5,000-$20,000+
- **Target margin:** 60-75%

### When to Recommend
- Transformative project with high strategic value
- Client willing to invest significantly
- Opportunity for recurring revenue or equity
- Showcase project for portfolio
- Long-term relationship potential

| Price Range | $500-$1.5K | $2.5K-$7.5K | $10K-$25K/mo | $25K+ |
| Timeline | 1-3 days | 1-2 weeks | Ongoing | 4-12 weeks |
| Support | 30 days | 60 days | Priority | SLA |
| Revisions | 1 round | 2 rounds | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Best For | Testing | Solving | Scaling | Transforming |
| Target Margin | 80-90% | 75-85% | 70-80% | 60-75% |

## Upsell Path

Starter -> Professional -> Enterprise -> Custom

Always plant the seed for the next tier. After delivering a Starter project, mention how a Professional engagement could solve their bigger problems. After a Professional project, discuss ongoing retainer value.

## Discount Policy

- **Annual prepay:** 10% discount on monthly retainers
- **Referral deals:** 15% discount for referred clients (first project)
- **Bundle discount:** 10% off when buying 3+ Starter projects
- **No discounts** below cost basis + 50% margin
- All discounts must be approved by Morgan (CFO) before quoting
