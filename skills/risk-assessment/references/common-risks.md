## How to Use This Reference

1. Identify the type of decision being assessed
2. Scan the relevant section for applicable risks
3. Adjust likelihood and severity for your specific context
4. Add any risks not on this list that are specific to your situation
5. Score each risk using the risk matrix (see `risk-matrix.md`)
6. Prioritize mitigations for HIGH and CRITICAL risks
7. Document in the risk register

**Note:** The likelihood and severity ratings here are starting points. Always adjust based on your specific context, history, and circumstances.
