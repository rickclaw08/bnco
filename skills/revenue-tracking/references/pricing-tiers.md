# Pricing Tiers

ClawOps AI Automation Agency pricing structure.

## Agency Services

### Starter Sprint - $600 (one-time)
- Target: Small businesses, solopreneurs
- Includes: 1 automation workflow, basic setup, 1 revision
- Timeline: 1-2 weeks
- Best for: Testing the waters, single pain point

### Growth Retainer - $2,000/month
- Target: Growing businesses, SMBs
- Includes: Ongoing automation management, 2-3 workflows/month, priority support
- Timeline: Month-to-month, 3-month minimum recommended
- Best for: Businesses ready to scale automation

### Automation Sprint - $7,500 (one-time)
- Target: Mid-market companies
- Includes: Full automation audit + implementation, 5-10 workflows, training
- Timeline: 4-6 weeks
- Best for: Companies needing comprehensive automation overhaul

### Enterprise - $5,000/month
- Target: Larger organizations
- Includes: Dedicated automation team, unlimited workflows, custom integrations, SLA
- Timeline: Annual contract preferred
- Best for: Organizations with complex, ongoing automation needs

## AutoPilot SaaS Product

### Free Tier - $0
- Limited features, taste of the platform
- Goal: funnel to paid tiers

### Pro - $49/month
- Full automation suite for individuals
- Target: Freelancers, solopreneurs

### Business - $149/month
- Team features, advanced automations
- Target: Small teams, agencies
