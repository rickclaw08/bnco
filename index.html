💪 Effort Score (40% of bnco Score)
Based on WHOOP workout strain (0-21 scale) and time spent in productive heart rate zones (zones 2-4). Pilates typically generates 8-14 strain. Higher effort = higher score.
📅 Consistency Score (35% of bnco Score)
Tracks your attendance streak and weekly class frequency. 3x/week = baseline, 4x = 1.1x multiplier, 5x+ = 1.2x. Shows up regularly, score goes up.
💚 Recovery Score (25% of bnco Score)
Direct from WHOOP recovery (0-100%). Factors in HRV, resting heart rate, sleep quality, blood oxygen, and skin temperature. Better recovered = better prepared.
⌚ Apple Watch Integration
Adds real-time heart rate during class, active calories burned, and workout duration verification via HealthKit.
