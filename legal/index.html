The Problem

Your Intake Process Is Costing You Cases

Law firms face a unique challenge: high lead volumes, high case values, and not enough people to handle it all.

📞

After-Hours Leads Go to Voicemail

Potential clients call evenings and weekends. They get voicemail. They call the next firm on Google. Criminal defense and PI leads are especially time-sensitive.

🕑

Slow Response Kills Conversions

WhatsApp messages, web form submissions, and phone leads sit for hours before anyone responds. Speed-to-lead determines who gets the retainer.

📋

Paralegals Buried in Intake

Your team spends 2-3 hours per day on initial intake calls that could be automated. That is time they should be spending on case work.

💰

Leads Fall Through the Cracks

Good cases get lost because follow-up is inconsistent. You are paying $200-$500 per lead from Google Ads, and half of them never get contacted properly.

The Solution

AI-Powered Intake That Never Sleeps

Intelligent intake systems that handle the entire front-end process for your firm:

✓

24/7 Lead Response

AI answers calls, texts, WhatsApp messages, and web form submissions instantly. No lead waits more than 60 seconds for a response, day or night.

✓

Smart Qualification

The AI asks the right questions: What is the legal issue? Where are you located? Do you have a court date? Budget? It filters out non-qualified leads automatically.

✓

Document Collection

Need ID, police reports, medical records, or contracts? The AI requests, collects, and organizes them before the attorney ever sees the case.

✓

Intelligent Routing

Qualified leads get routed to the right attorney based on practice area, case type, and availability, with full context attached. No blind transfers.

✓

CRM Integration

Everything syncs to your case management system: Clio, MyCase, Salesforce, or PracticePanther. No duplicate data entry. No lost information.

✓

Follow-Up Automation

Leads that go cold get nurture sequences. Retained clients get automated check-ins. Post-case clients get review requests. Nothing slips through.
