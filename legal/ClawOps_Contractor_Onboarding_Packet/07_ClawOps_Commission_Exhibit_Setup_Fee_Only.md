## 1. DEFINED TERMS

The following terms shall have the meanings set forth below. All other capitalized terms not defined in this Exhibit shall have the meanings assigned to them in the ICA.

**1.1 "Company"** means ClawOps.

**1.2 "Contractor"** means the individual identified above.

**1.3 "Setup Fee"** means the one-time implementation and setup fee charged to a Client for the initial deployment of Company's services, including but not limited to AI voice systems, lead capture solutions, booking systems, and CRM visibility platforms.

**1.4 "Closed Deal"** means a transaction in which (a) the Client has executed a signed services agreement with Company, and (b) the corresponding Setup Fee has been received by Company as cleared funds.

**1.5 "Company-Collected Funds"** means payments received by Company from a Client that have fully cleared the applicable payment processor, bank, or financial institution, with no pending holds, disputes, or reversals.

**1.6 "Chargeback Event"** means any refund, chargeback, payment reversal, cancellation, or fraud determination that results in the partial or full return of funds previously collected by Company from a Client.

## 2. COMMISSION RATE

**2.1** Contractor shall earn a commission equal to **fifteen percent (15%)** of the Setup Fee on each Closed Deal originated and closed solely by Contractor.

**2.2** Commission applies only to the Setup Fee component of the Closed Deal. No commission is earned on any other revenue category.

## 5. COMMISSION ELIGIBILITY AND CLEARED FUNDS

**5.1** Commission is earned only when Company receives Company-Collected Funds for the applicable Setup Fee. No commission accrues, vests, or becomes payable based on a signed agreement alone.

**5.2** If a Client makes a partial payment of the Setup Fee, Contractor's commission shall be calculated proportionally based on the funds actually collected by Company. The remaining commission, if any, shall be paid as and when additional funds are collected.

**Example:** If a $5,000 Setup Fee is partially paid ($3,000 collected), Contractor earns 15% of $3,000 = $450. The remaining $300 commission is payable only if and when the remaining $2,000 is collected.

---

## 6. REFUNDS, CHARGEBACKS, AND CANCELLATIONS

**6.1** In the event of a Chargeback Event, Contractor's commission shall be reduced or clawed back proportionally to the amount of funds returned, reversed, or otherwise not retained by Company.

**6.2** If commission has already been paid to Contractor and a Chargeback Event subsequently occurs, Contractor authorizes Company to deduct the overpaid commission amount from future commission payments. If no future payments are anticipated, Contractor shall remit the overpaid amount to Company within thirty (30) days of written notice.

**6.3** Company shall provide Contractor with written notice of any Chargeback Event affecting Contractor's commission within a reasonable time after the event occurs.

---

## 7. SPLIT DEALS

**7.1** If more than one Contractor is involved in originating or closing a deal, Company shall determine the commission split in its sole discretion. The split must be documented in writing and communicated to all involved Contractors before the deal closes.

**7.2** No Contractor shall have any claim to commission on a split deal absent a written split determination from Company executed prior to deal closure.

---

## 8. PAYOUT TIMING

**8.1** Commissions shall be paid **NET 15 business days** after the applicable Setup Fee funds have cleared and are confirmed as Company-Collected Funds.

**8.2** Payment shall be made via the method specified in the ICA or as otherwise agreed in writing between Company and Contractor.
