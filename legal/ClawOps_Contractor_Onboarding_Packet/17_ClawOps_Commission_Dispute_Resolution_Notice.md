# COMMISSION DISPUTE RESOLUTION NOTICE

**DRAFT - FOR LEGAL REVIEW ONLY**

**Document:** File 17 of 20 - ClawOps Contractor Onboarding Packet

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This Commission Dispute Resolution Notice ("Notice") provides a structured process for resolving disputes related to commission calculations, payouts, or non-payouts under the Independent Contractor Agreement ("ICA," File 04) and the Commission Exhibit (File 07). This form supplements, but does not replace, the dispute resolution provisions set forth in the ICA.

## 2. WHO MAY FILE

Either the Contractor or the Company may file a Commission Dispute Resolution Notice. The filing party is referred to as the "Claimant" and the other party as the "Respondent."

## 3. WRITTEN NOTICE REQUIREMENT

All commission disputes must be submitted in writing using this form or a substantially similar written notice. Email submission is acceptable, provided the notice is sent to the designated contact addresses specified in the ICA.

## 4. FILING DEADLINE

A Commission Dispute Resolution Notice must be filed within **thirty (30) calendar days** of the disputed payout date, the date on which the Claimant became aware of the disputed non-payout, or the date on which the Claimant became aware of the facts giving rise to the dispute, whichever is later.

Failure to file within this period does not automatically waive the Claimant's rights under the ICA but may be considered in evaluating the dispute.

## 5. REQUIRED INFORMATION

The Claimant must provide the following information:

(a) **Deal Identifier:** Client name, deal date, and any reference number associated with the transaction

(b) **Disputed Amount:** The specific dollar amount in dispute

(c) **Basis for Dispute:** A clear written explanation of the basis for the dispute, including relevant facts, dates, and calculations

(d) **Supporting Documentation:** Copies of any supporting documents, communications, or records relevant to the dispute

(e) **Requested Resolution:** A specific statement of the resolution the Claimant is requesting

## 6. COMPANY REVIEW PERIOD

Upon receipt of a complete Commission Dispute Resolution Notice, the Company shall review the dispute and all supporting documentation within **fifteen (15) business days** of receipt.

The Company may request additional information or documentation from the Claimant during the review period. The review period may be extended by mutual written agreement.

## 7. COMPANY RESPONSE

The Company shall provide a written determination to the Claimant within the review period (or any agreed extension). The written determination shall include:

(a) A summary of the dispute as understood by the Company

(b) The Company's findings

(c) The Company's decision, including any adjustment to commission amounts

(d) The reasoning supporting the decision

## 8. TEMPORARY HOLD PROVISION

During the review period, the Company may place a temporary hold on the disputed commission amount without waiving any rights of either party. Any undisputed commission amounts shall continue to be paid on the regular schedule.

The temporary hold shall be released upon resolution of the dispute in accordance with the Company's written determination or any subsequent agreement between the parties.

## 9. ESCALATION

If the dispute is not resolved to the Claimant's satisfaction through this process, the dispute shall be governed by the dispute resolution provisions set forth in the ICA (File 04), which provides for negotiation, mediation, and binding arbitration in Hamilton County, Ohio.

## 10. NON-WAIVER

Filing a Commission Dispute Resolution Notice does not constitute a waiver of any rights, claims, or defenses available to either party under the ICA, the Commission Exhibit, or applicable law.

## 11. RELATIONSHIP TO MASTER AGREEMENT

This Notice form and process supplement the dispute resolution provisions of the ICA (File 04). In the event of a conflict between this process and the ICA, the ICA controls. Nothing in this form creates rights or obligations not established by the ICA and its exhibits.
