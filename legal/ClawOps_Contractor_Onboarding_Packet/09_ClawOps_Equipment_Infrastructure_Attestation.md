## Purpose

This Equipment and Infrastructure Attestation confirms that Contractor possesses the minimum technical equipment, connectivity, and workspace conditions required to perform sales and outreach activities on behalf of Company. Company relies on the accuracy of this attestation when granting system access and onboarding Contractor.

---

## Attestation

By signing below, Contractor attests to each of the following:

### 1. Internet Connectivity

Contractor maintains a reliable, stable internet connection with a minimum recommended speed of 25 Mbps (download). Contractor's internet service is sufficient to support simultaneous use of VoIP calling platforms, CRM applications, and video conferencing tools without interruption or degradation.

### 2. Computer or Device

Contractor has access to a computer or device that meets the minimum requirements for operating Company-approved CRM, calling platforms, and communication tools. The device must be capable of running a modern web browser (current version of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari), supporting screen sharing, and handling multiple open browser tabs and applications concurrently.

### 3. Headset and Microphone

Contractor possesses a headset with a built-in or attached microphone suitable for professional-quality voice calls. The headset must provide clear audio input and output with minimal background noise. Speakerphone or built-in laptop microphones are not acceptable for client-facing calls unless expressly authorized by Company.

### 4. Workspace Environment

Contractor maintains a quiet, secure, and private workspace suitable for conducting professional sales calls and handling confidential Company and client information. The workspace must be free from excessive background noise, interruptions, and conditions that would compromise call quality or data security.

### 5. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Capability

Contractor confirms that all devices used to access Company systems are capable of supporting multi-factor authentication (MFA). Contractor agrees to enable and maintain MFA on all Company-related accounts and platforms as directed by Company. Contractor will not disable or bypass MFA protections without prior written authorization from Company.

### 6. No Credential Sharing

Contractor shall not share login credentials, passwords, access tokens, API keys, or any other authentication information for Company systems with any third party, including family members, associates, subcontractors, or other Company contractors. Each set of credentials issued by Company is for the exclusive use of the named Contractor.

### 7. No Unauthorized Recording or Use of Company Tools

Contractor shall not record, capture, screenshot, or otherwise duplicate Company-provided tools, interfaces, dashboards, or proprietary systems on personal devices outside the approved scope of Contractor's engagement. Contractor shall not use Company-provided software, accounts, or tools for personal purposes or for the benefit of any third party.

### 8. Accurate Representation of Technical Readiness

Contractor confirms that all statements made in this attestation are accurate and complete as of the date of signing. Contractor has not overstated or misrepresented Contractor's technical capabilities, equipment specifications, or workspace conditions. If Contractor's equipment or workspace conditions change materially after signing, Contractor agrees to notify Company within five (5) business days.

### 9. Company Right to Revoke Access

Contractor acknowledges and agrees that Company may revoke, suspend, or restrict Contractor's access to Company systems, tools, and platforms at any time if Company determines, in its reasonable discretion, that this attestation was inaccurate, that Contractor's equipment or workspace no longer meets minimum requirements, or that continued access poses a security or operational risk.
