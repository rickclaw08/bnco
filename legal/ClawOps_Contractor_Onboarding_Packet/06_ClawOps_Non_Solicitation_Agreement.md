# NON-SOLICITATION AGREEMENT

**ClawOps Contractor Onboarding Packet - File 06**

---

**This Non-Solicitation Agreement** ("Agreement") is entered into as of **[Effective Date]** ("Effective Date") by and between:

**ClawOps**, a technology and consulting firm organized under the laws of the State of Ohio, with its principal place of business in Hamilton County, Ohio ("Company"),

and

**[Contractor Name]**, an individual independent contractor ("Contractor").

Company and Contractor are each referred to individually as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."

---

## RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, Company operates a technology and consulting business providing AI voice systems, lead capture solutions, booking systems, and CRM visibility platforms to trades businesses across North America, including but not limited to HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and roofing companies;

**WHEREAS**, Contractor has entered into or will enter into an Independent Contractor Agreement ("ICA") with Company, under which Contractor will perform sales, consulting, or other services on behalf of Company;

**WHEREAS**, in the course of performing services for Company, Contractor will have access to confidential business information, client relationships, prospect pipelines, and proprietary processes that represent significant investment by Company;

**WHEREAS**, Company has a legitimate business interest in protecting its client relationships, prospect pipelines, workforce stability, and referral networks from unfair solicitation; and

**WHEREAS**, Contractor acknowledges that the restrictions contained in this Agreement are reasonable, necessary, and supported by adequate consideration, including but not limited to access to Company's clients, prospects, training, and proprietary systems.

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual promises contained herein and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

For purposes of this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings set forth below:

**1.1 "Client"** means any individual or entity that (a) has an active services agreement with Company as of the date of Contractor's termination or separation, or (b) was a client of Company at any time during the twelve (12) months preceding Contractor's termination or separation, and with whom Contractor had direct contact or about whom Contractor received Confidential Information during the course of Contractor's engagement with Company.

**1.2 "Prospect"** means any individual or entity that (a) was introduced to Contractor by Company or identified by Contractor through Company resources, leads, or systems, and (b) was actively being pursued by Company for a potential business relationship at any time during the six (6) months preceding Contractor's termination or separation.

**1.3 "Protected Relationship"** means any Client, Prospect, Company employee, Company contractor, or referral partner with whom Company maintains or maintained a business or professional relationship and with whom Contractor had direct contact or about whom Contractor obtained knowledge as a result of Contractor's engagement with Company.

**1.4 "Restricted Period"** means the period beginning on the date of Contractor's termination or separation from Company (for any reason, whether voluntary or involuntary) and ending **[12] months** thereafter.

**1.5 "Competing Services"** means AI voice systems, automated lead capture solutions, booking and scheduling systems, CRM visibility platforms, or any substantially similar technology or consulting services that compete directly with Company's core service offerings to trades businesses.

**1.6 "Confidential Information"** shall have the meaning assigned to it in the ICA or, if not separately defined, means all non-public information regarding Company's clients, prospects, pricing, business strategies, proprietary systems, and internal processes.

---

## 2. CLIENT NON-SOLICITATION

**2.1** During the Restricted Period, Contractor shall not, directly or indirectly, solicit, contact, or attempt to solicit any Client for the purpose of providing, offering, or selling Competing Services.

**2.2** This restriction applies whether Contractor acts on Contractor's own behalf, on behalf of any third party, or through any agent, affiliate, or intermediary.

**2.3** For the avoidance of doubt, this Section applies only to the solicitation of Clients for Competing Services and does not restrict Contractor from engaging with Clients in connection with products or services that do not compete with Company's core offerings.

---

## 3. PROSPECT NON-SOLICITATION

**3.1** During the Restricted Period, Contractor shall not, directly or indirectly, solicit, contact, or attempt to solicit any Prospect for the purpose of providing, offering, or selling Competing Services.

**3.2** This restriction applies whether Contractor acts on Contractor's own behalf, on behalf of any third party, or through any agent, affiliate, or intermediary.

---

## 4. WORKFORCE NON-SOLICITATION

**4.1** During the Restricted Period, Contractor shall not, directly or indirectly, recruit, solicit, induce, or attempt to recruit any employee, independent contractor, or consultant of Company to leave Company's employment or engagement, or to accept employment or engagement with Contractor or any third party.

**4.2** Contractor shall not, during the Restricted Period, directly or indirectly interfere with the relationship between Company and any of its employees, contractors, or consultants.

---

## 5. SCOPE LIMITATION

**5.1** The restrictions in Sections 2 and 3 apply solely to the solicitation of Protected Relationships for Competing Services as defined herein. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed as a non-compete provision, and Contractor is not restricted from engaging in any lawful business activity that does not involve the solicitation of Company's Protected Relationships for Competing Services.

**5.2** Contractor remains free to work in any industry, provide any services, and engage with any individuals or entities that are not Protected Relationships, without limitation.

---

## 6. CARVEOUTS AND EXCEPTIONS

**6.1 General Advertising.** Nothing in this Agreement shall prohibit Contractor from placing general advertisements for services or employment, including but not limited to job postings on public job boards, general social media marketing, or industry-wide promotional campaigns, provided that such advertising is not specifically targeted at or directed toward any Protected Relationship.

**6.2 Unsolicited Inbound Contact.** If a Client, Prospect, or other Protected Relationship contacts Contractor on an unsolicited basis without any direct or indirect encouragement or inducement by Contractor, Contractor's response to such unsolicited contact shall not constitute a violation of this Agreement. The burden of demonstrating that contact was genuinely unsolicited rests with Contractor.

---

## 7. REASONABLENESS

**7.1** Contractor acknowledges and agrees that:

(a) The restrictions contained in this Agreement are reasonable in scope, duration, and geographic reach, and are necessary to protect Company's legitimate business interests;

(b) Contractor has received adequate consideration for the obligations set forth herein, including access to Company's clients, prospects, training, proprietary systems, and business opportunities;

(c) The restrictions will not impose an undue hardship on Contractor's ability to earn a livelihood; and

(d) Contractor has had the opportunity to consult with legal counsel prior to executing this Agreement.

---

## 8. BLUE-PENCIL AND REFORMATION

**8.1** If any court of competent jurisdiction determines that any restriction in this Agreement is unenforceable due to its duration, scope, or breadth, the Parties agree that the court shall have the authority to reform, modify, or reduce such restriction to the minimum extent necessary to render it enforceable, and the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

**8.2** The Parties intend that each provision of this Agreement shall be interpreted in a manner that renders it valid and enforceable to the fullest extent permitted by applicable law.

---

## 9. INJUNCTIVE RELIEF

**9.1** Contractor acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement would cause Company irreparable harm for which monetary damages alone would be inadequate. Accordingly, Company shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief, specific performance, or other equitable remedies in addition to any other remedies available at law, without the requirement of posting a bond or proving actual damages.

**9.2** The right to seek injunctive relief is in addition to, and not in limitation of, any other rights or remedies available to Company under this Agreement, at law, or in equity.

---

## 10. SURVIVAL

**10.1** The obligations set forth in this Agreement shall survive the termination or expiration of the ICA and any other agreement between the Parties, regardless of the reason for termination, and shall remain in effect for the full duration of the Restricted Period.

---

## 11. GOVERNING LAW AND VENUE

**11.1 Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Ohio, without regard to its conflict-of-laws principles.

**11.2 Venue.** Any legal action, suit, or proceeding arising out of or related to this Agreement shall be brought exclusively in the state or federal courts located in Hamilton County, Ohio, and each Party irrevocably consents to the personal jurisdiction of such courts.

---

## 12. SEVERABILITY

**12.1** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable by a court of competent jurisdiction, such invalidity shall not affect the validity of the remaining provisions, which shall continue in full force and effect. The invalid provision shall be reformed to the minimum extent necessary to make it valid and enforceable.

---

## 13. ENTIRE AGREEMENT AND AMENDMENTS

**13.1** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding the subject matter herein and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous oral or written agreements, representations, or understandings related to non-solicitation obligations. This Agreement is supplementary to and does not replace any other agreements between the Parties, including the ICA.

**13.2** This Agreement may not be amended, modified, or waived except by a written instrument signed by both Parties.

---

## 14. WAIVER

**14.1** The failure of either Party to enforce any provision of this Agreement shall not constitute a waiver of that Party's right to enforce that provision or any other provision in the future.

---

## 15. COUNTERPARTS

**15.1** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument. Electronic and digital signatures shall be deemed valid and binding.

---

## IN WITNESS WHEREOF

The Parties have executed this Non-Solicitation Agreement as of the Effective Date first written above, each intending to be legally bound hereby.
