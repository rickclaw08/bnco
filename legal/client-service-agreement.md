**This Client Service Agreement ("Agreement")** is entered into as of [Date] ("Effective Date") by and between:

**Provider:** ClawOps LLC, an Ohio limited liability company, with its principal place of business in Cincinnati, Ohio ("ClawOps" or "Provider")

**Client:** [Client_Company_Name], a [Client_Entity_Type] with its principal place of business at [Client_Address] ("Client")

Provider and Client are each referred to individually as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."

---

## 1. SERVICES

1.1 **Scope of Services.** ClawOps shall provide the AI automation and implementation services described in the applicable Statement of Work ("SOW") attached as Exhibit A, which may include but is not limited to:

- AI receptionist deployment and configuration
- Inbound/outbound call handling automation
- SMS and text follow-up automation
- CRM integration and workflow setup (GoHighLevel or equivalent)
- Voice AI agent training and customization
- Staff training on AI systems and CRM tools
- Ongoing system monitoring and optimization

1.2 **Statement of Work.** Each engagement shall be governed by a SOW that specifies the particular services, pricing, deliverables, and timeline for that engagement. In the event of a conflict between this Agreement and a SOW, the SOW shall control with respect to the specific engagement.

1.3 **Service Standards.** ClawOps shall perform all Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with industry standards for AI automation services.

---

## 2. PRICING AND PAYMENT

2.1 **Fees.** Client shall pay ClawOps the fees set forth in the applicable SOW. Unless otherwise specified in the SOW, the standard pricing structure is:

- **Setup/Implementation Fee:** As quoted in the SOW (typical range: $3,000 to $7,500, depending on scope)
- **Monthly Service Fee:** As quoted in the SOW (typical range: $750 to $2,000/month, depending on services)

2.2 **Payment of Setup Fee.** The setup/implementation fee is due in full prior to the commencement of implementation work, unless alternative payment terms are specified in the SOW.

2.3 **Payment of Monthly Fees.** Monthly service fees begin on the date the AI system is deployed and operational ("Go-Live Date"). Monthly fees are billed on the first business day of each month and are due within ten (10) calendar days of the invoice date.

2.4 **Late Payments.** Payments not received within ten (10) days of the due date shall accrue a late fee of 1.5% per month (or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less) on the outstanding balance.

2.5 **Payment Methods.** Payments may be made via credit card, ACH transfer, wire transfer, or such other methods as ClawOps may designate.

---

## 3. IMPLEMENTATION TIMELINE

3.1 **Implementation Period.** ClawOps shall complete the initial setup and deployment within two (2) to four (4) weeks from the Effective Date, subject to timely cooperation by Client as described in Section 4.

3.2 **Go-Live Date.** The Go-Live Date is the date on which the AI system is deployed, tested, and operational for Client's business. ClawOps shall notify Client in writing when the system is live.

3.3 **Delays.** If implementation is delayed due to Client's failure to provide required information, access, or approvals in a timely manner, the implementation timeline shall be extended accordingly, and monthly fees may begin on the originally projected Go-Live Date regardless of the delay.

---

## 4. CLIENT OBLIGATIONS

Client acknowledges that successful implementation requires Client's active participation. Client agrees to:

4.1 **Provide Business Information.** Furnish all information reasonably requested by ClawOps, including but not limited to: business name, hours of operation, service offerings, pricing, staff names and roles, call handling preferences, scheduling rules, and FAQ content.

4.2 **Respond Promptly.** Respond to ClawOps setup questions, approval requests, and information requests within two (2) business days.

4.3 **Provide Access.** Grant ClawOps access to relevant systems, platforms, and accounts as needed for integration (e.g., phone system, CRM, booking software, website).

4.4 **Maintain Subscriptions.** Maintain any third-party subscriptions required for the Services (e.g., GoHighLevel, phone service) at Client's own expense, unless otherwise specified in the SOW.

4.5 **Designate a Point of Contact.** Designate a primary contact person authorized to make decisions regarding the implementation and ongoing services.

4.6 **Provide Feedback.** Participate in testing and provide timely feedback during the implementation period to ensure the AI system meets Client's requirements.

---

## 5. TERM AND TERMINATION

5.1 **Initial Term.** The initial term begins on the Effective Date and continues through the completion of the implementation period.

5.2 **Ongoing Service Term.** Following the Go-Live Date, the monthly service term continues on a month-to-month basis.

5.3 **Cancellation.** Either Party may cancel the ongoing monthly services by providing thirty (30) days' prior written notice to the other Party. Cancellation notice must be submitted via email to the contact addresses listed in this Agreement.

5.4 **Effect of Cancellation.**

- (a) Client remains responsible for any unpaid fees incurred prior to the cancellation effective date.
- (b) ClawOps shall provide reasonable transition assistance for up to five (5) business days following cancellation, including export of Client data and call recordings.
- (c) ClawOps's obligation to provide Services ceases on the cancellation effective date.

5.5 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within fifteen (15) days of receiving written notice of the breach.

5.6 **Refund Policy.** Setup/implementation fees are non-refundable once implementation work has commenced. If ClawOps fails to deliver a functional system within sixty (60) days of the Effective Date (absent Client-caused delays), Client may request a full refund of the setup fee.

---

## 6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

6.1 **ClawOps IP.** All AI agent configurations, prompts, scripts, workflows, automation templates, and proprietary methodologies developed or used by ClawOps remain the exclusive property of ClawOps. Client receives a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use these elements solely in connection with the Services during the term of this Agreement.

6.2 **Client Data.** All Client business data, customer records, call recordings, and contact information remain the exclusive property of Client. ClawOps shall not use Client data for any purpose other than providing the Services.

6.3 **Upon Termination.** Upon termination, ClawOps shall provide Client with an export of all Client data within ten (10) business days of the termination date. ClawOps shall delete Client data from its systems within thirty (30) days of termination, except as required by law.

---

## 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1 **Mutual Obligations.** Each Party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other Party's proprietary information and trade secrets. Neither Party shall disclose the other Party's confidential information to any third party without prior written consent.

7.2 **Exceptions.** Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving Party; (b) was already known to the receiving Party; (c) is independently developed without use of the disclosing Party's information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by law or court order.

---

8.4 **Third-Party Services.** ClawOps is not responsible for the performance, availability, or pricing of third-party platforms and services used in connection with the Services (e.g., GoHighLevel, Twilio, VoIP providers).

## 10. INDEMNIFICATION

10.1 **ClawOps Indemnification.** ClawOps shall indemnify and hold harmless Client from any third-party claims arising from ClawOps's gross negligence or willful misconduct in performing the Services.

10.2 **Client Indemnification.** Client shall indemnify and hold harmless ClawOps from any third-party claims arising from: (a) Client's use of the Services in violation of applicable law; (b) Client's business operations; or (c) any content or data provided by Client to ClawOps for use in the AI system.

## 11. COMPLIANCE

11.1 **Telecommunications Compliance.** ClawOps shall configure AI systems in compliance with applicable telecommunications regulations, including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and FCC regulations regarding automated calls and text messages.

11.2 **Client Responsibility.** Client is responsible for ensuring that Client's use of the AI system complies with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations, including but not limited to consumer protection laws, telemarketing regulations, and industry-specific requirements.

11.3 **Recording Disclosure.** If the AI system records calls, Client is responsible for providing appropriate recording disclosures to callers as required by applicable state and federal law.

## 12. GENERAL PROVISIONS

12.1 **Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Ohio, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

12.2 **Dispute Resolution.** Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall first be submitted to good-faith negotiation between the Parties. If negotiation fails within thirty (30) days, the dispute shall be resolved by binding arbitration in Hamilton County, Ohio, in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

12.3 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with any SOW(s), constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior agreements, proposals, and representations.

12.4 **Amendments.** This Agreement may not be amended except by a written instrument signed by both Parties.

12.5 **Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
