## Prompt Opener Rewrite Session (10:00-11:10 PM)

### Brand's Directive: Customer-Style Opener
Brand wanted the cold call opener completely restructured. Old approach ("Hey this is Jordan with Claw Ops, following up on your phone operations, is the owner around?") screamed sales call. New approach: sound like a potential customer.

### Key Changes
1. **firstMessage changed**: "Hey, this is Jordan with Claw Ops" -> just "Hey" (no name, no company)
2. **No identity reveal until asked**: Jordan doesn't say his name or Claw Ops until the prospect specifically asks "who is this?"
3. **Three-phase opener** (each phase is ONE separate turn, never combined):
   - **Phase A**: Casual niche service question ("Hey John, I was just wondering, do you guys handle both residential and commercial?")
   - **Phase B**: Acknowledge answer, share relatable missed-call story ("Oh nice, thanks John. Hey so I tried calling an HVAC company the other day and nobody picked up. You guys ever deal with that?")
   - **Phase C**: Ask for owner/decision-maker ("Yeah that's what I figured. Hey is the owner or someone in charge around?")
4. **Call screening updated**: "Yeah, just had a quick question about your services" (vague, customer-sounding, no name/company)
5. **AFTER SCREENING**: flows directly into Phase A
