Jordan and Kai both timed out on first attempt (spent all time searching web, never wrote files). Respawned with direct-write instructions and 10-min timeouts. All three delivered successfully on second try.

### Key Research Findings
- **Voss mirroring** (repeat last 1-3 words) is the most AI-friendly sales technique - mechanical, no emotional intelligence needed, buys processing time
- **Voss labeling** ("It sounds like...") works even if wrong - prospect corrects you, giving better data
- **Holmes disarming** ("I'm not calling to sell you anything") is most powerful opener for cold calls
- **Klaff time constraint** ("I'll be quick") reduces hangup risk by 40%+
- **Prompt length sweet spot for VAPI: 1,500-2,500 words** (ours was ~4,500, nearly 2x)
- **Examples override rules**: if examples are 4 sentences, AI ignores 2-sentence rule
- **Filler word prevention**: "Don't say um" activates the concept and makes it worse. Use vocabulary exclusion: "these words don't exist in your vocabulary"
- **Temperature 0.3-0.5** is production sweet spot for voice sales (balances natural sound vs hallucination risk)
