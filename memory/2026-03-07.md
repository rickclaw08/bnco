### Key Concepts Integrated into All 6 Agent Prompts

**The Three Tens** (core framework for every agent):
1. Product Certainty - caller believes the service/product solves their problem
2. Trust in You - caller trusts Jordan as knowledgeable and credible
3. Company Certainty - caller trusts the company behind the service

When prospect objects, identify WHICH of the three is low and address that specific one. Each objection handler is now tagged with which certainty is low.

**Straight Line Flow**: Open -> Qualify -> Pain -> Solution -> Close. Every response redirects to this line.

**Tonality directives**: Scarcity, Absolute Certainty, Reasonable Man, Empathy, Presupposing Close, Money-Aside - all embedded as tone instructions.

**Problem Awareness from NEPQ**: Agents now ask questions that make the CALLER realize the urgency (not told by agent). Future pacing negative outcomes. "How long has this been going on?" "Has it gotten worse?"

**The Loop**: First objection = don't address, loop to raise certainty. Second = different angle. Third = now it's real, address head-on.

**Strategic Silence**: After asking booking/closing questions, agent stops talking. "First one to speak loses."
